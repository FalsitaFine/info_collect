
Heat prostration: Overheating of the body due to 
extreme weather conditions. Unrelieved <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hyperthermia/article.htm">hyperthermia</a> can 
lead to collapse and death, particularly in the elderly. 
Prevention via air conditioning, ventilation, and drinking 
extra water is the key for vulnerable persons. In emergency 
cases, injections of saline solution and rapid cooling of 
the body may be needed. Also known as heatstroke or 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hyperthermia/article.htm"> hyperthermia</a>.
