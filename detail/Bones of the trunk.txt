
Bones of the trunk: The 51 <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8645" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">trunk bones</a> consist of 26 vertebrae, 24 ribs, and the sternum. The 26 vertebrae comprise 7 cervical, 12 thoracic, and 5 <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8769" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">lumbar vertebrae</a>, plus the sacrum and the coccyx. The 24 ribs comprise 14 true ribs, 6 false ribs, and 4 floating ribs. The sternum is the breastbone. Along with the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8644" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">bones of the head</a>, also known as axial bones.
