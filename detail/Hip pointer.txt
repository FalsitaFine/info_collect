
Hip pointer: A bruise of the upper edge of the ilium, one of the hip bones. This injury is typically caused by a direct blow or fall and is most common in those playing contact sports. Also known as iliac crest <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2838" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">contusion</a>.
