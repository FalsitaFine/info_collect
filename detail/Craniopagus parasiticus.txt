
Craniopagus parasiticus: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26738" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Conjoined twins</a> joined at the head (craniopagus) in which a rudimentary head (with little or no body) is attached to the head of the larger and usually more normal twin. The rudimentary head was thought to be parasitic.
