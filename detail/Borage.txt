
Borage: An herb, also known as the starflower, that has long been used for medicinal purposes. Borage is rich in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25787" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">gamma linolenic acid</a> (GLA), an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11349" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">essential fatty acid</a>.
