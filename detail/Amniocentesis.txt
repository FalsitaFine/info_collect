
Amniocentesis: A before-birth diagnostic procedure during which a long needle is used to obtain <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2226" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">amniotic fluid</a> from within the uterus. This fluid can be used for genetic and other diagnostic tests. Informally known as amnio.
