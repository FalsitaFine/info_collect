
Inverted repeat: A sequence of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4596">nucleotides</a> in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3090">DNA</a> that is identical to another except that it has been reversed (inverted) in direction. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25471">Repeat</a>.
