
Necrosis: The death of living cells or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5800">tissues</a>. Necrosis can be due, for example, to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4052">ischemia</a> (lack of blood flow). From the Greek "nekros" (dead body).
