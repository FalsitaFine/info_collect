
Hemolysis: The destruction of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5260">red blood cells</a> which leads to the release of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15738">hemoglobin</a> from within the red blood cells into the blood <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4934">plasma</a>.
<u>Etymology:</u> The word "hemolysis" is made up of "hemo-", blood + "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4228">lysis</a>", the disintegration of cells.
