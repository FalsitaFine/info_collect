
Albinism: A group of genetic disorders in which there is partial or total lack of the pigment melanin in the eyes, skin, and hair. 
See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23001">Albinism, oculocutaneous</a>; <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8379">Hermansky-Pudlak syndrome</a>.
