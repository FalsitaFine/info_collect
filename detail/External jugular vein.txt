
External jugular vein: The more <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9285">superficial</a> of the two jugular <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5970">veins</a> situated on each side of the neck. The other is the internal jugular vein. They drain blood from the head, brain, face and neck and convey it toward the heart.
