
Hemangioma, cavernous: A type of hemangioma composed of blood-filled 'lakes' and channels. A cavernous henangioma is raised and red or purplish. Small cavernous hemangiomas on the surface of the body may be removed or treated by <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9808" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">electrocoagulation</a>. Surgery is sometimes needed.
