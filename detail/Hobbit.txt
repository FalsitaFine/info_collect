
Hobbit: Nickname for the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40197" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Flores man</a> (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40193" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Homo floresiensis</a>). Derived from the fictional race from J. R. R. Tolkien's Middle-earth universe which first appears in the book <i>The Hobbit</i>.
