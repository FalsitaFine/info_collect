
Condyloma subcutaneum: A sexually transmitted disorder characterized by wartlike growths around the anus and genitals that is caused by the virus poxvirus. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/molluscum_contagiosum_facts/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">molluscum contagiosum</a>.
