
Cardiac ventricle: One of 
the two lower chambers of the heart. 
The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9124">right ventricle</a> is the chamber that receives blood from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26566">right atrium</a> and pumps 
it into the lungs via the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24368">pulmonary artery</a> while the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9127">left ventricle</a> is the chamber that receives blood from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26567">left atrium</a> and pumps 
it into the system circulation via the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2295">aorta</a>.
