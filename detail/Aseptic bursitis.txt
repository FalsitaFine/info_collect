
Aseptic bursitis: Inflammation of a bursa that is not
caused by infection. A bursa is a closed fluid-filled sac that functions as a gliding
surface to reduce friction between tissues of the body. When the bursa becomes inflamed,
the condition is known as "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/acute_and_chronic_bursitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">bursitis</a>." Most commonly this is not an infectious
condition (aseptic bursitis).
