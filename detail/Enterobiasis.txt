
Enterobiasis: Pinworm infection, an infection caused by a small, white intestinal worm -- the pinworm or, more formally, Enterobius vermicularis. The pinworm is about the length of a staple and lives for the most part within the rectum of humans. While an infected person is asleep, female <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pinworm_infection/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">pinworms</a> leave the intestines through the anus and deposit eggs on the skin around the anus.
