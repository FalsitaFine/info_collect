
Cruciate ligament, anterior: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8845">knee joint</a> is 
surrounded by a joint capsule with ligaments strapping the inside and outside of 
the joint (collateral ligaments) as well as crossing within the joint (cruciate 
ligaments). These ligaments provide stability and strength to the knee joint.
