
Heart disease: Any disorder that affects the heart. Sometimes the term "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/heart_disease_coronary_artery_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">heart disease</a>" is used narrowly and incorrectly as a synonym for coronary artery disease. Heart disease   is synonymous with cardiac disease but not with cardiovascular disease which is any disease of the heart or blood vessels. Among the many types of heart disease, see, for example: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/angina_symptoms/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Angina</a>;  <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/arrhythmia_irregular_heartbeat/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Arrhythmia</a>; Congenital heart disease; Coronary artery disease (CAD); Dilated <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cardiomyopathy_hypertrophic/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cardiomyopathy</a>; <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/heart_attack/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Heart attack</a> (myocardial infarction); <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/heart_failure/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Heart failure</a>; Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy;  Mitral regurgitation;  <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/mitral_valve_prolapse/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Mitral valve prolapse</a>; and  Pulmonary stenosis.
