
Assay, CEA: CEA (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/carcinoembryonic_antigen/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">carcinoembryonic antigen</a>) is a protein found in many types of cells but associated with tumors and the developing fetus.
CEA is tested in blood. The normal range is &lt;2.5 ng/ml in an adult non-smoker and &lt;5.0 ng/ml in a smoker.
