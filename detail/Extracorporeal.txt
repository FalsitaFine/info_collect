
Extracorporeal: Outside the body, in the anatomic sense. As in extracorporeal circulation, extracorporeal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/dialysis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">dialysis</a>, and  extracorporeal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/shock/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">shock</a> wave lithotripsy. From extra- + corpus, meaning body.
