
COL2A1: The gene responsible for the production of the alpha1(II) chain of type II collagen. Mutations in the COL2A1 gene lead to a number of different <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3719" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">heritable</a> skeletal disorders, including <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=34098" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">achondrogenesis type II</a>, hypochondrogenesis, Kniest <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3142" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">dysplasia</a>, spondyloepimetaphyseal dysplasia of the Strudwick type, spondyloepiphyseal dysplasia congenita, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=34216" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Stickler syndrome</a>.
