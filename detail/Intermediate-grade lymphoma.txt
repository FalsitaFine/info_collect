
Intermediate-grade lymphoma: A lymphoma that is more aggressive (grows and spreads faster) than a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25089" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">low-grade lymphoma</a>, but responds better to anticancer drugs. Intermediate-grade lymphomas include diffuse, small, cleaved cell lymphoma and diffuse, large, noncleaved cell lymphoma. Also called an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25085" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">aggressive lymphoma</a>.
