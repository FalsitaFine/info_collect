
Dihydroorotate dehydrogenase: An enzyme necessary to pyrimidine synthesis and therefore essential to the production of DNA and RNA. The drug <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/leflunomide/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">leflunomide</a> (Arava), an immunomodulatory agent, acts by specifically inhibiting dihydroorotate dehydrogenase.
