
Adhesion: The union of two opposing tissue surfaces. This term is often used to refer to the sides of a wound, as well as to scar tissue strands that can form at the site of a previous operation, such as within the abdomen after a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6212" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">laparotomy</a>.
