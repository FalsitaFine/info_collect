
Fiber and cholesterol: Soluble <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/fiber/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">fiber</a> substances are  effective in helping reduce the blood <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cholesterol_management/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cholesterol</a>. This is especially true with oat bran,  fruits, psyllium and legumes. High soluble-fiber diets may lower cholesterol and  low-density lipoproteins (the 'bad' lipoproteins) by as much as 15%.
