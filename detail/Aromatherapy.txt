
Aromatherapy: A form of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2211" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">alternative medicine</a> in which essential oils or other scents are inhaled to achieve therapeutic benefit. The mechanism of action in aromatherapy is unknown, but recent studies have shown that aromatherapy may be beneficial for some health problems.
