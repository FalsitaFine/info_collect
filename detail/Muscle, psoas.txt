
Muscle, psoas: Muscles of the lower back (the loin). There are two <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9653" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">psoas</a> muscles on each side of the back. The larger of the two is called the psoas major and the smaller the psoas minor.
