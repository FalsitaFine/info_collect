
Agnate: 1. As a noun, a relative on the father's side. 2. As an adjective, related on the father's side. As opposed to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32921" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">enate</a> (someone related on the mother's side) From the Latin agnatus, the past participle of agnasci (to issue forth), from ad+ nasci (to be born).
