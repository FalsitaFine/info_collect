
Abdominal pain: Pain in the belly. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/abdominal_pain_causes_remedies_treatment/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Abdominal pain</a> can be acute or chronic. It may reflect a major problem with one of the organs in the abdomen, such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/appendicitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">appendicitis</a> or a perforated intestine, or it may result from a fairly minor problem, such as excess buildup of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/intestinal_gas_belching_bloating_flatulence/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">intestinal gas</a>.
