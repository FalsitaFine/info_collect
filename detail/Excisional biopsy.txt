
Excisional biopsy: A biopsy in which an  entire  lesion is removed. A excisional biopsy is in contrast to an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=31000" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">incisional biopsy</a> in which only a sample of tissue is cut from a mass (incised) and removed.
