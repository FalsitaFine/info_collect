
Aspartic acid: An amino acid, one of the 20 building blocks of protein. A amino acid that is not essential to the human diet, aspartic acid was discovered in protein in 1868. It has a role as a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9973" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">neurotransmitter</a>. Symbol: Asp.
