
Autonomy, patient: The right of patients to make decisions about their medical care without their health care provider trying to influence the decision. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13551" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Patient autonomy</a> does allow for health care providers to educate the patient but does not allow the health care provider to make the decision for the patient.
