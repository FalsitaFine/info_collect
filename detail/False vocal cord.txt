
False vocal cord: A fold of mucous membrane covering muscle in the larynx. The false vocal cord separates the ventricle of the larynx from the vestibule of the larynx. Also called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33272" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">false vocal fold</a>.
