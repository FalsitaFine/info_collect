
Hypoglossal neuropathy: Disease of the hypo'<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25486" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">glossal</a> nerve. Paralysis of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7652" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hypoglossal nerve</a> affects the tongue, making speech sound thick and causing the tongue to deviate toward the paralyzed side. In time, the tongue diminishes in size '(atrophies).
