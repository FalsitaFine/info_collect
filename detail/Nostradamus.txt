
Nostradamus: Celebrated French physician and astrologer (1503-1566), born Michel de Notredame. 
Nostradamus studied medicine in Montpellier, started
practicing medicine in 1525, introduced better ways of treating <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/plague_facts/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">plague</a> victims, and became royal physician to King Charles IX. In 1550, he began writing a book of prophecies called "Centuries" which he published in 1555. His final prophecy came when he told a priest, "You will not find me alive at sunrise."  He died early the next morning.
