
Delta cell, pancreatic: A type of cell located in tissue that is called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4054" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">islets of Langerhans</a> in the pancreas. Delta cells make somatostatin, a hormone that inhibits the release of numerous hormones in the body.
