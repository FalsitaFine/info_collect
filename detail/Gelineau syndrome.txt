
Gelineau syndrome: A neurological disorder marked by a sudden recurrent uncontrollable compulsion to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sleep/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">sleep</a>. Named for a French neurologist, JBE Gelineau (1859-1906). Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/narcolepsy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">narcolepsy</a>.
