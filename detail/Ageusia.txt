
Ageusia: An inability to taste sweet, sour, bitter, or salty substances. People who can taste sweet, sour, bitter, or salty substances but have a reduced ability to do so are said to have <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9663" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hypogeusia</a>.
