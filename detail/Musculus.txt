
Musculus: The Latin for muscle. Plural: musculi. In anatomy and many medical textbooks and dictionaries, the muscles are referred to by their Latin names such as musculi abdominis (the abs), musculus <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25482" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">gluteus maximus</a> (the big buttock muscle), and musculus pectoralis major and minor (the pecs).
