
Blue baby: A baby  who is <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23457">cyanotic</a> (blue), due usually to a heart <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22055">malformation</a> that prevents the baby's blood from being fully oxygenated. The bluish color reflects the deoxygenated state of the blood. (Oxygenated blood is red.) Sometimes the term "blue baby" is also applied to a child who is cyanotic due to failure by the lungs to oxygenate the blood.
