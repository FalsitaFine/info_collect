
Center, trauma: A specialized facility in a hospital that is designed to provide diagnostic and treatment services for trauma patients with physical injuries. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8184" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Traumatology</a> is the branch of surgery which deals with trauma patients. "Trauma" is the Greek word for "a wound" (and also for "damage or defeat").
