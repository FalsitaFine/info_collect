
Antiandrogen: A drug that blocks the action of androgens (male sex hormones). Antiandrogens are sometimes used in the treatment of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/prostate_cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">prostate cancer</a>. The antiandrogens currently available include flutamide (Eulexin), <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/bicalutamide/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">bicalutamide</a> (Casodex), and nilutamide (Nilandron).
