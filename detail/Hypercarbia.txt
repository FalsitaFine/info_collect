
Hypercarbia: More than the normal level of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20132" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">carbon dioxide</a> in the blood. Hypercarbia is the opposite of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38434" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hypocarbia</a>.
