
Aortic insufficiency: Backflow of blood from the aorta into the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9127" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">left ventricle</a> across a weakened <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2298" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">aortic valve</a>. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=31850" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">aortic regurgitation</a>.
