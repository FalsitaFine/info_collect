
Allergy to cockroaches: A condition that manifests as an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/allergies_quiz/quiz.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="quiz">allergic reaction</a> when one is exposed to tiny particles from cockroaches. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/asthma_overview/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Asthma</a> can be due to exposure to cockroach allergens (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/allergy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">allergy</a>-provoking substances). These substances are the proteins shed or excreted by the cockroaches.
