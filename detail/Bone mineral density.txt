
Bone mineral density: BMD, a measure of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2501">bone density</a>, reflecting the strength of bones as represented by calcium content. The BMD test detects osteopenia (mild bone loss, usually without symptoms) and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/osteoporosis/article.htm">osteoporosis</a> (more severe bone loss, which may cause symptoms).  See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=21849">Bone mass density</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8048">Osteopenia</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4686">Osteoporosis</a>.
