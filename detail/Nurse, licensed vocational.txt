
Nurse, licensed vocational: A nurse who has completed a one- or two-year training program in health care and earned a state license. LVNs provide direct patient care for people with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2731" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chronic illness</a>, in nursing homes, hospitals, and home settings. They assist RNs in caring for acutely ill patients.
