
Bacteriaphobia: An abnormal and persistent fear of germs. Sufferers from bacteriaphobia experience undue <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anxiety/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anxiety</a> 
even though they realize that most germs are not pathogens (disease-causing 
germs). To avoid germs, they may repeat cleaning rituals, such as washing their 
hands or face.
