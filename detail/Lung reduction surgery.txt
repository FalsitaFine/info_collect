
Lung reduction surgery: A surgical treatment for patients with advanced <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/emphysema_lung_condition/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">emphysema</a> in which the volume of the emphysematous lung is reduced by wedge excision to allow the remaining tissue to expand more fully and restore some of the patient's breathing capacity. Also called reduction pneumoplasty or bilateral pneumectomy.
