
Breast abscess: A local accumulation of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5140">pus</a> within the breast due to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12923">infection</a>. Symptoms may include painful local swelling of the breast, a breast lump, and redness and tenderness of the breast. If the abscess forms in spite of antibiotics, it may need to be incised and drained, a minor surgical procedure, in order to heal. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24982">Breast infection</a>.
