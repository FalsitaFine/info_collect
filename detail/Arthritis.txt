
Arthritis: Inflammation of a joint. When joints are inflamed they can develop stiffness, warmth, swelling, redness and pain. There are over 100 types of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/arthritis/article.htm">arthritis</a>. (see <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/osteoarthritis/article.htm">osteoarthritis</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rheumatoid_arthritis/article.htm">rheumatoid arthritis</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/ankylosing_spondylitis/article.htm">ankylosing spondylitis</a>, 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/psoriatic_arthritis/article.htm">psoriatic arthritis</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/systemic_lupus/article.htm">lupus</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sickle_cell/article.htm">gout</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pseudogout/article.htm">pseudogout</a>).
