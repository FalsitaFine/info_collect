
Cochlea: The cochlea is the part of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7210" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">inner ear</a> that converts mechanical energy (vibrations) into nerve impulses sent to the brain. It is also known as the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22777" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">organ of hearing</a>.
