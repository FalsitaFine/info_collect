
COL4A5: The gene for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39157" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">basement membrane</a> type IV collagen alpha-5 chain. COL4A5 is on the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6030" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">X chromosome</a> in band Xq22.3. Mutations in COL4A5 are responsible for the X-linked form of  <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10745" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Alport syndrome</a>, a type of hereditary kidney disease. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33468" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Collagen IV alpha 5</a>; Collagen, type IV, alpha 5.
