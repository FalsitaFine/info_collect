
itis: Suffix meaning inflammation. For
example, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/colitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">colitis</a> is literally colon inflammation or figuratively inflammation of the
colon. The ending -itis is one of the building blocks derived from Greek (in this case) or
Latin used to construct medical terms.
