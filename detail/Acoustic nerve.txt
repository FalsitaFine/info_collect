
Acoustic nerve: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7616" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">eighth cranial nerve</a> which is concerned with hearing, balance, and head position. It branches into two parts'a cochlear part that transmits sound reception for hearing and a vestibular part that senses balance and head position. Also known as the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7618" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">vestibulocochlear nerve</a>.
