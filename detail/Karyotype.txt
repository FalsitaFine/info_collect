
Karyotype: A standard arrangement of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2724" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chromosome complement</a> prepared for chromosome analysis. A normal female karyotype would include each of the 22 pairs of autosomes (nonsex chromosomes), arranged in numeric order, together with the two X chromosomes.
