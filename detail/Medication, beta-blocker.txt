
Medication, beta-blocker: Drugs that antagonize the action of adrenaline (a beta adrenergic substance) and relieve <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/stress/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">stress</a> to the heart muscle.  Beta-blockers are often used to slow the heart rate or lower the blood pressure.  They are also used to prevent <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/migraine/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">migraine</a> headaches.
