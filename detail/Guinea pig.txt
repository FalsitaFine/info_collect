
Guinea pig: 1.Cavia porcellus, also known as Cavia cobaya, a plump rodent native to South America brought to Europe by the Spanish conquistadors. Often kept as pets and once very widely used a laboratory animal for research.   2.By extension, someone (or something) used as a subject of experimentation. In this sense, the term "guinea pig" may have negative connotations.  The name guinea pig is of unknown origin. In the pet trade, the term <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=34370" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cavy</a> is often used.
