
Cannabinoid: A chemical compound in cannabis or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/marijuana/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">marijuana</a>. Tetrahydrocannabinol is one of the cannabinoids. Also found in dronabinol (Marinol) and nabilone (Cesamet), synthetic medicines for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/nausea_and_vomiting/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">nausea and vomiting</a>
