
Hereditary kidney cancer: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/kidney_cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Kidney cancer</a> due to a gene than can be transmitted from parent to child. The vast majority of kidney <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a> cases are not hereditary, but a small proportion of renal cell cancers are caused by hereditary syndromes.
