
Hypervolemic: Pertaining to hypervolemia, an abnormal increase in blood volume. For example, hypervolemic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hyponatremia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hyponatremia</a> refers to the lowering of the blood sodium level due to hypervolemia, expansion of the blood volume.
