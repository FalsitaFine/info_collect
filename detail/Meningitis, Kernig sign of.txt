
Meningitis, Kernig sign of: This clinical hallmark of 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10375">meningitis</a>, inflammation 
of the meninges, the membranes covering the brain and spinal cord, is tested for by having the
person lie supine (flat on the back), flex the thigh so 
that it is at a right angle to the trunk, and completely extend the leg at 
the knee joint. If the leg cannot be completely extended due to pain, this 
is Kernig sign.
