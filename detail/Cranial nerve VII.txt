
Cranial nerve VII: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7576" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cranial nerves</a> emerge from or enter the skull (the cranium), as opposed to the spinal nerves which emerge from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11547" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">vertebral column</a>. There are twelve cranial nerves. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7518" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">facial nerve</a> is the seventh cranial nerve.
