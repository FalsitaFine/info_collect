
Clitorectomy: A surgical procedure in which all or part of the clitoris and sometimes also the labia are removed. Clitorectomy is a form of female circumcision (female genital mutilation).  Also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33610" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">clitoridectomy</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33611" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">clitoral amputation</a>.
