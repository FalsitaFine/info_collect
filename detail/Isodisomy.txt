
Isodisomy: A remarkable situation in which both chromosomes in a pair are from one parent only. Isodisomy causes some <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/birth_defects/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">birth defects</a> and is suspected to play a role in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a>. Also known as uniparental disomy.
