
Gene, regulatory: A gene that regulates the expression
of other genes. A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5283" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">regulatory gene</a> is a nosy gene whose prime preoccupation is to horn in
on other genes and control the rate at which they make products.
