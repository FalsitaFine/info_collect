
Male genitalia:  The genital organs of the male. These are usually separated by convention into the external and internal genitalia.
The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23052">male external genitalia</a> comprise the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4827">penis</a>, the male <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5907">urethra</a>, and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5424">scrotum</a>.
