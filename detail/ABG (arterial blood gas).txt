
ABG (arterial blood gas): The sampling of the blood levels of oxygen and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20132" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">carbon dioxide</a> within the arteries, as opposed to the levels of oxygen and carbon dioxide in venous blood.  Typically, the acidity, or pH, of the blood is measured simultaneously in ABG sampling.
