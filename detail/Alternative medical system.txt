
Alternative medical system: An umbrella term for a number of practices beyond the scope of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33527" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">conventional medicine</a>.  These forms of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2211" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">alternative medicine</a> are built upon a complete system of ideas and practice and may have evolved in Western or non-Western cultures.  Examples include Ayurveda, Chiropractic, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3775" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Homeopathy</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10788" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Naturopathic medicine</a>, Osteopathy, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33520" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Traditional Chinese medicine</a>.
