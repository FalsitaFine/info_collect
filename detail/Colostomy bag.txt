
Colostomy bag: A removable, disposable bag that attaches to the exterior opening of a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2796" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">colostomy</a> (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5559" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">stoma</a>) to permit sanitary collection and disposal of bodily wastes.
