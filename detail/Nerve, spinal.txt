
Nerve, spinal: One of the nerves that originates in the spinal cord. There are 31 pairs of spinal nerves. They consist of the 8 cervical nerves, 12 thoracic nerves, 5 lumbar nerves, 5 sacral nerves, and 1 <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18051" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">coccygeal</a> nerve.
