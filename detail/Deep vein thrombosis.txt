
Deep vein thrombosis: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22122" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">blood clot</a> within a deep vein, typically in the thigh or leg. The blood clot (thrombus) can break off as an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3224" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">embolus</a> and make its way to the lung, where it can cause lung problems. Abbreviated <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3135" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">DVT</a>. Also known as economy-class syndrome.
