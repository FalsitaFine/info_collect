
Activities of daily living (ADLs): The things we  normally do in daily living including any daily activity we perform for self-care such as  feeding ourselves, bathing, dressing, grooming, work, homemaking, and leisure. The  ability or inability to perform <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2152" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ADLs</a> can be used as a very practical measure of  ability/disability in many disorders.
