
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/canker_sores/article.htm">Aphthous ulcer</a>: A small sensitive painful <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11847">ulcer</a> crater in the lining of the mouth. Commonly called a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/canker_sores/article.htm">canker sore</a>.
