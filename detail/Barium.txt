
Barium: 1. A metallic element belonging to the alkaline earths with an atomic number of 56 and an atomic weight of 137.34. The symbol for barium is Ba.<br> 2. In medicine, refers to a chalky solution of barium used to coat the inside of organs, such as the stomach or intestines so that they will show up on an x-ray.<br>  See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/barium_enema/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">Barium enema</a>; Barium swallow.</br></br>
