
Heparin, low-weight: A newer form of the drug 
heparin (brand name: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/enoxaparin/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="PHARM">Lovenox</a>, Fragmin) that has a lower 
molecular weight than normal heparin. Fewer blood tests are 
needed, and it may be . superior to regular 
(unfractionated) heparin in cases of unstable <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/angina_symptoms/article.htm">angina</a> and 
other cardiac diseases. See also heparin.
