
ITP: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/idiopathic_thrombocytopenic_purpura_itp/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura</a>. A condition characterized by the sudden, abnormal lowering of the platelet count. The cause is unknown, but the immune system seems to play a major role in the process by eliminating the platelets. ITP can lead to dangerously low platelet counts that can be associated with spontaneous bleeding. Purpura are purplish <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/bruises/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">bruises</a> under the skin that are often seen in this condition. ITP requires treatments directed at suppressing the immune system.
