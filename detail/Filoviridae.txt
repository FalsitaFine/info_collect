
Filoviridae: A family of  viruses that cause hemorrhagic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a>.  Filoviruses have single-
stranded RNA as their genetic material.  Ebola virus and the Marburg virus are both filoviruses.
