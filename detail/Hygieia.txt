
Hygieia: In Greek mythology, a goddess considered to be the guardian or personification of health. Hygieia was the daughter of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33627" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Asclepius</a>, the god of healing. In Greek, "hygieia" means health.
