
Immunoglobulin A: A major class of immunoglobulins (antibodies) found in serum and external body secretions such as saliva, tears, and sweat, as well as in the gastrointestinal, respiratory, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3579" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">genitourinary</a> tracts. Abbreviated IgA. About 10 percent to 15 percent of the antibodies present in the body are IgA antibodies.
