
Miscarriage: Inadvertent loss of a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pregnancy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">pregnancy</a> before the fetus is viable. A considerable proportion of pregnancies end in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/miscarriage_causes_and_symptoms/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">miscarriage</a>. Also known as spontaneous abortion.
