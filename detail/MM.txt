
MM: In medicine, the abbreviation for 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4350">meningomyelocele</a> (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5526">spina bifida</a>), 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4262">malignant melanoma</a>, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/multiple_myeloma/article.htm">multiple myeloma</a>. 
(In lower case letters, mm stands for millimeter.)
mm: Abbreviation for millimeters. For example, mm Hg means millimeters of mercury, the height of a column of mercury, as in a blood pressure reading. 

(In upper-case letters, MM stands for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4350">meningomyelocele</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4262">malignant melanoma</a>, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/multiple_myeloma/article.htm">multiple myeloma</a>.)
