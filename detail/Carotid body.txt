
Carotid body: A small "body" of tissue rich in capillaries, at the spot the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2641" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">carotid artery</a> branches in the neck, containing cells that sense the oxygen and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20132" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">carbon dioxide</a> levels in blood and from which messages are dispatched to the medulla (in the brain) to regulate the heart rate.
