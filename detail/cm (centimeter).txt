
cm (centimeter): A unit of measure in the metric system which is 1/100'th of a meter. There are 2.54 centimeters (cms) in one inch. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10876" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">centimeter</a> is commonly used in medicine to state the size of objects or distance between points.
