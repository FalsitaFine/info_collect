
Endoscopy, upper: A procedure that enables the examiner (usually a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3553" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">gastroenterologist</a>) to examine the esophagus, the stomach, and the first portion of small bowel (duodenum) by using a thin, flexible tube that can be looked through or seen through on a TV monitor. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3323" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">esophagogastroduodenoscopy</a> (EGD).
