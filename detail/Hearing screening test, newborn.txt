
Hearing screening test, newborn: Screening of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/babies_quiz/quiz.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="quiz">newborn</a> baby's ability to hear. Newborn screening of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/deafness/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hearing</a> is done with automated auditory brainstem response tests or, less often, with what are called otoacoustic emission or conventional auditory brainstem response tests. The aim is to detect those <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/babies_quiz/quiz.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="quiz">babies</a> with hearing deficits and teach them <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sign_language/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">sign language</a> in infancy or give them hearing aids or cochlear implants.
