
Debride: To remove dead, contaminated, or adherent tissue and/or foreign material. To debride a wound is to remove all materials that may promote infection and impede healing. This may be done by enzymes (as with proteolytic enzymes), mechanical methods (as in a whirlpool), or sharp <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40483" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">debridement</a> (using intruments).
