
Deltoid: The muscle, roughly triangular in shape, that stretches from the collarbone (clavicle) over the shoulder to the upper bone of the arm (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3814" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">humerus</a>). It contracts to move the arm up from the side.
