
Forceps, obstetrical: A forceps designed as an aid in the vaginal delivery of a baby. Forceps may be used to ease delivery or to cope with problems of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3418" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">fetal distress</a> or fetal position. The decision to use forceps must be made by an obstetrician.
