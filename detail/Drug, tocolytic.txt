
Drug, tocolytic: A medication that can inhibit labor, slow down or
halt the contractions of the uterus. Tocolytic agents are widely used today to treat
premature labor and permit <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pregnancy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">pregnancy</a> to proceed and so permit the fetus to gain in size
and maturity before being born.
