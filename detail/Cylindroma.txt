
Cylindroma: A benign tumor of skin <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2153">adnexa</a> such as the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9301">sweat gland</a>, arising as a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4576">nodule</a> on the scalp and, less often, the face or limbs. Cylindromas may cover the scalp and so are called turban tumors. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26101">Familial cylindromatosis</a>.
