
Eyes, spots in front of the: Also known as "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/eye_floaters/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">floaters</a>", blurry spots that drift in front of the eyes but do not block vision. The blur is the result of debris from the vitreous casting a shadow on the retina. The spot is the image formed by a deposit of protein drifting about in the vitreous, the clear gel that fills the middle of the eye.
