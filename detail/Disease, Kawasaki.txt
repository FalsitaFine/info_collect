
Disease, Kawasaki: A syndrome of unknown origin,
mainly affecting young children, causing <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a>, reddening of the eyes (conjunctivitis),
lips and mucous membranes of the mouth, ulcerative <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/gum_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">gum disease</a> (gingivitis), swollen
glands in the neck (cervical lymphadenopathy), and a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">rash</a> that is raised and bright red
(maculoerythematous) in a glove-and-sock fashion over the skin of the hands and feet which
becomes hard, swollen (edematous), and peels off. Also called mucocutaneous lymph node
syndrome.
