
Meibomianitis: Inflammation of the little glands in the tarsus of the eyelids. Chronic inflammation of these glands can lead to a lump in the eyelid known as a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/chalazion/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">chalazion</a>. Also known as meibomitis.
