
Crapulent: Ill from excessive drinking or eating. Surcharged with liquor, or sick with intemperance. 
The word comes from the Late Latin crapulentus (very drunk), from the Latin crapula (drunkenness). See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/angina_symptoms/article.htm19">Crapulence</a>.
