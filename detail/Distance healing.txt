
Distance healing: Healing in which people seek to help patients simply with the power of the mind. Distance healing can include anything from <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33524" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">therapeutic touch</a> -- in which practitioners, without touching their patients, try to alter their energy fields -- to praying for people who are ill. Distance healing is part of the practice of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2211" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">alternative medicine</a>.
