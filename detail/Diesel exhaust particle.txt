
Diesel exhaust particle: A respirable particle produced during the compression ignition of diesel fuel. Diesel particles are composed of elemental and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11780">organic</a> carbon compounds as well as trace amount of other elements with toxic properties including transition metals. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13441">Diesel exhaust</a>.
