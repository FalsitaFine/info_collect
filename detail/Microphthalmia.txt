
Microphthalmia: An abnormally <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16258" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">small eye</a>. Microphthalmia is a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2820" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">congenital malformation</a> of the globe, a birth defect of the eye. A related term, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16199" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">anophthalmia</a>, indicates that there is no eye at all. Also known as microphthalmos.
