
Fear of marriage: An abnormal and persistent fear of being married. Sufferers of this phobia  experience undue <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anxiety/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anxiety</a> even though they may rationally realize that the married state itself poses no threat to them. They may fear the challenge of living with another person and the responsibility of rearing a family. Or they may worry about failing as a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sexual_sex_problems_in_men/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">sexual</a> partner.
Fear of being married is termed "gametophobia," a word derive from the Greek "gamete" (wife) and "gamein" (to marry).
