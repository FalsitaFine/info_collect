
Homo erectus: A primate believed to have been an extinct ancestor of modern humans being (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40191" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Homo sapiens</a>). Known to have been tall (average 1.78 m or 5 feet, 10 inches), erect stature, smaller brain (about 75% the size of Homo sapiens), and a lower forehead than modern humans. Homo erectus is Latin for erect or upright man.
