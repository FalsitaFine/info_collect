
Elastase 1: Pancreatic elastase, a member of the pancreatic family of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22833" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">serine</a> proteases. Although termed elastase, this powerful protease can hydrolyze numerous proteins, including elastin. The gene ELA1 that encodes elastase 1 is on chromosome 12q13.
