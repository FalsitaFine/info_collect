
Laryngoscopy: Examination of the larynx (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9019" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">voice box</a>), either with a mirror (indirect laryngoscopy) or with a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6221" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">laryngoscope</a> (direct laryngoscopy).
