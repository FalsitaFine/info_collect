
Andrology: The branch of medicine concerned with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/mens_health/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">men's health</a>, particularly male <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/infertility/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">infertility</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sexual_sex_problems_in_men/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">sexual</a> dysfunction. Andrology is literally the study of man (Greek andros, man). It is the male counterpart of gynecology.
