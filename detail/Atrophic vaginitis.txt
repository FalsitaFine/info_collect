
Atrophic vaginitis: Thinning of the lining (endothelium) of the vagina due to decreased production of estrogen. Atrophic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/vaginitis_overview/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">vaginitis</a> may occur with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/menopause/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">menopause</a>.
