
Embryonic hemoglobin: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hemoglobin/article.htm">Hemoglobin</a> E, the normal embryonic hemoglobin, the main type of hemoglobin found in the human embryo. The E stands for embryonic and also for epsilon, the chain unique to embryonic hemoglobin (which was originally known as Gower-2).
