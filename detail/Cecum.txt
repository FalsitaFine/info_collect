
Cecum: The first portion of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/erythema_nodosum/article.htm7" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">large bowel</a>, which is situated in the lower-right quadrant of the abdomen. The cecum receives fecal material from the small bowel (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6451" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ileum</a>), which opens into it. The appendix is attached to the cecum.
