
Excisional: Pertaining to the act of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38490" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">excision</a>, of removal by surgery. An <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=30999" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">excisional biopsy</a> is one in which the lesion is removed by the biopsy. From the Greek ektome meaning excision.
