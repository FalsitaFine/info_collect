
Glossitis: Inflammation of the tongue. There are many possible causes of glossitis, including vitamin B12 deficiency, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sjogrens_syndrome/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Sjogren's syndrome</a>, and side effects of medications or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/chemotherapy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">chemotherapy</a>.
