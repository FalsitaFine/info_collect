
Amitriptyline:  An antidepressant medication. In some patients with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/depression/article.htm">depression</a>, abnormal levels of brain chemicals called neurotransmitters may relate to the depression. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/amitriptyline/article.htm">Amitriptyline</a> elevates mood by raising the level of neurotransmitters in brain tissue. Amitriptyline is also a sedative that is useful for depressed patients with insomnia, restlessness, and nervousness. It is sometimes used to treat <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/fibromyalgia_facts/article.htm">fibromyalgia</a> and symptoms related to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/chronic_pain/article.htm">chronic pain</a>. Brand names are Elavil and Endep. A generic version is available.
