
Diabetes, brittle: A type of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diabetes_mellitus/article.htm">diabetes</a> when a person's blood <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3608">glucose</a> (sugar) level often swings quickly from high to low and from low to high. Also called "unstable diabetes" or "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7271">labile diabetes</a>."
