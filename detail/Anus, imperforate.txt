
Anus, imperforate: A birth defect in which the rectum is a blind alley and there is no anus. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3947" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Imperforate anus</a> occurs in about 1 in 5,000 births, and it can be corrected by surgery.
