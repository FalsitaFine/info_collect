
Antimicrobial agent: A general term for drugs, chemicals, or other substances that either kill or slow the growth of microbes.  Among the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9621" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">antimicrobial</a> agents are antibacterial drugs, antiviral agents, antifungal agents, and antiparasitic drugs.
