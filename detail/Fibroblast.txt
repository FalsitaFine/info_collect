
Fibroblast: A type of cell found in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2824" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">connective tissue</a> throughout the body that produces collagen and other proteins found in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24384" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">extracellular</a> (between cells) spaces.
