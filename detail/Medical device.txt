
Medical device: 1. Broadly, any physical   item used in medical treatment, from a cardiac <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pacemaker/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">pacemaker</a> to a wheelchair.<br> 2.In insurance terms,    usually synonymous an assistive device, although it may include items more frequently thought of as medical supplies, such as dressings needed for wound care at home   or syringes for self-administration of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/insulin/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">insulin</a>.   </br>
