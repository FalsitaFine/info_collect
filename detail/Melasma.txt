
Melasma: Pigmentation of the cheeks of the face (malar area). When it occurs during <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pregnancy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">pregnancy</a> it is referred to as chloasma or the mask of pregnancy. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/melasma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Melasma</a> darkens with sun exposure.
