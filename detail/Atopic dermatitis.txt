
Atopic dermatitis: A skin disease characterized by areas of severe <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/itch/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">itching</a>, redness, scaling, and loss of the surface of the skin. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/atopic_dermatitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Atopic dermatitis</a> is the most common of the many types of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/eczema_facts/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">eczema</a>. Atopic dermatitis is frequently associated with other allergic disorders, especially <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/asthma_overview/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">asthma</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hay_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hay fever</a>. A defect of the immune system within the skin has been detected in patients who have atopic dermatitis, but the reason for the defect is unknown.
