
Anticoagulant: An agent that is used to prevent the formation of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/blood_clots/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">blood clots</a>. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anticoagulants_drug_class_of_blood_thinners/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">Anticoagulants</a> have various uses. Some are used for the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/prevention/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">prevention</a> or treatment of disorders characterized by abnormal blood clots and emboli. Examples of diseases and conditions that require <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anticoagulants_drug_class_of_blood_thinners/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">anticoagulant</a> treatment to reduce the risk of blood clots include <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/heart_attack/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">heart attack</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/stroke_symptoms_and_treatment/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">stroke</a>, deep venous thrombosis, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pulmonary_embolism/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">pulmonary embolism</a>, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/atrial_fibrillation/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">atrial fibrillation</a>.
