
Atropine psychosis: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5613">syndrome</a> characterized by 
dry mouth, blurred vision, forgetfulness, and difficulty 
with urination triggered by <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10952">atropine</a> and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2281">anticholinergic</a> effects of other drugs, particularly anti-psychotic medications. Treatment is to reduce or stop the 
medication.
