
Hirudotherapy: The use of leeches for medical therapy.  From <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3762" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hirudin</a>, the active principle in the salivary secretion of leeches that acts as a potent anticoagulant (blood thinner).
