
Intracranial hematoma: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hematoma/article.htm">hematoma</a> (a collection  of blood) within the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2862">cranium</a> (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5507">skull</a>). The bleed may or may not be within the brain itself. 
See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32325">Epidural hematoma</a>; <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32330">Subdural hematoma</a>; <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32327">Intracerebral hematoma</a>.
