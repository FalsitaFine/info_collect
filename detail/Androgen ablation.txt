
Androgen ablation: Treatment designed to suppress or block the production of male hormones. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38548" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Androgen suppression</a> can be achieved by surgical removal of the testicles, by taking female sex hormones, or by taking other drugs called antiandrogens. Also called androgen suppression.
