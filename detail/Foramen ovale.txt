
Foramen ovale: An oval opening between the two upper chambers of the heart (the atria) that is a normal feature of fetal and newborn <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2735" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">circulation</a>. The foramen ovale normally closes by 3 months of age.
