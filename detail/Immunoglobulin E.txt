
Immunoglobulin E: A class of immunoglobulins that includes the antibodies that are important in an allergic response. Abbreviated IgE. A person who has an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/allergy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">allergy</a> often has elevated blood levels of IgE. IgE antibodies attack and engage the invading army of allergens.
