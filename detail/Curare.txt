
Curare: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=77812" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">muscle relaxant</a> used in anesthesia (and, in the past, in arrow poisons by South American Indians). Curare competes with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23278" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">acetylcholine</a>, a chemical that carries information between nerve and muscle cells, and blocks transmission of the information.
