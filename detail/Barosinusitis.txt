
Barosinusitis: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sinusitis/article.htm">Sinus</a> troubles, particularly with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4723">pain</a>, due to
changing atmospheric pressures, as when going up or down in a plane. Also called
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2162"> aerosinusitis</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5496">sinus barotrauma</a>.
