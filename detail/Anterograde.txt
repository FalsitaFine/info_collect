
Anterograde: Moving forward or extending forward. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15298" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Anterograde memory</a> is memory for what occurs after an event such as an accident. Also called antegrade. From the Latin antero- + gredior (to step or go).
