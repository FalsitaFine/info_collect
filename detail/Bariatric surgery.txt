
Bariatric surgery: Surgery on   the stomach and/or intestines to help a person with extreme <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/obesity_weight_loss/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">obesity</a> lose weight.   Bariatric surgery is an option for   people who have a body mass index (BMI)   above 40. Surgery is also an option for people with a body mass index between 35 and 40 who   have health problems like type 2 <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diabetes_mellitus/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">diabetes</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/heart_disease_coronary_artery_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">heart disease</a>.
