
Ankle pain: Pain in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8983">ankle</a>, a "hinged" joint. Ankle pain is commonly due to  a sprain or tendinitis.
The severity of ankle sprains ranges from mild (which can resolve within 24 hours) to
severe (which can require surgical repair). <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13023">Tendinitis</a> of the ankle can be caused by
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8171">trauma</a> or inflammatory forms of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2337">arthritis</a>. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10612">Achilles tendonitis</a>; <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24348">Ankle sprain</a>.
