
Herbicide chemical 2,4-DCP: A poisonous chemical used to produce a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25547" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">herbicide</a>. 2,4-DCP (short for 2,4-dichlorophenol) is employed in the manufacture of the herbicide 2,4-D (which is short for 2,4-dichloropheoxyacetic acid). 2,4-DCP is so highly toxic that even a small amount on the skin can be lethal. Five chemical-plant workers died from 2,4-DCP exposure in the US from 1980 -98. There is no known antidote to 2,4-DCP poisoning.
