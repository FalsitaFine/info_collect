
Calcaneocuboid joint: The joint located in the foot between the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9013" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">calcaneus</a> bone and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9014" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cuboid bone</a>. It is a gliding type of joint. The ligaments that serve to support and strengthen this joint are called the capsular, dorsal calcaneocuboid, bifurcated, long plantar, and plantar calcaneocuboid ligaments.
