
Advance directives: These directives pertain to treatment preferences and the designation of a surrogate decision-maker in the event that a person should become unable to make medical decisions on their own behalf. Advance directives generally fall into three categories: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4181" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">living will</a>, power of attorney and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3666" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">health care proxy</a>.
