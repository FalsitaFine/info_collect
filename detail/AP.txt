
AP: AP is a versatile abbreviation. It serves in cardiology to
abbreviate <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/angina_symptoms/article.htm">angina</a> pectoris (AP) and arterial pressure (AP). In endocrinology, it stands
for the anterior pituitary (AP). And in anatomy, AP means anteroposterior, i.e., from
front-to-back.
