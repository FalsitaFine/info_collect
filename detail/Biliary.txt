
Biliary: Having to do with the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3536">gallbladder</a>, bile <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10672">ducts</a>, or bile. The biliary system itself consists of the gallbladder and bile ducts and, of course, the bile.  
For example, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16255">biliary atresia</a> is the absence or closure of the major bile ducts, the ducts that drain bile from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4179">liver</a>.
