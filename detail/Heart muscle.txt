
Heart muscle: A type of muscle with unique features only found in the heart. The heart muscle, or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/angina_symptoms/article.htm9" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cardiac muscle</a>, is medically called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/polymyalgia_rheumatica/article.htm7" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">myocardium</a> ("myo-" being the prefix denoting muscle).
