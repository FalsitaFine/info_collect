
Asthenia: Weakness. Lack of energy and strength. Loss of strength.  Myasthenia refers to a loss of muscle strength, as in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/myasthenia_gravis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">myasthenia gravis</a>.   Asthenia is from the Greek asthenes, from a- (without) + sthenos (strength).
