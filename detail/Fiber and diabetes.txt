
Fiber and diabetes: Soluble fibers (oat bran, apples,  citrus, pears, peas/beans, psyllium, etc.) slow down the digestion of carbohydrates  (sugars), which may results in better <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/glucose/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">glucose</a> metabolism, particularly with post-meal glucose elevations.
