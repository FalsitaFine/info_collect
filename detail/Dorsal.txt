
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9282" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Dorsal</a>: Relating to the back or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9277" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">posterior</a> of a structure. As opposed to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9289" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ventral</a>, or front, of the structure. Some of the dorsal surfaces of the body are the back, buttocks, calves, and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4118" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">knuckle</a> side of the hand.
