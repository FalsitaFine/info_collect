
GDB: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10721" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Genome Database</a> (GDB), the official central repository for genomic mapping data resulting from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3818" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Human Genome</a> Initiative. Established at Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore, Maryland, USA in 1990. In 1999, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16836" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Bioinformatics</a> Supercomputing Centre (BiSC) at The Hospital for Sick Children in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, assumed the management of GDB.
