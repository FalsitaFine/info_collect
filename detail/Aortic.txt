
Aortic: Pertaining to the  aorta, the largest artery in the body.
"Aortic" is in common usage as, for example,  in an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/abdominal_aortic_aneurysm/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">abdominal aortic aneurysm</a>. The term "aortal" has the same meaning but is rarely encountered (except in The New York Times crossword).
