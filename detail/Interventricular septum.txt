
Interventricular septum: The stout wall that separates the lower chambers (the ventricles) of the heart from one another. A hole in the interventricular septum is termed a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/ventricular_septal_defect/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">ventricular septal defect</a> (VSD).
