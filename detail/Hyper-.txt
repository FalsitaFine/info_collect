
Hyper-: Prefix meaning high, beyond, excessive, or above normal, as in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hyperglycemia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hyperglycemia</a> (high sugar in the blood) and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hypercalcemia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hypercalcemia</a> (high calcium in the blood). The opposite of hyper- is hypo-.
