
Enteropathy, protein-losing: A condition in which an excessive amount of plasma protein is lost into the intestine. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5083" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Protein-losing enteropathy</a> can be due to diverse causes, including celiac sprue, extensive ulceration of the intestine, intestinal lymphatic blockage, and infiltration of leukemic cells into the intestinal wall.
