
American Association for the Advancement of Science: Spoken of as the "triple-AS", the American Association for the Advancement of Science is an organization concerned not only with the biomedical sciences but with all of the sciences. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2071" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">AAAS</a> publishes the weekly journal "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13754" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Science</a>", one of the more famous scientific periodicals. "Science" carries a wide range of new scientific information.
