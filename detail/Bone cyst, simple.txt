
Bone cyst, simple: A solitary fluid-filled cavity (cyst) in a bone, usually in the shaft of a long bone, especially the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3814" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">humerus</a>, in a child. A simple bone cyst can cause pain in or near the bone. Also known as unicameral bone cyst and solitary bone cyst.
