
Holandric inheritance: Inheritance of genes on the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6049" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Y chromosome</a>. Because only males normally have Y chromosomes, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6059" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Y-linked</a> genes can only be transmitted from father to son.
