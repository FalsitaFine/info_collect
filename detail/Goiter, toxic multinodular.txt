
Goiter, toxic multinodular: A condition in which the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5778" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroid gland</a> contains multiple lumps (nodules) that are overactive and that produce excess <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5780" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroid hormones</a>. Also known as Parry disease and Plummer disease.
