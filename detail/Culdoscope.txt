
Culdoscope: The viewing tube (endoscope) introduced through the end of the vagina into the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8463" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">rectouterine pouch</a> (the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8502" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pouch of Douglas</a>), an extension of the peritoneal cavity between the rectum and back wall of the uterus, in a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8497" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">culdoscopy</a>.
