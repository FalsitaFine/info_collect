
Acute coronary syndromes: A spectrum of conditions involving chest discomfort or other symptoms caused by lack of oxygen to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9658" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">heart muscle</a> (the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/polymyalgia_rheumatica/article.htm7" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">myocardium</a>). The unification of these manifestations of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10267" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">coronary artery disease</a> under a single term reflects the understanding that these are caused by a similar pathophysiology (sequence of pathologic events) characterized by erosion, fissuring, or rupture of a pre-existing plaque, leading to thrombosis (clotting) within the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7250" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">coronary arteries</a> and impaired blood supply to the heart muscle.
