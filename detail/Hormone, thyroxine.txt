
Hormone, thyroxine: A chemical substance made by the
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5778" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict"> thyroid gland</a> The thyroid gland uses iodine to make <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5780" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroid hormones</a>. Thyroxine (T4), one
of the most important thyroid hormones, has four iodine molecules attached to its
molecular structure.
