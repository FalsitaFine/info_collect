
Acromioclavicular joint: A gliding joint located between the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9699" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">acromion</a> (a projection of the scapula that forms the point of the shoulder) and the clavicle (the collar bone). It is served and supported by the capsular, superior, and inferior acromioclavicular ligaments; the articular disk; and the coracoclavicular (trapezoid and conoid) ligaments. Abbreviated AC joint.
