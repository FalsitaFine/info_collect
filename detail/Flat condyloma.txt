
Flat condyloma: A manifestation of the secondary stage of syphilis that takes the form of broad flat wartlike growths in moist creased areas, as around the anus and external genitalia. Also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11425" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">condyloma latum</a>.
