
Adenopathy: Large or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/swollen_lymph_nodes/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">swollen lymph nodes</a>. Lymph nodes can become enlarged as a result of inflammatory diseases, infection, or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a>. Synonymous with lymphadenopathy.
