
Bartter syndrome: A group of disorders that are inherited in an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15971" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">autosomal recessive</a> manner and are characterized by impaired salt reabsorption by the kidney with pronounced salt wasting, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3857" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hypokalemia</a> (low blood potassium), <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6852" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">alkalosis</a> (an alkaline body pH), and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24224" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hypercalciuria</a> (high urine calcium).
