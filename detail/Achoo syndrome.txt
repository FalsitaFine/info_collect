
Achoo syndrome: A disorder characterized by nearly uncontrollable paroxysms of sneezing provoked in a reflex fashion by the sudden exposure of a dark-adapted subject to intensely bright light, usually to brilliant sunlight. The number of successive sneezes is usually 2 or 3, but can be up to about 40. The Achoo syndrome is also called  the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15801" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">photic sneeze reflex</a> or the helio-ophthalmic outburst syndrome.
