
Gamma globulin: A major  class of immunoglobulins found in the blood, including many of the most common antibodies circulating in the blood. Also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=21067" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immunoglobulin G</a> (IgG).
