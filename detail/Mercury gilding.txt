
Mercury gilding: A perilous process for gold-plating in which an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11923">amalgam</a> of mercury and gold is applied to an object and then exposed to heat to vaporize the mercury and leave the gold behind in a thin layer. The risk is <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10991">mercury poisoning</a>. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39600">Fire gilding</a>.
