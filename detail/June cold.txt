
June cold:  A popular term for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hay_fever/article.htm">hay fever</a>. Although the symptoms of a "June cold" may be quite similar to those of a real cold, the term "June cold" is a misnomer. Unlike 
a real cold, it is not caused by a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5997">virus</a>, but by <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/allergy/article.htm">allergy</a>. It is therefore also known as allergic rhinitis a runny nose due to an allergy.
