
Myelogenous: Referring to the nonlymphocytic groups of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6017">white blood cells</a>, including the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8780">granulocytes</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4426">monocytes</a> and  <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4941">platelets</a>. 

Synonymous with myeloid. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=19283">Acute myelogenous leukemia</a> is therefore also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=19297">acute myeloid leukemia</a>.
