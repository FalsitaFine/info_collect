
Cast: 1) A protective shell of fiberglass, plastic, or plaster, and 
bandage that is molded to protect broken or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/broken_bone_types_of_bone_fractures/article.htm">fractured</a> limb(s) as it heals. 2) An 
abnormal mass of dead cells that forms in a body cavity. For example, cases of 
cells that form in the tubules of the kidneys are sometimes detected in urine 
samples.
