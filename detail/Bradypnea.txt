
Bradypnea: Abnormally slow breathing. A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5330" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">respiratory rate</a> that is too slow. The normal rate of respirations (breaths per minute) depends on a number of factors, including the age of the individual and the degree of exertion.
