
Aneurysm, brain: An <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/brain_aneurysm/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">aneurysm</a> of a blood vessel in the brain, usually due to a defect in the vessel at birth or from <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/high_blood_pressure_hypertension/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">high blood pressure</a>. Rupture of the aneurysm causes a sudden severe <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/headache/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">headache</a>, often with nausea, vomiting, decreased consciousness, and can be life threatening.
