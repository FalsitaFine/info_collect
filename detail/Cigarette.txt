
Cigarette: A small roll of finely cut tobacco enclosed in a wrapper of thin paper designed for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/smoking_and_quitting_smoking/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">smoking</a>.
"Cigarettes are the only product sold on the free market that, when used as directed, can kill people." (L Chasan-Taber &amp; M Stampfer, New Engl J Med 2001;345:1841-2) The word "cigarette" is the French diminutive of "cigare" (cigar), from the Spanish "cigarro."
