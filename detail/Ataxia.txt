
Ataxia: Poor coordination and unsteadiness due to the brain's failure to regulate the body's posture and regulate the strength and direction of limb movements. Ataxia is usually due to disease in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2672" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cerebellum</a> of the brain, which lies beneath the back part of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2678" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cerebrum</a>.
