
Eczema, contact: A localized reaction that involves redness, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/itch/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">itching</a>, and burning that occurs where the skin has come into contact with an allergen (an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/allergy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">allergy</a>-causing substance) or an irritant such as an acid, a cleaning agent, or another chemical.
