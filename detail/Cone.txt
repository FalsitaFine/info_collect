
Cone: A type of specialized light-sensitive cell (photoreceptor) in the retina of the eye that provides color vision and sharp <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8544" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">central vision</a>.  By contrast, the rods are the retinal photoreceptors that provide side vision and the ability to see objects in dim light (night vision).
