
Nasal polyp: A growth or protrusion of tissue in the nose or sinuses. Nasal polyps may produce no symptoms or may lead to obstruction of the nasal passages, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sinusitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">sinus infection</a>, and a decreased sense of smell. Nasal polyps are almost always benign. Treatment can involve medications or surgery.
