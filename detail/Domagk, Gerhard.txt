
Domagk, Gerhard: German <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4887">physician</a> and chemist (1895-1946) who discovered the first <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=14498">sulfa drug</a>, prontosil, which ushered in the era of antibacterial medicine.

For more information, see <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=19325">Prontosil</a>.
