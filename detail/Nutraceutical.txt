
Nutraceutical: A food or part of a food that allegedly provides medicinal or health benefits, including the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/prevention/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">prevention</a> and treatment of disease. A nutraceutical may be a naturally nutrient-rich or medicinally active food, such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/garlic_allium_sativum_l-oral/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">garlic</a> or soybeans, or it may be a specific component of a food, such as the omega-3 fish oil that can be derived from salmon and other cold-water fish.
