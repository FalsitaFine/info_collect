
Myocardial infarction: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/heart_attack/article.htm">heart attack</a>. Abbreviated MI.
The term "myocardial infarction" focuses on the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/polymyalgia_rheumatica/article.htm7">myocardium</a> (the heart muscle) and the changes that occur in it due to the sudden deprivation of circulating blood. The main change is <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4514">necrosis</a> (death) of myocardial tissue.
