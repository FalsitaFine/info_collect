
Enfeeble: To make feeble or weaken. Years of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2731" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chronic illness</a> may leave someone enfeebled. The word enfeeble is not often used in medicine, perhaps because of a negative connotation of the word.
