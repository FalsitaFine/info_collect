
genesis: A suffix referring to the beginning, development, or production of something. For example, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12352" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">gametogenesis</a> is the development and production of the male and female germ cells (the gametes) required to form a new individual. Osteogenesis is the production of bone. Pathogenesis is the development of a disease, the sequence of events leading up to that disease. The Latin word "genesis" is derived from the Greek "gignesthai" meaning "to be born."
