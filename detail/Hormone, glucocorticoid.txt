
Hormone, glucocorticoid: A hormone that predominantly
affects the metabolism of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15381" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">carbohydrates</a> and, to a lesser extent, fats and proteins (and has
other effects). Glucocorticoids are made in the outside portion (the cortex) of the
adrenal gland and chemically classed as steroids. Cortisol is the major natural
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3609" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict"> glucocorticoid</a>. The term glucocorticoid also applies to equivalent hormones synthesized in
the laboratory.
