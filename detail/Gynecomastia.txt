
Gynecomastia: Excessive development of the male
breasts. Temporary enlargement of the breasts is not unusual or abnormal in boys during
adolescence or during recovery from malnutrition. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/gynecomastia/article.htm">Gynecomastia</a> may be abnormal as, for
example, in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/klinefelter_syndrome/article.htm">Klinefelter's syndrome</a>.
