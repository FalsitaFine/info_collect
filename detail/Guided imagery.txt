
Guided imagery: An alternative medicine technique in which patients use their imagination to visualize improved health, or to 'attack' a disease, such as a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a>. Guided imagery may be utilized as complementary medicine in some oncology centers and other medical facilities.
