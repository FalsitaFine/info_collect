
Juxtapyloric: Near the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5148" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pylorus</a>, the muscular area at the junction of the stomach and the first part of the small intestine. For example, a juxtapyloric ulcer is located near the pylorus.
