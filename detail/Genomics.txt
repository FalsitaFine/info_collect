
Genomics: The study of genes and their function. Genomics aims to understand the structure of the genome, including the mapping genes and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5465" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">sequencing</a> the DNA. Genomics examines the molecular mechanisms and the interplay of genetic and environmental factors in disease.
