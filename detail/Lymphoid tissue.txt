
Lymphoid tissue: The part of the body's <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3907" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immune system</a> that is important for the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3906" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immune response</a> and helps protect it from infection and foreign bodies. Lymphoid tissue is present throughout the body and includes the lymph nodes, spleen, tonsils, adenoids, and other structures.
