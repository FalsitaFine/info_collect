
Gray: A unit of absorbed <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11946">radiation</a> equal to the dose of one joule of energy absorbed per kilogram of matter, or 100 <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11944">rad</a>. The unit is named for the British physician L. Harold Gray (1905-1965), an authority on the use of radiation in the treatment of cancer. The abbreviation for a gray is Gy. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32308">Milligray</a>. 
Gray is, of course, also a hue between black and white.
