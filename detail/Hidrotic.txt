
Hidrotic: Pertaining to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20783" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hidrosis</a>, sweating, the production of perspiration. Hidrotic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20848" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ectodermal dysplasia</a> is the form of this hereditary disease in which the patient can sweat, as opposed to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16310" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">anhidrotic</a> <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20841" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ectodermal</a> <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3142" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">dysplasia</a> in which the patient is unable to sweat.
