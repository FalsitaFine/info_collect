
Clinical depression: Depressed mood that meets the DSM-IV criteria for a depressive disorder. The term clinical <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/depression/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">depression</a> is commonly used to describe depression that is a type of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/mental_illness/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">mental illness</a>'not a normal, temporary mood caused by life events or grieving.
