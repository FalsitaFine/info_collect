
Immunocompetent: Able to develop an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3906" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immune response</a>. An immunocompetent body is able to <br/> recognize antigens and act against them. Immuno-<br/> competent is the opposite of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3936" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immunodeficient</a>.
