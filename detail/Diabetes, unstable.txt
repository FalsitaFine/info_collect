
Diabetes, unstable: A term used when a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11148">diabetic</a> 
person's blood <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3608">glucose</a> (sugar) level often swings quickly and widely from high to low and from low to high. Also called brittle and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7271">labile diabetes</a>.
