
Metabolic: Relating to metabolism, the whole range of biochemical processes that occur within us (or any living organism). Metabolism consists of anabolism (the buildup of substances) and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11103" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">catabolism</a> (the breakdown of substances). The term "metabolic" is often used to refer specifically to the breakdown of food and its transformation into energy.
