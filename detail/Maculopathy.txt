
Maculopathy: Any pathologic condition or disease of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4241" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">macula</a>, the small spot in the retina where vision is keenest. Also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32565" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">macular retinopathy</a>.
