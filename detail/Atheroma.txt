
Atheroma: A fatty deposit in the inner lining (intima) of an artery, resulting from <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15018" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">atherosclerosis</a>. Also called an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25928" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">atherosclerotic</a> plaque, an arterial plaque, or a plaque.
