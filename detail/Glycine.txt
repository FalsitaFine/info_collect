
Glycine: An amino acid, one of the 20 building blocks of protein. A nonessential amino acid, glycine is part of many proteins, participates in purine synthesis, and is a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9973" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">neurotransmitter</a>. Symbol: Gly.
