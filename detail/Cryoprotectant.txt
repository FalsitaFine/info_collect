
Cryoprotectant: A chemical component of a freezing solution used in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7252" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cryopreservation</a> to help protect what is being frozen from freeze damage. The chemical glycerol, for example, is commonly used as a cryoprotectant to protect frozen red blood cells.
