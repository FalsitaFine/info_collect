
Appendiceal: Relating to the appendix. As, for example, an appendiceal abscess. Perforation of the appendix can lead to a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40107" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">periappendiceal</a> abscess or diffuse peritonitis (infection of the entire lining of the abdomen and the pelvis).
