
Efferent: Carrying away. For example, an artery is an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3199" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">efferent vessel</a> that carries blood away from the heart, and an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3198" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">efferent nerve</a> carries impulses away from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2667" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">central nervous system</a>. The opposite of efferent is afferent.
