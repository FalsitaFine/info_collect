
Coccus: A bacterial cell that has the shape of a sphere. Coccus is part of the name of a number of bacteria, such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20162" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">enterococcus</a>, meningococcus, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4957" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pneumococcus</a>, staphylococcus, and streptococcus.
