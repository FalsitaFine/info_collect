
Femoral vein: The large vein in the groin that passes with the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40601" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">femoral artery</a> under the inguinal ligament to enter the abdomen, at which point it becomes the external iliac vein. The femoral vein is a continuation of the popliteal vein, and it carries blood back to the heart from the lower extremities.
