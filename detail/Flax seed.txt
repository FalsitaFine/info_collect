
Flax seed: The seed of the common flax plant (also known as linseed and, botanically, Linum usitatissimum). Flax seed and flax seed oil are a rich souce of ALA (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38994" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">alpha-linolenic acid</a>), the only one of the omega-3 <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15387" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">fatty acids</a> that is essential in the sense that the body cannot make it so, if one is to get it, it must be in the diet.
