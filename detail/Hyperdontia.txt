
Hyperdontia: the presence of extra (supernumerary) teeth. Hyperdontia can be associated with developmental abnormalities such as cleft lip and/or palate, Gardner syndrome, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cleidocranial_dysplasia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="DT">cleidocranial dysplasia</a>. Hyperdontia may lead to problems with alignment of the bite (malocclusion) and the formation of dentigerous <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cyst/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="DT">cysts</a>. Extra teeth may also become impacted and lead to damage to the root of an adjacent tooth.
