
Ear bones: Also know as the ossicles, these are the three tiny bones in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7867" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">middle ear</a> that carry the sound wave from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33105" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ear drum</a> into the cochlea.
