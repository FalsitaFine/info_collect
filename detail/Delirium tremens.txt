
Delirium tremens: A central nervous system symptom of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/alcohol_withdrawal_symptoms_and_signs/symptoms.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sym">alcohol withdrawal</a> that is seen in chronic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/alcohol_abuse_and_alcoholism/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">alcoholism</a>. Symptoms include uncontrollable trembling, hallucinations, severe <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anxiety/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anxiety</a>, sweating, and sudden feelings of terror. Abbreviated DTs. DTs can be both frightening and, in severe cases, deadly. Treatment includes observation, comfort care, and in some cases medication.
