
HLA-B57: An HLA type that is strongly associated with unusually slow progression of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/human_immunodeficiency_virus_hiv/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">human immunodeficiency virus</a> (HIV) from the primary infection to AIDS. In other words, HLA-B57 provides some protection against HIV progression.
