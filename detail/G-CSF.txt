
G-CSF: Granulocyte colony-stimulating factor. A colony-stimulating factor that stimulates the production of neutrophils (a type of white blood cell). G-CSF is a cytokine that belongs to the family of drugs called hematopoietic (blood-forming) agents. Also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/filgrastim/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">filgrastim</a>.
