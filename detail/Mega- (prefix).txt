
Mega- (prefix): The   combining form "mega-" comes from the Greek "megas", great or big and means   abnormally large. Examples include: Megalocephaly is too large a head. Megacardia is too large a   heart. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4332" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Megacolon</a> is too large a colon.
