
Acetabular: Pertaining to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2110" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">acetabulum</a>, the cup-shaped socket of the hip joint which is a key feature of the pelvis. The head (upper end) of the femur (the thighbone) fits into the acetabulum and articulates with it, forming a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8748" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ball-and-socket joint</a>.
