
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/birth_control_methods/article.htm">Birth control</a>: Birth control is the use of any practices, methods, or devices to prevent <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pregnancy/article.htm">pregnancy</a> from occurring in a sexually active woman. Also referred to as family planning, pregnancy prevention, fertility control, or contraception; birth control methods are designed either to prevent fertilization of an egg or implantation of a fertilized egg in the uterus.
