
Indolent lymphoma: A lymphoma that tends to grow and spread slowly, in contrast to an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25085" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">aggressive lymphoma</a> which tends to grow and spread quickly. Indolent lymphomas include chronic lymphocytic lymphoma and follicular small cleaved cell lymphoma. Also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25089" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">low-grade lymphoma</a>.
