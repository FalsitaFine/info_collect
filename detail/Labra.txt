
Labra: The plural of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18656" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">labrum</a>, a ring of fibrocartilage (fibrous cartilage) around the edge of the articular (joint) surface of a bone. In Latin, "labrum" means "lip." The term is generally used to designate a lip, edge, or brim.
