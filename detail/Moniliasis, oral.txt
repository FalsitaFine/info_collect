
Moniliasis, oral: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/yeast_infection_in_women_and_men/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Yeast infection</a> of the mouth and throat caused by a fungus formerly called <i>Monilia</i>, now known as Candida albicans. This infection is also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/thrush/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">thrush</a>. Yeast organisms are part of the germs normally found in various parts of the body. They ordinarily do not cause any symptoms.
