
Kernicterus: A disorder that is due to severe <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/jaundice_in_adults/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">jaundice</a> in the newborn, with deposition of the pigment bilirubin in the brain that causes damage to the brain, potentially leading to athetoid <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cerebral_palsy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cerebral palsy</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/deafness/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hearing loss</a>, vision problems, or mental retardation. Also known as bilirubin <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/encephalopathy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">encephalopathy</a>.
