
MCHC:  Abbreviation for mean cell <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hemoglobin/article.htm">hemoglobin</a> concentration, which is the average concentration of hemoglobin in a given volume of blood. The MCHC is a calculated value derived from the measurement of hemoglobin and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hematocrit/article.htm">hematocrit</a>. (The hemoglobin value is the amount of hemoglobin in a volume of blood while the hematocrit is the ratio of the volume of red cells to the volume of whole blood.) The MCHC is a standard part of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/complete_blood_count/article.htm">complete blood count</a>.
