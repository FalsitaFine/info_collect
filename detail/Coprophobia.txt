
Coprophobia: An abnormal and persistent fear of feces (bowel waste). Sufferers of coprophobia experience <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anxiety/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anxiety</a> even though they realize their fear is irrational. They go out of their way to avoid coming into contact with feces or sometimes even seeing feces.
"Coprophobia" is derived from the Greek "kopros" (dung) and "phobos" (fear). Alternate names: Koprophobia, scatophobia.
