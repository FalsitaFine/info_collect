
Ear tag: A rudimentary tag of ear tissue, often containing a core of cartilage, usually located just in front of the ear (auricle). This <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12759" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">minor anomaly</a> is common and harmless. However, the presence of two or more minor anomalies such as this one in a child increases the probability that the child has a major malformation. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5017" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">preauricular tag</a>.
