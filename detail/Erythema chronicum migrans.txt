
Erythema chronicum migrans: The classic initial <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm">rash</a> of
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/lyme_disease/article.htm"> Lyme disease</a>. In the early phase of the illness, within hours to weeks of the tick bite,
the local skin develops an expanding ring of unraised redness. There may be an outer ring
of brighter redness and a central area of clearing. For more information, see <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/lyme_disease/article.htm">Lyme Disease</a>.
