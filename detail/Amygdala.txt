
Amygdala: 1. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39203">amygdaloid nucleus</a> in the brain. 
2. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5815">tonsils</a>.<pr>
These structures were so named because they appeared to be shaped like an almond. From the Greek amydale (almond) + the Greek eidos (like).</pr>
