
Homotrisomy: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38317">Trisomy</a> of the same <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=14018">chromosome</a>. The occurrence of two children in the same family with trisomy 21 is an instance of homotrisomy. 
See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38320">Heterotrisomy</a>.
