
Jennerian method: The production of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3908">immunity</a> to a disease by inoculation of an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13398">attenuated</a> form of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5997">virus</a> causing the disease. Also called jennerization. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25619">Jennerization</a>.
