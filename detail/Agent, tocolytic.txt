
Agent, tocolytic: A medication that can inhibit labor, slow down or  halt the contractions of the uterus. Tocolytic agents are widely used today to treat  premature labor and permit <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pregnancy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">pregnancy</a> to proceed so the fetus can gain in size and  maturity before birth.
