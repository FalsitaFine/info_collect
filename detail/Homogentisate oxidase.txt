
Homogentisate oxidase: The enzyme encoded in the HGD gene. Mutation of HGD causes the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/genetic_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">genetic disease</a> <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/alkaptonuria/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">alkaptonuria</a>. Homogentisate oxidase is also called Homogentisate 1,2-dioxygenase .
