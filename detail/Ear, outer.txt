
Ear, outer: The part of the ear that is visible along the side of the head. The outer ear consists of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4910" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pinna</a>, or auricle (the visible projecting portion of the ear), the external acoustic meatus (the outside opening to the ear canal), and the external ear canal, which leads to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3177" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">eardrum</a>. The outer ear concentrates air vibrations on the eardrum to make the eardrum vibrate.
