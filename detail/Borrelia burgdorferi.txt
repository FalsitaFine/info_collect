
Borrelia burgdorferi: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5530">spirochete</a> (a type of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13954">bacteria</a>) that causes <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/lyme_disease/article.htm">Lyme disease</a>. Named after its discoverer, Dr. Willy Burgdorfer.
