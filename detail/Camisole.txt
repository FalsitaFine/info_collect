
Camisole: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40263" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">straitjacket</a>. The term camisole was borrowed from the French and refers to a woman's garment, usually sleeveless. The term came from the Latin camisia for linen shirt or nightgown.
