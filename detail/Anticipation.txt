
Anticipation: The progressively earlier appearance and increased severity of a disease from generation to generation. The phenomenon of anticipation was once thought to be an artifact, but a biological basis for it has been discovered in a number of genetic disorders, such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4494" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">myotonic dystrophy</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3820" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Huntington disease</a>.
