
Nephrolithiasis: The process of forming a kidney stone, a stone in the kidney (or lower down in the urinary tract). <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/kidney_stones/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Kidney stones</a> are a common cause of blood in the urine and pain in the abdomen, flank, or groin. Kidney stones occur in 1 in 10 people at some time in their life.
