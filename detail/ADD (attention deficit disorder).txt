
ADD (attention deficit disorder): <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/adhd_in_children/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">ADHD</a> is a common condition that affects children and adolescents, while ADD is more common in adults.    ADHD is the most commonly diagnosed mental disorder of children and is more common in boys than in girls. Children with ADHD generally have  greater problems paying attention or concentrating. They can't seem to follow directions and are easily bored or frustrated with tasks. They also tend to move constantly and are impulsive, not stopping to think before they act.
