
Cardiac index: A cardiodynamic measure based on the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7524" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cardiac output</a>, which is the amount of blood the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9127" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">left ventricle</a> ejects into the systemic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2735" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">circulation</a> in one minute, measured in liters per minute (l/min). Cardiac output is indexed to a patient's body size by dividing by the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39851" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">body surface area</a> to yield the cardiac index.
