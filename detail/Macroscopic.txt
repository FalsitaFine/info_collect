
Macroscopic: Large enough to be seen with the naked eye,
as opposed to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4380">microscopic</a>. For example, a macroscopic tumor is big enough to be seen without the aid of a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4375">microscope</a>. From the Greek 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4232">macro-</a> (large, long) + -scopic, from skopos (aim, mark).
