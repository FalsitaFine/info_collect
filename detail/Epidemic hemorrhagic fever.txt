
Epidemic hemorrhagic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a>: A syndrome caused by a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hantavirus_pulmonary_syndrome/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hantavirus</a> which is transmitted to humans by contact with infected rodents urine or feces. Patients typically develop a high fever, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/chronic_cough/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cough</a>, shortness of breath and abdominal or back pain and may go on to develop <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/kidney_failure/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">kidney failure</a>, which is usually temporary.
