
Diaphragm pacing: A procedure to help patients with spinal cord injuries to breathe. Their breathing is helped by setting the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5330" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">respiratory rate</a> by electrical stimulation (pacing) of the phrenic nerve using electrodes surgically implanted into the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9758" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">diaphragm</a> muscle that is innervated by the nerve.
