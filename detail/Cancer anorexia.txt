
Cancer anorexia: Loss of desire to eat due to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a>.  Cancer <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anorexia_nervosa/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anorexia</a> contributes to malnutrition, increases morbidity (illness) and mortality (death), and impinges on the quality of life.
