
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3980" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Influenza</a>: Influenza is an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2133" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">acute</a> <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5329" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">respiratory</a> illness caused by influenza A or B <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6000" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">viruses</a> Most people who get the influenza recover completely <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">in</a> 1 to 2 weeks, but some people develop serious and potentially life-threatening medical complications, such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4962" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pneumonia</a>. Much of the illness and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33438" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">death</a> caused by influenza can be prevented by annual influenza <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5925" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">vaccination</a>.
