
Bony tarsus: A structure that is made up of seven bones situated between the bones of the lower leg and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25074" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">metatarsus</a> bones of the feet. The seven bones of the bony tarsus are the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9013" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">calcaneus</a>, talus (astragalus), cuboid, and navicular (scaphoid), plus the first, second, and third cuneiform bones. The bony tarsus contributes to the broad, flat framework of the foot.
