
GBV-C: GB virus C, a virus first identified in 1995 that is genetically related to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hepatitis_c/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hepatitis C</a> virus but which does not cause hepatitis in humans. The exact role of GBV C in producing disease in humans remains unclear.
