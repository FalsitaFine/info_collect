
Glycopeptide: A short chain of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39555" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">amino acids</a> that has sugar molecules attached to it. A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16842" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">glycoprotein</a> is similar in structure to a glycopeptide but has a longer chain of amino acids.
