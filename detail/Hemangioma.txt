
Hemangioma: A benign <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a> formed by a collection of excess blood vessels. A hemangioma may be visible through the skin as a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/birthmarks_and_other_skin_pigmentation_problems/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">birthmark</a>, known colloquially as a 'strawberry mark.' Most hemangiomas that occur at birth disappear after a few months or years.
