
Bedblocker: See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33991">Bed blocker</a>.
Bed blocker: A patient who uses a hospital bed for a long period of time because they are chronically ill. The patient inadvertently blocks the use of the bed by other patients. The term "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33990" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">bed blocker</a>" appears to date back into the 1980s.
