
Azoospermic: containing no sperm. This adjective is typically used to describe the semen of men when no sperm (spermatozoa) are present. Azoospermia is the condition of having no sperm and affects about 1% of men. Azoospermia is a cause of male <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/infertility/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">infertility</a>. About 10%-15% of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/infertility_treatments_pictures_slideshow/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="slide">infertile</a> men are found to have azoospermia.
