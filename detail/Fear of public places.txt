
Fear of public places: This irrational fear is called   <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/agoraphobia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">agoraphobia</a>. It applies to open areas, especially those from which   escape could be difficult or help not immediately accessible. Persons   with agoraphobia frequently also have panic disorder.
