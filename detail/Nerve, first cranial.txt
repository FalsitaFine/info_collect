
Nerve, first cranial: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7566" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">first cranial nerve</a> is the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7571" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">olfactory nerve</a>, the nerve that carries impulses for the sense of smell from the nose to the brain.
