
Hypocapnia: Less than the normal level of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20132" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">carbon dioxide</a> in the blood. Hypoapnia is the opposite of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38432" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hypercapnia</a>.  The origin of the suffix "-capnia" is curious. It comes from the Greek "kapnos" referring to carbon dioxide which is a principal part of smoke.
