
Actinic: Referring to the ultraviolet (UV) rays from sunlight and UV lamps. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sunburn_and_sun_poisoning/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Sunburn</a> is an actinic burn. An <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/actinic_keratosis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">actinic keratosis</a> is a skin lesion that is the consequence of chronic sun exposure.
