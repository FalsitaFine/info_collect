
Minor anomaly: An unusual anatomic feature that is of no serious medical or cosmetic consequence to the patient. A minor anomaly of the feet might, for example, be curvature of the second toe so it overlaps the third toe a little. A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12760" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">major anomaly</a>, by contrast, might be a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6537" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cleft lip</a> and palate, a birth defect of serious medical and cosmetic consequence to the child.
