
Disorders, lymphoproliferative: Malignant diseases of the lymphoid cells
and cells from  the reticuloendothelial system (which take up and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=31422" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">sequester</a> inert particles). The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7859" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">lymphoproliferative disorders</a> are in contrast to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/polymyalgia_rheumatica/article.htm5" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">myeloproliferative disorders</a> which are malignancies of certain bone marrow
 cells including those that give rise to the red blood cells, the granulocytes (types of white blood cells), and the platelets (crucial to blood clotting).
