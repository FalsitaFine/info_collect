
Bone cyst, aneurysmal: A benign lesion in a bone that contains connective tissue and blood inside a thin bony shell. Aneurysmal bone <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cyst/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cysts</a> act like tumors and expand the bone, and they typically occur in the second decade of life. They can affect any bone in the arms, legs, trunk, or skull.
