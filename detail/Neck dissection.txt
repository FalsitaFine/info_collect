
Neck dissection: A surgical procedure in which the neck is opened -- to "dissect" means to "cut apart" -- in order to identify, examine, and sometimes remove structures such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4213">lymph nodes</a> or other <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5800">tissue</a> for diagnosis and/or treatment. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23043">Radical neck dissection</a>.
