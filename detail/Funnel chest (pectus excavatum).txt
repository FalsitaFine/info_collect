
Funnel chest (pectus excavatum): "Caved-in" chest.
Usually an unimportant isolated finding evident at birth. (Funnel chest can occasionally
be part of a connective tissue disorder such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/marfan_syndrome/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Marfan syndrome</a>).
