
Intestinal pseudo-obstruction: A condition in which the patient has symptoms of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4003" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">intestinal obstruction</a> with no sign of actual physical obstruction. This condition may be due to problems with the nerves that control intestinal muscles or to other causes. Treatment depends on the cause.
