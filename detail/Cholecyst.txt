
Cholecyst: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3536" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">gallbladder</a>. The word cholecyst is not much used today but it figures into a number of other terms to do with the gallbladder <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39741" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Cholecystectomy</a> is removal of the gallbladder.
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2709" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict"> Cholecystitis</a> is inflammation of the gallbladder. Cholecystogram is an x-ray of the gallbladder. Cholecyst, literally, means, bilebladder. It comes from chol, referring to bile + cyst, a bladder.
