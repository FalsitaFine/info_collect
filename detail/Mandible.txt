
Mandible: The bone of the lower jaw. The joint where the mandible meets the upper jaw at the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10463" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">temporal bone</a> is called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5730" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">temporomandibular joint</a>.
