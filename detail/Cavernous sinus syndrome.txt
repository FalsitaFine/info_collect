
Cavernous sinus syndrome: A condition characterized by swelling of the eyelids and the conjunctivae of the eyes, as well as paralysis of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7576" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cranial nerves</a> that course through the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9153" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cavernous sinus</a>. It is caused by a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9156" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cavernous sinus thrombosis</a>.
