
African tick typhus: One of the tick-borne rickettsial
diseases of the eastern hemisphere, similar to Rocky Mountain spotted <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm">fever</a>, but less
severe, with fever, a small ulcer (tache noire) at the site of the tick bite, swollen
glands nearby (satellite lymphadenopathy), and a red raised (maculopapular) <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm">rash</a>. Also
called fievre boutonneuse.
