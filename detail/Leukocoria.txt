
Leukocoria: A white pupillary reflex. When one shines a bright light on the pupil, it normally appears red. In leukocoria, the light makes the pupil look white. This occurs with a number of eye diseases including congenital cataract and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/retinoblastoma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">retinoblastoma</a> (a malignancy of the retina).
