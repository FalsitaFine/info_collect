
Bone mass density (BMD): A measure of bone density. Loss of bone mass is due to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/osteopenia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">osteopenia</a> or, if more severe, to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/osteoporosis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">osteoporosis</a>.
