
CT colonography: Another name for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/virtual_colonoscopy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">virtual colonoscopy</a>. The term CT colonography provides a more accurate description of the technique that employs computerized tomography (CT) but not a colonoscope. This scan may also be useful in detecting cancers outside the colon, such as renal, lung, and lymph node cancers.
