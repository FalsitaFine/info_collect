
Amygdaloid: Like an almond. Almond-shaped. As the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39203" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">amygdaloid nucleus</a> in the brain, a structure that anatomists thought resembled an almond. Amygdaloid is pronounced in three syllables: amyg da loid. From the Greek amydale (almond) + the Greek eidos (like).
