
Leucovorin: Folinic acid, the active form of folic acid in the body. Used as an antidote to protect normal cells from high doses of the anticancer drug <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/methotrexate/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">methotrexate</a> and to increase the antitumor effects of fluorouracil (5-FU) and tegafur-uracil. Also used to prevent or treat <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anemia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anemia</a> due to folic acid deficiency. Also known as citrovorum factor and Wellcovorin.
