
Capsid: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6554">protein</a> coat of a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5997">virus</a>. The term capsid came in 1960 from the French capside, from the Latin capsa, case. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24449">Nucleocapsid</a>.
