
AZT: Azidothymidine, now renamed <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/zidovudine/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">zidovudine</a>, but still best known by the abbreviation AZT. This antiviral drug is prescribed, usually in combination with protease inhibitors and other drugs, to treat HIV infection in patients with AIDS.
