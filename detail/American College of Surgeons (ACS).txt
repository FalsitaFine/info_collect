
American College of Surgeons (ACS): The American College of Surgeons (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9340" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ACS</a>) was formed in 1913 under the aegis of Dr. John Miller Turpin Finney "to elevate the standard of surgery, to establish a standard of competency and of character for practitioners of surgery, and to educate the public and the profession to understand that the practice of surgery calls for special training."
