
Induced menopause (and "menopause, induced"):
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/menopause/article.htm">Menopause</a> induced 
by an unusual event, such as occurs when the ovaries are damaged by radiation, 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/chemotherapy/article.htm"> chemotherapy</a> or other medications; or as occurs when the ovaries are surgically 
removed (by bilateral oophorectomy).
