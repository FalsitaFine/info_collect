
Exanthem:  A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm">rash</a>. The word 
"exanthem" comes from the Greek "exanthema" which means "a 
breaking out." "Anthos" in Greek is "a flower," 
particularly a flower blossom, so a child breaking out with 
an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm">rash</a> is likened to a flower bursting into bloom.
