
Assisted living: A type of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24859" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">long-term care facility</a> for elderly or disabled people who are able to get around on their own but who may need help with some <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2131" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">activities of daily living</a> or simply prefer the convenience of having their meals in a central cafeteria and having nursing staff on call.
