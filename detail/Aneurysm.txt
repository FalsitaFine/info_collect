
Aneurysm: A localized widening (dilatation) of an artery, a vein, or the heart. At the point of an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/brain_aneurysm/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">aneurysm</a>, there is typically a bulge. The wall of the blood vessel or organ is weakened and may rupture.
