
Amebic colitis: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6590" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Amebic dysentery</a> with ulcers in the colon from infection with the ameba <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6526" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Entamoeba histolytica</a>. This single-celled parasite is transmitted to humans via contaminated water and food.
