
Halitosis: An unpleasant odor from the mouth, commonly referred to as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/bad_breath/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">bad breath</a>. Halitosis can be caused by the consumption of certain foods, poor oral hygiene, alcohol or tobacco use, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/dry_mouth/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">dry mouth</a>, or by certain chronic medical conditions.
