
Humor: In medicine, a fluid or semifluid substance. For example, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10588" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">aqueous humor</a> is the fluid normally present in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10586" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">anterior chamber</a> of the eye, between the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7248" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cornea</a> and the iris.
