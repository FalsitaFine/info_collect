
Cancer, melanoma: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/skin_cancer_overview/article.htm">skin cancer</a> that begins in cells
called melanocytes that can grow together to form benign (not cancerous) <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/moles/article.htm">moles</a>. A change in size, shape, or color of a mole can be a sign of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/melanoma/article.htm">melanoma</a>. It can be cured if detected
early, before spread (metastasis) to other areas. Diagnosis is confirmed by a biopsy of the abnormal skin. Sun exposure can cause skin damage that can lead to melanoma.
