
Hydrophobia: 1. Literally, an irrational fear of water, to drink or to swim in. Someone who is scared of the water is hydrophobic. 2. A term once commonly used for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rabies_virus/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">rabies</a> because in the later stages of that disease, the animal (or person) has difficulty swallowing and so fears a drink of water.
