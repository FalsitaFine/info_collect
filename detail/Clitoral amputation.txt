
Clitoral amputation: A surgical procedure in which all or part of the clitoris and sometimes also the labia are removed. Clitoral amputation is a form of female circumcision (female genital mutilation).  Also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33609" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">clitorectomy</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33610" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">clitoridectomy</a>.
