
Heterotaxy syndrome: This rare birth defect is characterized by heart defects and abnormal locations of internal organs. The condition is also known as isomerism. Typically, the organs develop on the opposite side of the body from which they are normally located. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/babies_quiz/quiz.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="quiz">Babies</a> who have this syndrome are usually identified because of defects in the heart or other organs.
