
Bone, blade: Familiar term for the scapula, also called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8207" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">shoulder blade</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10308" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">wing bone</a>, the flat triangular bone at the back of the shoulder.
