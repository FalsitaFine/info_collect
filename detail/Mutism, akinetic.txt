
Mutism, akinetic: A state in which a person is unable to speak (mute) or move (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6989" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">akinetic</a>). <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6990" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Akinetic mutism</a> is often due to damage to the frontal lobes of the brain.
