
Adam's apple: The familiar feature on the front of the neck that is the forward protrusion of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15568" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroid cartilage</a>, the largest cartilage of the larynx. It tends to enlarge at adolescence, particularly in males. It is usually said to take its name from the extrabiblical story that a piece of the forbidden fruit stuck in Adam's throat.
