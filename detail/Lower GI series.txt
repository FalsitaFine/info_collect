
Lower GI series: Short for lower <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3555">gastrointestinal</a> series. A series of x-rays of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5253">rectum</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2787">colon</a> and lower section of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5512">small intestine</a> taken after the patient has a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2425">barium enema</a>. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25935">Barium</a> is a white, chalky substance that coats the organs so they will show up on the x-ray. Also called a barium enema or a barium enema x-ray.
