
Granulomatosis with Polyangiitis: An uncommon type of inflammation of small arteries and veins (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/vasculitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">vasculitis</a>) that classically involves the vessels supplying the tissues of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/image-collection/lungs_picture/picture.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="img">lungs</a>, nasal passages (sinuses), and kidneys.
