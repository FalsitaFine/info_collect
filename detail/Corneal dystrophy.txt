
Corneal dystrophy: A condition in which one or more parts of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7248" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cornea</a> lose their normal clarity due to a buildup of cloudy material. There are over 20 corneal dystrophies that affect all parts of the cornea.
