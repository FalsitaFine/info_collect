
Globe, pale: Also called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9026" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">globus pallidus</a>, this is a pale-appearing spherical area in the brain. Globus is a Latin word meaning a globe or sphere. Pallidus refers to its pallor relative to the surrounding brain substance.
