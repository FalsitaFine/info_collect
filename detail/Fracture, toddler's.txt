
Fracture, toddler's: A torsion <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/broken_bone_types_of_bone_fractures/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fracture</a> of the tibia that occurs without bone displacement. This fracture is called toddler's fracture because it occurs in infants who are early on in their <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/walking/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">walking</a>, causing a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/stress/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">stress</a> breakage of bone in the large bone of the leg below the knee.
