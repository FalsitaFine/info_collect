
Certified nurse-midwife: A person with an AS, BS, or Master's degree in nursing who has also completed specialized training in midwifery. In the US, certified nurse-midwives (CNMs) must earn certification from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40418" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">American College of Nurse-Midwives</a>.
