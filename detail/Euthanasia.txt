
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7365" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Euthanasia</a>: The practice of intentionally ending a life in order to relieve <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pain_management/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">pain</a> and suffering. The word "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/grief_bereavement_mourning_quiz/quiz.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="quiz">euthanasia</a>" comes straight out of the Greek -- "eu", goodly or well + "thanatos", death = the good death.
