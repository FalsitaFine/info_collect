
Athymic mouse: A laboratory mouse lacking a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=31561">thymus gland</a>. Athymic mice have no <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11300">T cells</a> and useful in research because they do not reject tumor or other cells transplanted from mice, humans or other species. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33771">Nude mouse</a>.
