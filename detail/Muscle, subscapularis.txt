
Muscle, subscapularis: A muscle that moves the arm by turning it inward (internal rotation). The tendon of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8196" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">subscapularis muscle</a> is one of four tendons that stabilize the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9059" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">shoulder joint</a> and constitute the rotator cuff.
