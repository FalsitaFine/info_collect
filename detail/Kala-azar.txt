
Kala-azar: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2728">chronic</a> and potentially fatal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11869">parasitic</a> disease of the viscera (the internal organs, particularly the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4179">liver</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5531">spleen</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4286">bone marrow</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4213">lymph nodes</a>) due to infection by the parasite called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4128">Leishmania</a> donovani.
