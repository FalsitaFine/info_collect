
Collateral knee ligament, medial: A ligament on the inner side of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8845" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">knee joint</a>. The medial collateral knee ligament adds stability and strength to the knee joint. Abbreviated <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=14854" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">MCL</a>.
