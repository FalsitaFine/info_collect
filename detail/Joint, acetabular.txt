
Joint, acetabular: The hip joint. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2110" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">acetabulum</a> is the cup-shaped socket of the hip joint, and it is a key feature of the pelvic anatomy. The upper end of the femur (thighbone) fits right into the acetabulum, articulates with it, and thereby forms the largest <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8748" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ball-and-socket joint</a> in the human body.
