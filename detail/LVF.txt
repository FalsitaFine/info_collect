
LVF: 1. Left ventricular failure, failure of the left side of the heart.<br>
2. Left ventricular function, the function of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9127" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">left ventricle</a>.<br><br>
In both of these senses, LVF is in contrast to RVF (right ventricular failure or function).</br></br></br>
