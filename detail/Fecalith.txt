
Fecalith: A hard stony mass of feces in the intestinal tract. A fecalith can obstruct the appendix, leading to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/appendicitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">appendicitis</a>. Fecaliths can also obstruct diverticuli. Also known as coprolith and stercolith.
