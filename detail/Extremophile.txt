
Extremophile: An organism that lives under extreme conditions. An example of an extremophile is <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25598" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Methanococcus jannaschii</a>, a microbe that lives near hydrothermal vents deep beneath the sea. The term extremophile was coined late in the 20th century from extreme + -phile, liking = liking extreme (conditions).
