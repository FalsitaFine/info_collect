
Macule: A circumscribed change in the color of skin that is neither raised nor depressed. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=14073" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Macules</a> are completely flat and can only be appreciated by visual inspection and not by touch. Physicians refer to flat skin spots on the skin as macules, as opposed to papules.
