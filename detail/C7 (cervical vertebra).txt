
C7 (cervical vertebra): C7 is the symbol for the 7th cervical (neck) vertebral bone (C7) which is sometimes called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8783" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">prominent vertebra</a> due to the length of its <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39727" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">spinous process</a> (the projection off the back of the vertebral body).
