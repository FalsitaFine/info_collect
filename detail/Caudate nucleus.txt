
Caudate nucleus: In each hemisphere of the brain, the most medial of the four <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10030" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">basal ganglia</a>, partly responsible for body movement and coordination. So named because it looks anatomically tail-like (the Latin cauda, tail).
