
Antiinfective: Something capable of acting against infection, by inhibiting the spread of an infectious agent or by killing the infectious agent outright.
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2285" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Anti-infective</a> is a general term that encompasses antibacterials, antibiotics, antifungals, antiprotozoans and antivirals.
