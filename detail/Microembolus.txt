
Microembolus: An <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3224" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">embolus</a> of microscopic size. For example, a tiny <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22122" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">blood clot</a> or little clump of bacteria. Like any embolus, it travels through the bloodstream, lodges in a blood vessel and blocks it.
