
Deviated septum: Failure of the nasal septum to be in the center of the nose and divide the nasal passages evenly. Deviation of the nasal septum may be congenital (present at birth) or acquired (occur later). The major problem it causes is airway obstruction. A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/deviated_septum/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">deviated septum</a> can be corrected with surgery.
