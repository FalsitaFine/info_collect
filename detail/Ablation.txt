
Ablation: Removal or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38490" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">excision</a>. Ablation is usually carried out surgically. For example, surgical removal of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5778" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroid gland</a> (a total <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5783" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroidectomy</a>) is ablation of the thyroid.
