
Facial canal introitus: The entrance to the facial canal, a passage in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10463" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">temporal bone</a> of the skull through which the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7518" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">facial nerve</a> (the seventh cranial nerve) travels. In anatomy, an introitus is an entrance that goes into a canal or hollow organ.
