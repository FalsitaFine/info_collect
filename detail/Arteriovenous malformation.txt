
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/arteriovenous_malformation/article.htm">Arteriovenous malformation</a> 
(AVM): An arteriovenous malformation (AVM) is a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15599">congenital</a> disorder 
(one present at birth) of blood vessels in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2516">brain</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23623">brainstem</a>, or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=17889">spinal cord</a> that is 
characterized by a complex, tangled web of abnormal arteries and veins connected by one or more fistulas (abnormal communications).
