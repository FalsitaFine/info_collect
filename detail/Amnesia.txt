
Amnesia: An impairment to or lack of memory. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10940" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Antegrade amnesia</a> refers to a lack of memory of events occurring after a traumatic event, whereas <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11959" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">retrograde amnesia</a> refers to lack of memory of events that occurred before the event.
