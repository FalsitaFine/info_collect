
Appendix epididymis: A small cystic projection from the surface of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3280" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">epididymis</a> (a structure within the scrotum that is attached to the backside of the testis), which represents a remnant of the embryologic mesonephros.
