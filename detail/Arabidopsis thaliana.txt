
Arabidopsis thaliana: A plant belonging to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13893" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">mustard</a> family.  Arabidopsis was the first plant genome to be sequenced, and is a popular tool for understanding the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25720" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">molecular biology</a> of many plant traits, including flower development and light sensing.
