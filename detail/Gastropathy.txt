
Gastropathy: Disease of the stomach, as in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15455" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hypoproteinemic hypertrophic gastropathy</a>. Gastropathy comes from gastro- referring to the stomach + the Greek pathos meaning disease. Pronounced gas-trop-a-the with the emphasis on the trop.
