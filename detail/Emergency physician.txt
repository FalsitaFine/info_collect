
Emergency physician: A physician focused on the immediate decision making and action necessary to prevent death or any further disability both in the prehospital setting by directing emergency medical technicians and in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12156" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">emergency department</a>. The emergency physician provides immediate recognition, evaluation, care, stabilization, and disposition of a generally diversified population of adult and pediatric patients in response to acute illness and injury.
