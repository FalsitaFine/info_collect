
Asbestos: A natural material made up of tiny fibers that is used as thermal insulation. Inhalation of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/asbestos-related_disorders/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">asbestos</a> fibers can lead to asbestosis and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/mesothelioma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">mesothelioma</a>.
