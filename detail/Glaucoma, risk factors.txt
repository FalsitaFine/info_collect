
Glaucoma, risk factors: if you belong to a high-risk
group for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/glaucoma/article.htm">glaucoma</a>, have your eyes examined through dilated pupils every 2 years by an eye
care professional. High-risk groups include everyone with a family history of glaucoma,
everyone over the age of 60 and any Black over the age of 40. (Among Blacks, studies show
that glaucoma is: 5 times more likely to occur in Blacks than in Whites and about 4 times
more likely to cause blindness in Blacks than in Whites).
