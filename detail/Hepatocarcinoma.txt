
Hepatocarcinoma: Carcinoma (a form of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2580">cancer</a>) that originates in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4179">liver</a> cells. Hepatocarcinoma is better known today as hepatocellular carcinoma. See:  <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18505">Hepatocellular carcinoma</a>.
