
Histocompatible: Literally, tissue compatible, meaning that the tissue can exist together with tissue of another organism without the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3907" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immune system</a> rejecting it. If a tissue donor and tissue recipient are histocompatible, a transplant is expected to be easily accepted.
