
Cesarean section, vaginal birth after: It was once the rule that after a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/c-section_cesarean_birth/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">C-section</a>, the next delivery also had to be by C-section. Now vaginal delivery after Cesarean section (VBAC) is frequently feasible.
