
Dermatan sulfate: An glycosaminoglycan (formerly called a mucopolysaccharide) found mostly in skin but also in blood vessels, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6417" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">heart valves</a>, tendons, and the lungs. Dermatan sulfate accumulates abnormally in several of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11679" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">mucopolysaccharidosis</a> disorders.
