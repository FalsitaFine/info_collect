
Cross-sectional study: A research study done at one time, not over the course of time. A cross-sectional study might be a study of a disease such as AIDS at one point in time, to learn its prevalence and distribution within the population. Also known as a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5611" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">synchronic study</a>.
