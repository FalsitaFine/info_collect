
Lunule: 1. The crescent-shaped area at the base of a fingernail or toenail. 2. Any small crescent- or moon-shaped area or structure.<br>
Borrowed from the French lunule, from the Latin <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/angina_symptoms/article.htm14" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">lunula</a>, the diminuitive of luna, the moon. Also called the lunula.</br>
