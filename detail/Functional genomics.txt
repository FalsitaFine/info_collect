
Functional genomics: The function of each gene in the genome. Functional genomics is the study of genes, their resulting proteins, and the role played by the proteins the body's biochemical processes. Functional <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23242" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">genomics</a> also involves understanding the genetic control mechanisms.
