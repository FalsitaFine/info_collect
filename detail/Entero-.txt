
Entero-: Prefix referring to the intestine, as in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3260" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">enteropathy</a> (a disease of the intestine) and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11326" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">enterospasm</a> (a painful, intense contraction of the intestine).
