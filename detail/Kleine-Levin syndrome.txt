
Kleine-Levin syndrome: A rare condition that is characterized by periods of excessive drowsiness and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sleep/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">sleep</a>, lasting up to 20 hours per day. Affected individuals usually have excessive food intake and may be confused, irritable, and lethargic. Some patients have hallucinations or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sexual_sex_problems_in_men/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">sexual</a> disinhibition. Most patients with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/kleine-levin_syndrome/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Kleine-Levin syndrome</a> are adolescent males. The cause is unknown. Most cases resolve over time without treatment. Also known as Kleine-Levin hibernation syndrome.
