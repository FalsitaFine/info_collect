
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11357">Fibroma</a>, cemento-ossifying: A hard fibrous <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4135">lesion</a> that 
continues to grow, sometimes to very large size, unless 
treated, most 
frequently seen in the jaw or mouth, sometimes in 
connection with a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3513">fracture</a> or another type of injury. Treatment is by 
surgery. Abbreviated COF.
