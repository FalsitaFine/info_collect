
Fiber: The parts of fruits and vegetables that cannot be digested. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/fiber/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">Fiber</a> is of vital importance to digestion; it helps the body move food through the digestive tract, reduces serum <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cholesterol_management/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cholesterol</a>, and contributes to disease protection.
