
Blood glucose: The main sugar that the body makes from the food in the diet. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/glucose/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">Glucose</a> is carried through the bloodstream to provide energy to all cells in the body. Cells cannot use glucose without the help of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/insulin/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">insulin</a>.
