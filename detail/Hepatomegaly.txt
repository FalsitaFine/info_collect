
Hepatomegaly: An abnormally enlarged liver. Hepatomegaly can be caused by <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/heart_failure/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">heart failure</a>, blockage of blood vessels from the liver, or be a sign of chronic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/liver_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">liver disease</a>.
