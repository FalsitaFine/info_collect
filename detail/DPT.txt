
DPT: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diphtheria_facts/article.htm">Diphtheria</a>-Pertussis-Tetanus vaccine. Today the more
frequent abbreviation is <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3126">DTP</a> (for Diphtheria-Tetanus-Pertussis vaccine).
