
North Asian tick-borne rickettsiosis: One of the tick-borne rickettsial diseases of the eastern hemisphere, similar to but less severe than <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rocky_mountain_spotted_fever_rmsf/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Rocky Mountain spotted fever</a>. Symptoms include <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a>, a small ulcer (eschar) at the site of the tick bite, swollen glands near the site of the bite (satellite lymphadenopathy), and a red, raised (maculopapular) <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">rash</a>.
