
Chronic traumatic encephalopathy (CTE): a degenerative disease of the brain (form of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/brain_damage_symptoms_causes_treatment/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">brain damage</a>) that occurs in athletes or others who have experienced repetitive <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/concussions_brain_injuries_pictures_slideshow/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="slide">concussions</a> or other <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/trauma_and_first_aid_quiz/quiz.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="quiz">trauma</a> to the brain. Some of the most common symptoms and signs of CTE can include <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/dementia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">memory loss</a>, behavioral difficulties like difficulty with impulse control, impaired judgment, aggression, balance problems, a slow onset of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/dementia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">dementia</a>, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/depression/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">depression</a>. Symptoms of CTE might be mistaken for symptoms due to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/senior_health/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">aging</a> process or other conditions like <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/alzheimers_disease_causes_stages_and_symptoms/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Alzheimer's disease</a>.
