
Iodide: The chemical form to which <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4038">iodine</a> in the diet is reduced before it is absorbed through the intestinal wall into the bloodstream and carried through the blood to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5778">thyroid gland</a>.
