
Human herpesvirus 7: A herpes virus that causes seizures and other central nervous system symptoms in children. Abbreviated HHV-7. Closely related to HHV-6, HHV-7 has also been linked to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/roseola/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">roseola</a>. Diagnosis is made via rapid blood culture or other blood test. Treatment is experimental at this time, but antiviral drugs or beta <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/interferon/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">interferon</a> may be tried.
