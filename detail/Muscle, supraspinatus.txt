
Muscle, supraspinatus: A muscle that is responsible for elevating the arm and moving it away from the body. The tendon of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8199" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">supraspinatus muscle</a> is one of four tendons that stabilize the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9059" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">shoulder joint</a> and constitute the rotator cuff.
