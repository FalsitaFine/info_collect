
JAK3: Or Jak3. An enzyme found only in cells in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3907" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immune system</a> that is critical for the cell signaling process resulting in the development of white blood cells. Mutation of the gene encoding JAK3 is responsible for a form of severe combined <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3935" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immunodeficiency</a> (SCID). JAK stands for Janus kinase or Just Another Kinase.
