
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=19449" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Darier disease</a>: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3573" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">genetic</a> <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7901" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">skin</a> <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3011" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">disease</a> characterized by an abnormality of the horny layer of the skin around the hair follicles. This disorder is inherited <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">in</a> an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11974" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">autosomal dominant</a> manner and is due to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4471" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">mutation</a> in a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3560" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">gene</a> called ATP2A2 on <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=14018" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chromosome</a> 12. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11537" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">keratosis follicularis</a>.
