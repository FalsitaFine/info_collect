
Acetaminophen: A nonaspirin pain reliever or analgesic. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/acetaminophen/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">Acetaminophen</a> may be given alone to relieve pain and inflammation or it may be combined with other drugs, as in some <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/migraine/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">migraine</a> medications, which contain acetaminophen, a barbiturate, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/caffeine/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">caffeine</a>.
