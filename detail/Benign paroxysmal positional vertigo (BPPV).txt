
Benign paroxysmal positional vertigo (BPPV): A balance disorder that results in the sudden onset of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/dizziness_dizzy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">dizziness</a>, spinning, or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/vertigo_overview/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">vertigo</a> when moving the head.
