
CpG island: In molecular <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15390" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">genetics</a>, a short region of DNA rich in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26443" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">CpG</a> sites, often located near the promoters of genes. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/human_immunodeficiency_virus_hiv/article.htm87" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Methylation</a> of the CpG sites influences the activity of nearby genes and is critical to the regulation of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3564" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">gene expression</a>.
