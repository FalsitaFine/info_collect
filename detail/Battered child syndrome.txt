
Battered child syndrome: A disease in which children are physically abused. The battered child syndrome is a form of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/child_abuse_facts/article.htm">child abuse</a>.
Not until the 19th century were children granted the same legal status as domesticated animals in regard to protection against cruelty and/or neglect.  In 1962 the term "battered child syndrome" entered medicine. By 1976 all states in the United States had adopted laws mandating the reporting of suspected instances of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/child_abuse_facts/article.htm">child abuse</a>.
