
Hysterectomy: An operation to remove the uterus. Hysterectomies are most commonly performed as a treatment for fibroids, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a> or severe dysplasia of the uterus, and for dysfunctional uterine bleeding.
