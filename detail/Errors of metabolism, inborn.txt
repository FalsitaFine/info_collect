
Errors of metabolism, inborn: Heritable (genetic) disorders of biochemistry. Examples of inborn errors of metabolism include albinism, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cystinuria/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cystinuria</a> (a cause of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/kidney_stones/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">kidney stones</a>), <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/phenylketonuria/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">phenylketonuria</a> (PKU), and some forms of gout, sun sensitivity, and thyroid disease. These are only a very few of the hundreds of known inborn errors of metabolism.
