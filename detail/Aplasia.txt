
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2307" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Aplasia</a>: Failure to develop. This is not to be confused with the word, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2389" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">atrophy</a>, which means that something develops and then wastes away.
