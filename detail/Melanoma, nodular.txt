
Melanoma, nodular: One of the four clinical types of malignant <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/melanoma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">melanoma</a>, typically presents as a raised, distinct, bluish-black <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a>  possibly shaped like a pencil eraser, that may be encircled by particularly pale skin, most often in middle-aged or older adults.
