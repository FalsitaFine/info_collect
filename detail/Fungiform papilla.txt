
Fungiform papilla: The fungiform papillae are broad flat structures that house taste buds in the central portion of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8802" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">dorsum</a> (back) of the tongue.   These papillae were thought to resemble a fungus: a little mushroom.  Fungiform means mushroom-shaped. From fungi-, fungus + forma, shape.
