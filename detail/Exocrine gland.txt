
Exocrine gland: A gland that secretes a substance out through a duct. The exocrine glands include the salivary glands, sweat glands and glands within the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25976" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">gastrointestinal tract</a>. The exocrine glands are the "glands of external secretion."
