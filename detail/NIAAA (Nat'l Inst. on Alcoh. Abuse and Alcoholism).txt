
NIAAA (National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism): The National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/alcohol_abuse_and_alcoholism/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Alcoholism</a> is one of the National Institutes of Health (NIH) in the U.S. The mission of NIAAA is to "conduct research focused on improving the treatment and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/prevention/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">prevention</a> of alcoholism and alcohol-
related problems to reduce the enormous health, social, and economic consequences of this disease."
