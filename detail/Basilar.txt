
Basilar: Located at or near the base of a structure, especially the base of the skull.
For example, a basilar <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/broken_bone_types_of_bone_fractures/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fracture</a> is a break in the bone at the base of the skull and can be life-threatening.
