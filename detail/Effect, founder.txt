
Effect, founder: The positive effect on gene frequency when a population (colony) has only a small number of original settlers, one or more of whom had that gene. For example, the gene for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/huntington_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Huntington's disease</a> was introduced into the Lake Maracaibo region in Venezuela early in the nineteenth century. This is now the largest known aggregation of the Huntington's gene in the world.
