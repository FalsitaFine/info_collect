
Diverticulitis, bleeding from: Diverticular bleeding  typically occurs intermittently and rapidly over several days. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/colonoscopy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">Colonoscopy</a> is usually performed to  confirm the diagnosis and exclude bleeding from other causes. Thermal probes cannot be  employed to stop active diverticular bleeding. Therefore, surgical removal of the bleeding  diverticula is necessary for those with persistent or recurrent bleeding.
