
Laxative: Something that promotes emptying of the bowels. Laxatives are used to combat <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/constipation/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">constipation</a>. They are sometimes overused, producing <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diarrhea/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">diarrhea</a>. Laxatives include milk of magnesia and many others.
