
Fibroma, nonossifying: Fibromas (or fibroid tumors or fibroids) are benign tumors that are composed of fibrous or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2824" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">connective tissue</a>. They can grow in all organ. A growing lesion with a fibroma structure. Treatment is by surgery.
