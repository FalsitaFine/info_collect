
Macrognathia: An abnormally large jaw. Macrognathia can be associated with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4914" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pituitary gigantism</a>, tumors, and other disorders. Macrognathia can often be corrected with surgery. Also known as prognathic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4269" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">mandible</a>.
