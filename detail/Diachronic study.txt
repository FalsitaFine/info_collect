
Diachronic study: A study done over the course of time. For example, a
longitudinal study of children with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/down_syndrome_overview/article.htm">Down syndrome</a> (trisomy 21) might involve the study of
100 children with this condition from birth to 10 years of age. Also called a longitudinal
study. The opposite of a synchronic (cross-sectional) study.
