
Malformation: A structural defect in the body due to abnormal embryonic or fetal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2964">development</a>. There are many types of malformations. For example, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6537">cleft lip</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6538">cleft palate</a>. See also <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2820">Congenital malformation</a>.
