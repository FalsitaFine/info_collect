
Infant, small for gestational age: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6327" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Small for gestational age</a> (SGA) is a term used to describe a baby who is smaller than the amount a baby typically weighs at her stage of development. Small for gestational age babies usually have birth weights below the tenth percentile for babies of the same gestational age.
