
Deficiency, sphingomyelinase: Also called Niemann-<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pick_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Pick disease</a>, this is a disorder of the metabolism of a lipid (fat) called sphingomyelin that usually causes the progressive development of enlargement of the liver and spleen (hepatosplenomegaly), "swollen glands" (lymphadenopathy), <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anemia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anemia</a> and mental and physical deterioration.
