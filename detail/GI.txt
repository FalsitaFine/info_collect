
GI: 1. In medicine, commonly used abbreviation for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3555">gastrointestinal</a>, referring collectively to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5560">stomach</a> and the small and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6214">large intestine</a>.<br>
2. Outside of medicine, GI may stand for galvanized iron, general issue or government issue (as in GI Joe).
