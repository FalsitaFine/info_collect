
Autosomal: Pertaining to a chromosome that is not a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20692" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">sex chromosome</a>. People normally have 22 pairs of autosomes (44 autosomes) in each cell, together with 2 sex chromosomes, X and Y in a male and X and X in a female.
