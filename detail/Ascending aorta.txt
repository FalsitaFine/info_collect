
Ascending aorta: The first section of the aorta, which starts from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9127" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">left ventricle</a> of the heart and extends to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8637" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">aortic arch</a>. The right and left <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7250" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">coronary arteries</a> that supply blood to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9658" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">heart muscle</a> arise from the ascending aorta.
