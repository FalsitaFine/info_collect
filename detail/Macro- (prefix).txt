
Macro- (prefix): Prefix from the Greek "makros" meaning large or long. Examples of terms involving macro- include macrobiotic, macrocephaly, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4236" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">macrocytic</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4237" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">macroglossia</a>, macrophage, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4239" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">macroscopic</a>, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4240" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">macrosomia</a>. The opposite of macro- is micro-.
