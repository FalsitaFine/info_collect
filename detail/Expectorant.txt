
Expectorant: A medication that helps bring up mucus and other material from the lungs, bronchi, and trachea. An example of an expectorant is <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/guaifenesin/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">guaifenesin</a>, which promotes drainage of mucus from the lungs by thinning the mucus, and also lubricates the irritated respiratory tract.
