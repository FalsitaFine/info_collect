
Abatement: A diminution, decrease or easing. In medicine there may be abatement of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4723">pain</a> or any other symptom or sign.   In the environment there may abatement in the degree of pollution. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=31483">Abate</a>.
