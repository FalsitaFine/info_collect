
Disease, Conor and Bruch: African tick <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/typhus/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">typhus</a>, one of the tick-borne rickettsial diseases of the eastern hemisphere similar to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rocky_mountain_spotted_fever_rmsf/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Rocky Mountain spotted fever</a> but less severe. Characteristic features include <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a>, a small ulcer (tache noire) at the
site of the tick bite, swollen glands nearby (satellite lymphadenopathy), and a red raised
(maculopapular) <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">rash</a>. Also called boutonneuse and fievre boutonneuse.
