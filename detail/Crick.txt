
Crick: 1. A painful sudden spasmodic stiffness in the muscles of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16929">neck</a> or back. 
2. Francis Crick who, with James Watson, devised the Watson-Crick model of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3090">DNA</a> as a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3109">double helix</a>.
