
Moebius syndrome: This rare neurologic disorder results in facial <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/paralysis/symptoms.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sym">paralysis</a> and is associated with facial abnormalities, including small chin and mouth, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cleft_palate_and_cleft_lip/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cleft palate</a>, and missing <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/habits_that_wreck_your_teeth_pictures_slideshow/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="slide">teeth</a>.  The facial paralysis leads to an inability to form expressions with the facial muscles. Treatment is supportive and directed at relief of symptoms.
