
iatrics: Suffix meaning healing. From the Greek  "iatros" meaning healer or physician. For example, Pediatrics is the healing of children, Bariatric surgery is designed to heal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/obesity_weight_loss/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">obesity</a>, Psychiatric care is meant to heal the mind. See also: Iatric; Iatrogenic.
