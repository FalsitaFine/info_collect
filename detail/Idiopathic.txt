
Idiopathic: Of unknown cause. Any disease that is of uncertain or unknown origin may be termed idiopathic. For example, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8011">acute idiopathic polyneuritis</a>, 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2993">diffuse idiopathic skeletal hyperostosis</a>,  
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10874">idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis</a>, 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11192">idiopathic scoliosis</a>, etc. 
From the New Latin idiopathia (primary disease), from the Greek idiopatheia, from idio-, from idios (one's own, personal) + -patheia, -pathic (feeling, suffering).
