
Dysplasia, thanatophoric: A form of short-limbed (micromelic) dwarfism that usually causes death within the first few hours after birth. Thanatophoric dysplasia is due to a lethal mutation (change) in the same gene that produces <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/achondroplasia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">achondroplasia</a>, a familiar and far more common form of short-limbed dwarfism that is compatible with life.
