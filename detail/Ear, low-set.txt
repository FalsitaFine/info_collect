
Ear, low-set: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12759" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">minor anomaly</a> involving an ear situated  down below its normal location.  The ear is low-set when the helix (of the  ear) meets the cranium at a level below that of a horizontal plane through both inner  canthi (the inside corners of the eyes).  .
