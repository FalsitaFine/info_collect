
Lunula: 1. The crescent-shaped area at the base of a fingernail or toenail. 2. Any small crescent- or moon-shaped area or structure.<br>
 Lunula is the diminuitive of the Latin luna, the moon. Also called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26215" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">lunule</a>.</br>
