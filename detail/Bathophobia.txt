
Bathophobia: An abnormal and persistent fear of depths. Sufferers from bathophobia experience <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anxiety/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anxiety</a> even though they realize they are safe from falling into or being consumed by depths. The feared object may be a long, dark hallway, a well or a deep pool or lake.
"Bathophobia" is derived from the Greek "bathos" (depth) and "phobos" (fear).
