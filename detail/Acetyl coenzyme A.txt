
Acetyl coenzyme A: An important metabolic intermediate, derived from various pathways, such as glycolysis, fatty acid oxidation, and degradation of some <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39555" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">amino acids</a>. It also represents a key intermediate in lipid biosynthesis. Commonly referred to as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32064" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">acetyl CoA</a>.
