
Atresia, anal: Congenital absence of an opening at the bottom end of the intestinal tract. Also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3947" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">imperforate anus</a>. Occurs in about 1 in 5,000 births. It is corrected by surgery.
Atresia refers to the absence of a normal opening.
