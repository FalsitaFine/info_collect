
Isoleucine: An amino acid, one of the 20 building blocks of protein. A  dietary essential amino acid, isoleucine is needed for optimal growth in childhood. It is  one of the three branched-chain <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39555" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">amino acids</a>.  Symbol: Ile.
