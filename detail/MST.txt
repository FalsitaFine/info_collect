
MST: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/multiple_subpial_transection/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">multiple subpial transection</a> (MST) is a procedure is used to help control seizures that begin in areas of the  brain that cannot be safely removed. The surgeon makes a series of shallow cuts (transections) in the brain tissue. These cuts interrupt the movement of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/seizure/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">seizure</a> impulses but do not disturb normal brain activity, leaving the person's abilities intact.
