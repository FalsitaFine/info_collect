
Balanitis: Inflammation of the rounded head (the glans) of the penis. Inflammation of the foreskin is called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7694" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">posthitis</a>. In the uncircumcised male, balanitis and posthitis generally occur together as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7696" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">balanoposthitis</a>: inflammation of both the glans and foreskin.
