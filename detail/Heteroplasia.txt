
Heteroplasia: The replacement of normal tissue of the body by abnormal tissue or by malpositioned normal tissue, a tissue growing in the wrong place.
Progressive <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4673" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">osseous</a> heteroplasia is a disorder in which bone develops in areas where bone is normally absent. There is heteroplasia of bone.
