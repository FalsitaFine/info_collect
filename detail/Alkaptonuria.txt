
Alkaptonuria: A genetic metabolic disorder due to deficiency of the enzyme homogentisic acid (HGA) dioxygenase. Deficiency of this enzyme leads to the three cardinal features of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/alkaptonuria/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">alkaptonuria</a> (the presence of homogentisic acid in the urine), ochronosis (bluish-black pigmentation in connective tissue), and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/arthritis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">arthritis</a>. Urine that turns dark is a characteristic feature.
