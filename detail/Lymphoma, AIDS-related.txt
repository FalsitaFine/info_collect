
Lymphoma, AIDS-related: A condition that occurs in people with AIDS, in which lymphoid tumors are present, presumably due to immune-system impairment. Treatment is like that of other lymphomas but must take into account the fact that the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/human_immunodeficiency_virus_hiv/article.htm07" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">natural immunity</a> is impaired.
