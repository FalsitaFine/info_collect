
Binaural: Relating to both ears. While hearing aids may be binaural (in both ears) or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22562" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">monaural</a> (in just one ear), binaural aids are generally considered to be superior. Synonymous with stereophonic.
