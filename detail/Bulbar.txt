
Bulbar: Pertaining to a bulb, in medicine any rounded mass of tissue (that is shaped somewhat like a crocus or tulip bulb). For example, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9898" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">bulbar conjunctiva</a> is that part of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9893" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">conjunctiva</a>, a clear membrane of the eye, which covers the outer rounded surface of the eye. Bulbar can also apply to a rounded enlargement.
