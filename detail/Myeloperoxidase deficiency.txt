
Myeloperoxidase deficiency: Deficiency of the enzyme myeloperoxidase which may predispose to severe fungal infections. The enzyme is critical to the oxygen-dependent killing of bacteria by <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4145" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">leukocytes</a> (white blood cells).
