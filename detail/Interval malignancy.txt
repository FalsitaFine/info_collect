
Interval malignancy: In mammography, a malignancy that becomes evident during the period between annual screening mammograms.  The finding of an interval malignancy indicates that it either went undetected on the prior <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/breast_anatomy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">breast</a> imaging scan or that it developed during the interval since that last scan.
