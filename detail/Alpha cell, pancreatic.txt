
Alpha cell, pancreatic: A type of cell found in areas within the pancreas called the islets of Langerhans. Alpha cells make and release <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/glucagon/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">glucagon</a>, which raises the level of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/glucose/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">glucose</a> (sugar) in the blood.
