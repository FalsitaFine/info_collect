
Labia, vaginal: The two pairs of labia (lips)'the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6190" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">labia majora</a> and labia minor'at the entrance to the vagina.
