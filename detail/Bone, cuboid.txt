
Bone, cuboid: The outer bone in the instep of the foot. It is called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9014" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cuboid bone</a> because it is shaped like a cube. The cuboid bone is jointed in back with the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6420" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">heel bone</a> (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9013" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">calcaneus</a>) and in front with the bones just behind the fourth and fifth toes (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4365" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">metatarsals</a>).
