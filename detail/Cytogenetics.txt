
Cytogenetics: The study of chromosomes, which are the visible carriers of the hereditary material. Cytogenetics is a fusion <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13754" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">science</a>, joining <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=80540" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cytology</a> (the study of cells) with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15390" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">genetics</a> (the study of inherited variation).
