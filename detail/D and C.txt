
D and C: Stands for dilatation and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10985" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">curettage</a>. This is a very common gynecologic procedure in which the cervix of the uterus is
expanded (dilated) so the cervical canal and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3242" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">endometrium</a> (the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9921" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">uterine lining</a>) can be scraped off with a spoon-shaped instrument called a curet or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2881" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">curette</a>. This is a relatively minor procedure.
