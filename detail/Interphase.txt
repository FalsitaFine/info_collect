
Interphase: The interval in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7107" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cell cycle</a> between two cell divisions, during which the individual chromosomes cannot be distinguished. Interphase was once thought to be a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5334" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">resting phase</a>, but it is actually the time when DNA is replicated in the cell nucleus.
