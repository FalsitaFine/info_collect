
Eschar: The scab formed when a wound or
skin is sealed by the heat of cautery or burning. Also the dark crusted ulcer (tache
noire) at the site of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/chiggers_bites/article.htm">chigger</a> (mite larva) bite in scrub <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/typhus/article.htm">typhus</a>.
