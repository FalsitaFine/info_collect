
Aggressive lymphoma: A lymphoma that tends to grow and spread quickly and cause severe symptoms.  In contrast to an indolent or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25089" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">low-grade lymphoma</a> which tends to grow quite slowly and cause fewer symptoms. Aggressive lymphoma are also known as intermediate or high-grade lymphomas.
