
Ectomy: The surgical removal of something. For example, a lumpectomy is the surgical removal of a lump, a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tonsillectomy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">tonsillectomy</a> is the removal of the tonsils, and an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/appendectomy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">appendectomy</a> is removal of the appendix.
