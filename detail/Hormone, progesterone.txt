
Hormone, progesterone: A female hormone, progesterone
is the principal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23937" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">progestational hormone</a>. Progestational hormones prepare the uterus (the
womb) to receive and sustain the fertilized egg.
