
Denver Developmental Screening Test: The Denver <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/developmental_screening/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">Developmental Screening</a> Test (DDST) is a widely used assessment for examining children 0-6 years of age as to their developmental progress. The name "Denver" reflects the fact that this screening test was created at the University of Colorado Medical Center in Denver.
