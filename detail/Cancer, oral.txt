
Cancer, oral: A malignant <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a> of the mouth area. A sore in the mouth that does not heal can be a warning sign of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/oral_cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">oral cancer</a>. A biopsy is the only way to determine whether an abnormal area in the oral cavity is cancerous. Oral <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a> is almost always caused by tobacco (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/smoking_and_quitting_smoking/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">smoking</a> and chewing) or alcohol use. Surgery to remove the tumor in the mouth is the usual treatment.
