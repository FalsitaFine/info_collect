
Heart ventricle: One of the two lower chambers of the heart. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9124" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">right ventricle</a> receives blood from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26566" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">right atrium</a> and pumps it into the lungs via the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24368" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pulmonary artery</a>, and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9127" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">left ventricle</a> receives blood from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26567" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">left atrium</a> and pumps it into the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2735" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">circulation</a> system via the aorta.
