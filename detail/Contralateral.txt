
Contralateral: Of or pertaining to the other side. The opposite of ipsilateral (the same side). For example, a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/stroke_symptoms_and_treatment/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">stroke</a> involving the right side of the brain may cause contralateral <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/paralysis/symptoms.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sym">paralysis</a> of the left leg.
