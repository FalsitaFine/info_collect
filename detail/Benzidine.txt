
Benzidine: A compound used mainly for dyeing textiles and paper that is a known human carcinogen (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a>-causing agent). Benzidine was one of the first chemicals for which an association of occupational exposure and increased incidence of urinary <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/bladder_cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">bladder cancer</a> in humans was reported.
