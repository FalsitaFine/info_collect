
Cannabinoid receptor 2: One of the two known receptors in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=31402" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">endocannabinoid</a> (EC) system. The gene encoding cannabinoid receptor 2 (CNR2) is located on <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=31292" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chromosome 1</a>. Also known as the CB2 receptor or CB2.
