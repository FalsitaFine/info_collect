
Allopathic: Pertaining to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10981" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">allopathy</a> (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33527" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">conventional medicine</a>). The term was coined in 1842 by C.F.S. Hahnemann to designate the usual practice of medicine as opposed to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3775" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">homeopathy</a>, the system of therapy that he founded.
