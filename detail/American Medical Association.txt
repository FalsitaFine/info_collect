
American Medical Association: The AMA. The AMA's mission statement proclaims:
"We are the Voice of the American <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=153734" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Medical Profession</a>.
"We are the partnership of physicians and their professional associations dedicated to promoting the art and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13754" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">science</a> of medicine and the betterment of public health. "We serve the physicians and their patients by establishing and promoting ethical, educational, and clinical standards for the medical profession and by advocating for the highest principle of all--the integrity of the physician/patient relationship."
