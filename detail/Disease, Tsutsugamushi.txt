
Disease, Tsutsugamushi: Scrub <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/typhus/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">typhus</a>, a mite-borne
infectious disease caused by a microorganism, Rickettsia tsutsugamushi, characteristically
with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/headache/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">headache</a>, a raised (macular) <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">rash</a>, swollen glands (lymphadenopathy) and a dark
crusted ulcer (called an eschar or tache noire) at the site of the chigger (mite larva)
bite. This disease occurs in the area bounded by Japan, India, and Australia. Known also
as mite-borne typhus and tropical typhus.
