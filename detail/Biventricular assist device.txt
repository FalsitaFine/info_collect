
Biventricular assist device: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=19095" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ventricular assist device</a> that helps both <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26788" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ventricles</a> of the heart. It helps the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9124" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">right ventricle</a> of the heart to pump blood to the lungs and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9127" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">left ventricle</a> to pump blood to the body. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38576" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Berlin Heart</a> is an example of a biventricular assist device.
