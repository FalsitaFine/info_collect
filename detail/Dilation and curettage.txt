
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/dilation_and_curettage/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">Dilation and curettage</a>: A minor operation in which the cervix is expanded enough (dilation) to permit the cervical canal and uterine lining to be scraped with a curet (or curette), an instrument shaped like a small spoon. This procedure also is called D and C.
