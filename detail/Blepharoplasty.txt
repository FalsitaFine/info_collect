
Blepharoplasty: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/plastic_surgery_cosmetic_surgery/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">Plastic surgery</a> on the eyelids. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/blepharoplasty__eyelid_surgery/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">Blepharoplasty</a> may be done to correct ptosis (sagging eyelids), remove fatty bulges around the eyes, and eliminate hanging skin from the eyelids, often times a cosmetic procedure to improve appearance but may be medically necessary as when the sagging is blocking the field of vision.
