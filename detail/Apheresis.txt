
Apheresis: The process of removing a specific component from blood temporarily. Also known as hemapheresis and pheresis. Forms of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hemapheresis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">apheresis</a> include plasmapheresis, harvesting plasma or liquid part of the blood; leukapheresis, harvesting leukocytes or white blood cells; granulocytapheresis, harvesting granulocytes; lymphocytapheresis, harvesting lymphocytes; lymphoplasmapheresis, harvesting lymphocytes and plasma; and plateletpheresis, harvesting platelets.
