
Costochondritis: Inflammation and swelling of the cartilage of the chest wall, usually involving the cartilage that surrounds the breastbone (sternum) but sometimes including the adjacent tip of a rib. Costochondritis causes local pain and tenderness of the chest around the sternum. Treatment options include anti-inflammatory medications and, in severe cases, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/asthma_overview/article.htm9" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">corticosteroid</a> injections. Also known as Tietze syndrome.
