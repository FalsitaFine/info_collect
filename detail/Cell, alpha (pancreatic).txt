
Cell, alpha (pancreatic): A type of cell in the pancreas (the organ of the
digestive system located behind the stomach). Within the pancreas, the alpha
cells are located in areas called the islets of Langerhans. Alpha cells make
and release <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/glucagon/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">glucagon</a> which raises the level of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/glucose/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">glucose</a> (sugar) in the blood.
