
Conjunctivitis: Inflammation of the membrane covering the surface of the eyeball. It can be a result of infection or irritation of the eye, or it can be related to systemic diseases, such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5290" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Reiter syndrome</a>. Also known as pinkeye.
