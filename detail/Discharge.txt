
Discharge:
1.The flow of fluid from part of the body, such as from the nose or vagina.<br>
2. The passing of an action potential, such as through a nerve or muscle <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/fiber/article.htm">fiber</a>.<br>
3.  The release of a patient from a course of care. The doctor may then dictate a discharge summary.</br></br>
