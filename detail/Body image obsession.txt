
Body image obsession: Excessive concern about one's appearance. Some concern about one's appearance is normal. But an obsession with one's appearance is not. Body image obsession is also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/body_dysmorphic_disorder/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">body dysmorphic disorder</a>.  The condition carries a high risk of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/suicide/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">suicide</a>. Drug treatment can be lifesaving.
