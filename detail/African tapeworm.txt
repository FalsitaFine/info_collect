
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2170" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">African tapeworm</a>: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5705" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Taenia saginata</a>, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2445" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">beef tapeworm</a>, is the most common of the big tapeworms that parasitizes people, contracted from infected raw or rare beef. Can grow to be 12-25 <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3405" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">feet</a> (3.6-7.5 m) long <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">in</a> the human <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4004" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">intestine</a>.
