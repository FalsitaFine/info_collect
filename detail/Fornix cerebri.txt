
Fornix cerebri: One of two arching fibrous bands in the brain that connect the two lobes of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2678" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cerebrum</a>. Each of the two <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11366" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">fornices</a> in the brain is an arched tract of nerves.
