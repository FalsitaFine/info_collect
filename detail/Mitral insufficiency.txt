
Mitral insufficiency: A malfunction of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4410" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">mitral valve</a> that permits the backflow of blood (regurgitation) from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9127" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">left ventricle</a> into the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26567" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">left atrium</a>. Most mitral insufficiency is mild and requires no treatment. When severe, however, treatment with medications and sometimes surgery is necessary.
