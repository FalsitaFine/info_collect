
Booster shot: An additional dose of a vaccine needed periodically to 'boost' the immune system. For example, a booster shot of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tetanus/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tetanus</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diphtheria_facts/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">diphtheria</a> (Td) vaccine is recommended for adults every 10 years.
