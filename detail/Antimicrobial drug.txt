
Antimicrobial drug: A drug used to treat a microbial infection. "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9621" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Antimicrobial</a>" is a general term that refers to a group of drugs that includes antibiotics, antifungals, antiprotozoals, and antivirals.
