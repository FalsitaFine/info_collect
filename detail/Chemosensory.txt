
Chemosensory: Pertaining to the sensing of chemicals, as by olfaction (the sense of smell). <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diabetes_mellitus/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Diabetes</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/parkinsons_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Parkinson's disease</a> are among the diseases accompanied or signaled by chemosensory problems such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/smell_disorders/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">smell disorders</a>.
