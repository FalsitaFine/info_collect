
Horror autotoxicus: Literally, the horror of self-toxicity. A term coined by the great German bacteriologist and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3940" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immunologist</a> <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=31950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Paul Ehrlich</a> (1854-1915) to describe the body's innate aversion to immunological self-destruction. However, as we now know, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3907" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immune system</a> can upon occasion attack itself and does so in the autoimmune disorders.
