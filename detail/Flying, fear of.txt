
Flying, fear of: An irrational <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9960" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">fear of flying</a> is called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9945" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">aerophobia</a>. Aerophobia also refers to irrational fear of fresh air or drafts of air.  From the Greek "aero-", air or gas + "phobos", fear.
A virtual reality program that simulates the experience of take-offs, landings, normal flying and turbulence reportedly helps some people overcome their fear of flying.
