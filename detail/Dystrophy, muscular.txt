
Dystrophy, muscular: One of a group of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3573">genetic</a> diseases characterized by <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10697">progressive</a> weakness and degeneration of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16376">skeletal</a> or voluntary muscles which control movement. The muscles of the heart and some other involuntary muscles are also affected in some forms of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4468">muscular</a> dystrophy, and a few forms involve other organs as well.
