
Biotechnology: The fusion of biology and technology, the application of biological techniques to product research and development. In particular, biotechnology involves the use by industry of recombinant DNA, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32440" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cell fusion</a>, and new bioprocessing techniques to produce large molecules useful in treating and preventing disease.
