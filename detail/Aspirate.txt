
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2368" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Aspirate</a>: To draw <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">in</a> by suction. A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39154" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">patient</a> may aspirate for example by accidentally drawing material from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5560" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">stomach</a> into the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4209" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">lungs</a>. A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9237" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">doctor</a> can aspirate a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4074" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">joint</a>.
