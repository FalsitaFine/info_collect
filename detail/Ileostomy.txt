
Ileostomy: An opening into the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6451" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ileum</a>, part of the small intestine, from the outside of the body. An ileostomy provides a new path for waste material to leave the body after part of the intestine has been removed.
