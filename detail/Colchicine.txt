
Colchicine: A substance found in a plant that is used
in clinical medicine for the treatment of gouty <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/arthritis/article.htm">arthritis</a> and in the laboratory to arrest
cells during cell division (by disrupting the spindle) so their chromosomes can be
visualized. The name <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/colchicine/article.htm">colchicine</a> is from the Greek kolchikon meaning autumn crocus or
meadow saffron, the plant from which <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/colchicine/article.htm">colchicine</a> was originally isolated.
