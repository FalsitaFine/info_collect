
Enanthem: Or enanthema, is a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">rash</a> inside the body. An example: the spots in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/measles_rubeola/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">measles</a> (Koplik's spots) inside the mouth that look like a tiny grains of white sand surrounded by a red ring.
