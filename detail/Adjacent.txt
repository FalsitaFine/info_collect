
Adjacent: Lying nearby. Related terms include <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40136" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">superjacent</a>, subjacent, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40138" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">circumjacent</a>. From ad-, near + the Latin jacere, to lie = to lie near.
