
Materteral: 1. Literally, relating to a maternal aunt.<br> 2. Figuratively, suggestive of an aunt on either side of the family. 3 In medical <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15390" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">genetics</a>, pertaining to an aunt. An materteral relationship is the genetic relationship between aunts and their nieces and nephews. Materteral is synonymous with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32722" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">materterine</a>. From the Latin matertera meaning maternal aunt, from mater meaning mother. The feminine counterpart of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25597" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">avuncular</a> (like an uncle).</br>
