
Acid reflux:  A common condition and an abnormal one in which acid in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5560">stomach</a> rises up into the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3326">esophagus</a>. This occurs because the valve separating the contents of the stomach from the esophagus does not function properly. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/gastroesophageal_reflux_disease_gerd/article.htm">GERD</a>.
