
Band, Q: A form of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18598" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chromosome band</a>, one of the bright and dull fluorescent bands seen alternating along the length of chromosomes under ultraviolet light after the chromosomes are stained with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5176" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">quinacrine</a>. 
The Q in Q band stands for Quinacrine, an agent used as an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16900" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">antimalarial</a> agent and, in the laboratory, as a fluorescent dye.
