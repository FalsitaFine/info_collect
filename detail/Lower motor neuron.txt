
Lower motor neuron: A nerve cell that goes from the spinal cord to a muscle. The cell body of a lower motor neuron is in the spinal cord and its termination is in a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5502" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">skeletal muscle</a>. The loss of lower motor neurons leads to weakness, twitching of muscle (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13290" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">fasciculation</a>), and loss of muscle mass (muscle atrophy).
