
Enzyme, restriction: An enzyme from bacteria that can recognize specific base sequences in DNA and cut the DNA at that site (the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5340" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">restriction site</a>). A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5337" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">restriction enzyme</a> acts as a biochemical scissors. Also called a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5336" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">restriction endonuclease</a>.
