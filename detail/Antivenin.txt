
Antivenin: Antibodies created in the blood of a horse or sheep when the animal is injected with snake venom. Antivenin acts by neutralizing snake venom that has entered the body. Also called antivenom, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33630" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">snake antivenin</a>, or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33629" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">snake antivenom</a>.
