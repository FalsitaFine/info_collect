
Fiber and constipation: Insoluble <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/fiber/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">fiber</a> retains water  in the colon, resulting in a softer and larger stool. It can be used effectively in treating   <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/constipation/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">constipation</a>. Bran is particularly rich in insoluble  fiber.
