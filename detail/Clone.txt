
Clone: Literally a fragment, 
the word in modern medical science has come to mean a replica, for example, of a 
group of bacteria or a macromolecule such as
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13963"> DNA</a>. Clone also refers to an individual developed from a single <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25477">somatic</a> (non-germ) 
cell 
from a parent, representing an exact replica of that parent. A clone is a group of cells
derived from a single ancestral cell.
