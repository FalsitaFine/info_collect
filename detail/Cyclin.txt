
Cyclin: One of a group of proteins that regulate the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7107" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cell cycle</a>. The cyclins bind to and activate cyclin-dependent kinases. The cyclins include cyclin A, cyclin B, cyclin C,   <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=17567" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cyclin D</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=21787" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cyclin E</a>, cyclin F, cyclin G, cyclin H, cyclin K, and cyclin T.
