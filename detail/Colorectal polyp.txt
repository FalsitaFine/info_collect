
Colorectal polyp: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4996">polyp</a> (a fleshy growth) on the inside 
lining of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2787">colon</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5253">rectum</a>. For more information, see: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38872">Colon polyp</a>.
