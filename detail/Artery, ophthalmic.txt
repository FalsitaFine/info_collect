
Artery, ophthalmic: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9147" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ophthalmic artery</a> supplies blood to the eye and adjacent structures of face. It arises from the internal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2641" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">carotid artery</a> that courses up deep within the front of the neck.
