
Cataract, primary: A cataract that develops independently of other diseases. A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24425" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">primary cataract</a> is in contrast to a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24426" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">secondary cataract</a>, one that is secondary to another disease.
