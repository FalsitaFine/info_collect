
Ligament, medial collateral knee: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8845">knee joint</a> is surrounded by a joint capsule with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4159">ligaments</a> strapping the inside and outside of the joint (collateral ligaments) as well as crossing within the joint (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8866">cruciate</a> ligaments). These ligaments provide stability and strength to the knee joint.
