
DVT (deep vein thrombosis): Blood clotting in the   veins of the inner thigh or leg. In air travel, DVT is the   "economy-class syndrome." DVT can be associated with any state of relative immobility, including prolonged sitting, following surgery, or with illness. Some DVT are associated with familial syndromes.  <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/blood_clots/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Blood clots</a> can break off (as emboli)   and makes their way to the lung where they have the potential of   causing respiratory distress and respiratory failure.
