
Nodule: A small collection of tissue that is palpable (can be felt) at any level of the skin (in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3278" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">epidermis</a>, dermis, or subcutis) or in another tissue of the body. Nodules characteristically range in size from 1 to 2 cm in diameter.
