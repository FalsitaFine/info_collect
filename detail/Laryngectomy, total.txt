
Laryngectomy, total: A laryngectomy in which the whole larynx (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9019" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">voice box</a>) is removed, and the opening in the front of the neck (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5559" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">stoma</a>) is permanent. The patient breathes through the stoma and must learn to talk in a new way.
