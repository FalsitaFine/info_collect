
Mycobacterium avium: A slow-growing bacterium  found in the soil and in dust particles that causes <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tuberculosis_tb_facts/article.htm">tuberculosis</a> in birds and swine and is responsible for the mycobacterium avian complex (MAC) in 
humans. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8702">Mycobacterium avian complex</a>.
