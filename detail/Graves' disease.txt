
Graves' disease: Generalized diffuse overactivity ("toxicity") of the entire thyroid gland which becomes enlarged into a goiter. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/graves_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Graves' disease</a> is the most common cause of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hyperthyroidism/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hyperthyroidism</a>.
