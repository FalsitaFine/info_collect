
Connective tissue:  A material made up of fibers forming a framework and support structure for body tissues and organs. Connective tissue surrounds many organs. Cartilage and bone are specialized forms of connective tissue. All connective tissue is derived from <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20839">mesoderm</a>, the middle <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8591">germ cell</a> layer in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3225">embryo</a>.
