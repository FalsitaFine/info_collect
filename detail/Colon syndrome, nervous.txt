
Colon syndrome, nervous: A common gastrointestinal disorder involving an abnormal condition of gut contractions (motility) characterized by <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/abdominal_pain_causes_remedies_treatment/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">abdominal pain</a>, bloating, mucous in stools, and irregular bowel habits with alternating
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diarrhea/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt"> diarrhea</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/constipation/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">constipation</a>, symptoms that tend to be chronic and to wax and wane over the
years. Although the disorder can cause chronic recurrent discomfort, it does not lead to any
serious organ problems. Making the diagnosis usually involves excluding other illnesses. Treatment is
directed toward relief of symptoms and includes changes in diet (eating high <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/fiber/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">fiber</a> and avoiding <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/caffeine/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">caffeine</a>, milk products and sweeteners),  <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/exercise/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">exercise</a>, relaxation
techniques, and medications. Alternative names include <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/irritable_bowel_syndrome_ibs_triggers_prevention/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">irritable bowel syndrome</a> (IBS), spastic
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/colitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt"> colitis</a>, and mucus colitis.
