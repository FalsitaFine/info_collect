
Merkle cell carcinoma: A rapidly growing type of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/skin_cancer_overview/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">skin cancer</a>, spelled Merkel cell carcinoma, after the German anatomist and pathologist Friedrich Sigmund Merkel (1845-1919) It is a rare, aggressive malignant cancer of the skin that primarily affects elderly Caucasians.
