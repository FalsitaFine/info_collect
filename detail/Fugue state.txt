
Fugue state: An altered state of consciousness in which a person may move about purposely and even speak, but is not fully aware. A fugue state is usually a type of complex partial <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/seizure/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">seizure</a>. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11202">Seizure, complex partial</a>.
