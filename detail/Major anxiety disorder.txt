The major anxiety disorders are:  <ul> <li>panic disorder;  <li>obsessive-compulsive disorder;  <li>PTSD (post-traumatic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/stress/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">stress</a> disorder);  <li>social phobia;  <li>specific <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/phobias/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">phobias</a> such as claustrophobia; and  <li>generalized anxiety disorder.  </li> </li></li></li></li></li></ul>
