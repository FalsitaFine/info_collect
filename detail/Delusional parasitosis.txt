
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23449" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Delusional parasitosis</a>: A mistaken belief that one is infested by parasites such as mites, lice, fleas, spiders, worms, or other organisms.
