
Meatus, female urethral: The meatus (opening) of the female urethra, the transport tube that leads from the bladder to discharge urine outside the body. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8878" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">female urethral meatus</a> is above the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8836" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">vaginal opening</a>.
