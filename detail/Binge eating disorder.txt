
Binge eating disorder: An eating disorder characterized by periods of extreme overeating, but not followed by purging behaviors, as in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/bulimia_nervosa/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">bulimia</a>. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/binge_eating_disorder/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Binge eating disorder</a> can occur alone or in association with abnormality of the brain's hypothalamus gland, Prader-Willi disorder, or other medical conditions. It can contribute to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/high_blood_pressure_hypertension/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">high blood pressure</a>, weight gain, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diabetes_mellitus/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">diabetes</a>, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/heart_disease_coronary_artery_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">heart disease</a>. Treatment may include therapy, dietary education and advice, and medication.
