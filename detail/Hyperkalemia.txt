
Hyperkalemia: Elevated potassium in the blood. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hyperkalemia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Hyperkalemia</a> can be caused by taking excessive amounts of potassium, by medications, tissue trauma, and by diseases such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/kidney_failure/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">kidney failure</a>. Hyperkalemia may not produce any symptoms, but severe hyperkalemia can lead to potentially fatal arrythmias of the heart.
