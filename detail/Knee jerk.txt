
Knee jerk: The reflex that is tested by tapping just below the bent knee on the patellar tendon. Normally this causes the quadriceps muscle to contract and bring the lower leg forward. It has given rise to the saying 'a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=34168" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">knee-jerk</a> reaction.' Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=34192" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">patellar reflex</a>.
