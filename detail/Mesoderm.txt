
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20839" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Mesoderm</a>: One of the three <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12237" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">primary</a> <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8591" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">germ cell</a> layers, the others being the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20835" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ectoderm</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20838" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">endoderm</a>. The mesoderm is the middle layer. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">In</a> the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3225" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">embryo</a>, it differentiates to gives rise to a number of tissues and structures including <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2499" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">bone</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4464" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">muscle</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2824" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">connective tissue</a>, and the middle layer of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7901" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">skin</a>. Some cells in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20842" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">mesodermal</a> tissues retain the capacity to differentiate in diverse directions. For example, some cells in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2502" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">bone marrow</a> (mesoderm) can become <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4179" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">liver</a> (endoderm).
