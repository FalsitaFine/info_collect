
Muscle, teres minor: A muscle that assists in the lifting of the arm during outward turning (external rotation) of the arm. The tendon of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8202" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">teres minor muscle</a> is one of four tendons that stabilize the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9059" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">shoulder joint</a> and constitute the rotator cuff.
