
Mucolipidosis III: A type of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11680" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">mucolipidosis</a> that is characterized by deficiency of the enzyme N-acetylglucosamine-1-phosphotransferase and features of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3821" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Hurler syndrome</a>, but with much slower progression. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11904" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pseudo-Hurler polydystrophy</a>.
