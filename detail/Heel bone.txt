
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6420" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Heel bone</a>:  Formally called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9013" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">calcaneus</a>. A more or less rectangular <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2499" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">bone</a> at the back of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3499" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">foot</a>. The words "calcaneus" and "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2575" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">calcium</a>" come from the same source.
