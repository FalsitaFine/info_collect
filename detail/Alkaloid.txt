
Alkaloid: A member of a large group of chemicals that are made by plants and have nitrogen in them. Many alkaloids possess potent pharmacologic effects. The alkaloids include cocaine, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/nicotine/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">nicotine</a>, strychnine, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/caffeine/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">caffeine</a>, morphine, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pilocarpine/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">pilocarpine</a>, atropine, methamphetamine, mescaline, ephedrine, and tryptamine.
