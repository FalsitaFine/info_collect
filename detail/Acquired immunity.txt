
Acquired immunity: Immunity acquired by infection or vaccination (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2130" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">active immunity</a>) or by the transfer of antibody or lymphocytes from an immune donor (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10091" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">passive immunity</a>). Acquired immunity is in contrast to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/human_immunodeficiency_virus_hiv/article.htm04" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">innate immunity</a>  (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/human_immunodeficiency_virus_hiv/article.htm07" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">natural immunity</a>).
