
Khat: a flowering evergreen shrub native to East Africa and the Arabian Peninsula whose leaves, twigs, and shoots are chewed like tobacco and used as a recreational drug in many parts of the world. It can also be smoked, made into tea, or sprinkled on food. The greatest numbers of users are found in the areas where <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/khat/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">khat</a> grows naturally.
