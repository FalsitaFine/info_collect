
Alb-: Prefix from the Latin "albus" meaning "white." As in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2186">albino</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2185">albinism</a>. The term "albino" was first applied by the Portuguese to "white" people they encountered in West Africa. Those "white" people probably had partial or complete albinism, an inherited lack of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/stroke_symptoms_and_treatment/article.htm9">pigment</a> in the skin, hair, and eyes.
