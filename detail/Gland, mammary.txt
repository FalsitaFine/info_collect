
Gland, mammary: One of the glands within the 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/breast_anatomy/article.htm"> breast</a> that secrete milk when prompted to do so by special 
hormones. Mammary glands are slightly tender to the touch, 
and may become more so during menstruation or pregnancy. 
They become enlarged when engorged with milk. See also 
mastitis.
