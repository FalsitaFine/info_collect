
Catheter, pulmonary artery:  A light flexible balloon-tipped tube that is introduced into the pulmonary artery (the artery from the right <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5984">ventricle</a> of the heart to the lungs). See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11108">Catheter, Swann-Ganz</a>.
