
Involute: 1. Literally, to turn inward or roll inward. 2.  To decrease in size after an enlargement. The uterus involutes after <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pregnancy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">pregnancy</a>. The thymus involutes after adolescence. 3. To undergo a retrograde change. After treatment, a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a> may involute. 4. To shrink physically and emotionally with advancing age.
From the Latin involutio, from in- meaning into + volvere meaning to roll.
