
Hypomagnesemia: Low magnesium in the blood, which can occur due to inadequate intake or impaired intestinal absorption of magnesium. Hypomagnesemia is often associated with low <br/> calcium (hypocalcemia) and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/low_potassium_hypokalemia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">low potassium</a> (hypokalemia). It causes increased irritability of the nervous system with tetany (spasms of the hands and feet, muscular twitching and cramps, spasm of the larynx, and overly active reflexes).
