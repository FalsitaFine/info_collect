
Foot pain: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/foot_pain/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Foot pain</a> is the sensation of discomfort in the foot arising from disorders or injury to any of the structures in the foot including bones, nerves, muscles, blood vessels, or connective tissue. There are numerous and varied causes for foot pain, since the foot is an extremely complex anatomic structure made up of 26 bones and 33 joints along with 19 muscles and 107 ligaments.
