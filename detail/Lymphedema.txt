
Lymphedema: A condition in which excess fluid collects in tissue and causes swelling. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/lymphedema/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Lymphedema</a> may occur in the arm or leg after lymph vessels or lymph nodes in the underarm or groin are removed. It usually causes painless swelling.
