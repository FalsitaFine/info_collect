
Brainstem implant: A type of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22561" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hearing aid</a> that bypasses the cochlea in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7867" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">middle ear</a> and the auditory nerve and is implanted in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23623" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">brainstem</a>. This type of implant helps individuals who cannot benefit from a cochlear implant because the auditory nerves are not working.
