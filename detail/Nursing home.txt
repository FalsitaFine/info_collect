
Nursing home: A residential facility for people with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2731" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chronic illness</a> or disability, particularly older people who have mobility and eating problems. Also known as a convalescent home and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24859" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">long-term care facility</a>.
