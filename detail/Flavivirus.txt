
Flavivirus: One of a family of viruses transmitted by mosquitos and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/ticks/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">ticks</a> that cause some important diseases, including dengue, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/yellow_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">yellow fever</a>, tick-borne encephalitis, and West Nile <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a>. Flaviviruses have single-stranded RNA as their genetic material.
