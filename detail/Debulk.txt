
Debulk: To remove part of the bulk, usually of a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a> as in surgery to remove as much tumor as possible to increase the likelihood of success with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/chemotherapy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">chemotherapy</a>, for example, or dead tissue. Debulking may be done by surgery, irradiation, laser or chemotherapy.
