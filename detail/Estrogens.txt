<li>esterified <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/estrogens_conjugated-vaginal_cream/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">estrogens</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/esterified_estrogens_and_methyltestosterone/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">esterified estrogens and methyltestosterone</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/estradiol/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">estradiol</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/estrogens_conjugated/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">estrogens conjugated</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/medroxyprogesterone/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">medroxyprogesterone</a></li>
<li>estrogens conjugated</li>
</ul>
