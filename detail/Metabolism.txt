
Metabolism: The whole range of biochemical processes that occur within a living organism. Metabolism consists of anabolism (the buildup of substances) and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11103" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">catabolism</a> (the breakdown of substances). The term metabolism is commonly used to refer specifically to the breakdown of food and its transformation into energy.
