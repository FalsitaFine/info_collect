
Incontinence, rectal: Inability to hold feces in the
 rectum due to failure of voluntary control over the anal sphincters with involuntary passage of feces and gas. Also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/fecal_incontinence/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fecal incontinence</a>.
