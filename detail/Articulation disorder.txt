
Articulation disorder: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11250" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">speech disorder</a> involving difficulties in articulating specific types of sounds. Articulation disorders often involve substitution of one sound for another, slurring of speech, or indistinct speech. Treatment is <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11252" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">speech therapy</a>.
