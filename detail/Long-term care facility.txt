
Long-term care facility: A facility that provides rehabilitative, restorative, and/or ongoing skilled nursing care to patients or residents in need of assistance with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2131" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">activities of daily living</a>. Long-term care facilities include nursing homes, rehabilitation facilities, inpatient behavioral health facilities, and long-term chronic care hospitals.
