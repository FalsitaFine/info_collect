
Ethmoid: 1. As an adjective: spongy or sievelike. From the Greek ethmos, sieve + eidos, resemblance = like a sieve.  2. As a noun; short for the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10339" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ethmoid bone</a>, a spongy bone that serves as the front floor of the skull and the roof of the nose. 3. As a noun: short for the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=31868" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ethmoid sinus</a>, which is made up of numerous thin-walled air cells within the ethmoid bone.
