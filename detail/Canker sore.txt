
Canker sore: A common small, frequently painful and sensitive crater in the lining of the mouth. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2316" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">aphthous ulcer</a>. Sores typically last for 10 to 14 days and generally heal without scarring.
