
Filovirus: A virus in the family filoviridae that causes hemorrhagic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a>. Filoviruses have single-stranded RNA as their genetic material. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/ebola_hemorrhagic_fever_ebola_hf/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Ebola</a> virus and the Marburg virus are both filoviruses.
