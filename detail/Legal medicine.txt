
Legal medicine: The branch of medicine that deals with the application of medical knowledge to legal problems and legal proceedings. Legal medicine is also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10606" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">forensic medicine</a>. 
A physician may be engaged in legal (or forensic) medicine while a lawyer with identical interests is said to be in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23312" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">medical jurisprudence</a>. It just depends upon the direction you are coming from.
