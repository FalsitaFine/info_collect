
Debridement: The act of debriding (removing dead, contaminated or adherent tissue or foreign material). Debridement encompasses enzymatic debridement (as with proteolytic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15392">enzymes</a>), mechanical nonselective debridement (as in a whirlpool), and sharp debridement (by <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5603">surgery</a>). See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40481">Debride</a>.
