
Dizziness: Painless head  discomfort with many possible causes including disturbances of  vision, the brain, balance (vestibular) system of the inner  ear, and gastrointestinal system.  Dizziness is a medically  indistinct term which laypersons use to describe a variety of  conditions ranging from lightheadedness, unsteadiness to vertigo.
