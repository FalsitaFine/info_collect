
Amino acid, essential: An amino acid that cannot be made by humans and so is essential to the human diet. There are 9 essential <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39555" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">amino acids</a>: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22827" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">histidine</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22828" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">isoleucine</a>, leucine, lysine, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22831" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">methionine</a>, phenylalanine, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22834" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">threonine</a>, tryptophan, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22837" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">valine</a>.
