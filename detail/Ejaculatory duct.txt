
Ejaculatory duct: A canal in the male formed by union of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20863" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">vas deferens</a> and the duct from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5448" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">seminal vesicle</a>. The ejaculatory duct passes through the prostate. Semen passes through them at the time of ejaculation.
