
Geriatric medicine: The branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis, treatment and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/prevention/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">prevention</a> of disease in older people and the problems specific to aging. Also called geriatrics. From the Greek "geron" meaning "old man" + "iatreia" meaning "the treatment of disease.".
