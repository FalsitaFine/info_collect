
Epididymis: A structure within the scrotum that is attached to the back side of the testis. The epididymis is a coiled segment of the spermatic ducts that stores spermatozoa while they mature and then transports the spermatozoa between the testis and the tube connecting the testes with the urethra (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20863" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">vas deferens</a>).
