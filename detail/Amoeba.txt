
Amoeba: Also ameba.  A single-celled 
(protozoan) organism that constantly changes shape. The word "ameba" 
is from the Greek "amoibe" meaning "change." Ameba can infect the 
bowels to cause <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diarrhea/article.htm">diarrhea</a> and the liver to cause abscess formation.
