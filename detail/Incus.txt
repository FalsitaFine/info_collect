
Incus: One of the three tiny bones in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7867" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">middle ear</a>. Sound impulses coming from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3177" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">eardrum</a> are conveyed from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4264" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">malleus</a> to the incus and from the incus to the stapes at the oval window to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7210" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">inner ear</a>. Incus is Latin for anvil.
