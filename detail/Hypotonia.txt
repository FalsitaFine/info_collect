
Hypotonia: Decreased muscle tone and strength that results in floppiness. Hypotonia is a common finding with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cerebral_palsy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cerebral palsy</a> and other neuromuscular disorders. Untreated hypotonia can lead to hip dislocation and other problems. Treatment is via physical therapy. In some cases, braces may be needed to permit a full range of movement in patients with hypotonia.
