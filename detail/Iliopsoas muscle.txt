
Iliopsoas muscle: A blending of two muscles (the iliacus and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9653" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">psoas</a> major) that run from the lumbar portion of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11547" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">vertebral column</a> to the femur. The main action of the iliopsoas is to flex the thigh at the hip joint.
