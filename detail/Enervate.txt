
Enervate: 1. To remove part or all of a nerve, a procedure also called a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24484" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">neurectomy</a>. 2. To lose nervous energy and feel sapped of energy.<br>  From the Latin enervare (e-, out of + nervare from nervus, nerve) = out of nerve.</br>
