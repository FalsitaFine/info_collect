
Encephalomyelitis: Inflammation of both the brain and the spinal cord. Encephalomyelitis can <br/> be caused by a variety of conditions, including viruses that infect the nervous system. One type <br/> of encephalomyelitis, acute disseminatedencephalomyelitis, occurs most commonly after an acute viral infection, such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/measles_rubeola/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">measles</a> (rubeola). It is due to an autoimmune attack on the nervous system, meaning that the immune system mistakenly attacks body tissue that it believes to be the measles virus. Also known as myeloencephalitis.
