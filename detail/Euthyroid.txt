
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11352" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Euthyroid</a>: The state of having normal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5778" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroid gland</a> function. As opposed to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3849" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hyperthyroid</a> (overactive <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=19509" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroid</a>) or hypothyroid (underactive thyroid).
