
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2232" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ANA</a>: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6709" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Antinuclear antibody</a>, an unusual <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/liver_anatomy_and_function/article.htm01" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">antibody</a> directed against structures within the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4597" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">nucleus</a> of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2661" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cell</a>. ANAs are found <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">in</a> patients whose <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3907" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immune system</a> is predisposed to cause <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3979" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">inflammation</a> against their own body tissues. Antibodies that are directed against one's own tissues are referred to as autoantibodies. The propensity for the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3905" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immune</a> system to work against its own body is referred to as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18985" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">autoimmunity</a>. ANAs <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32544" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">indicate</a> the possible presence of autoimmunity.
