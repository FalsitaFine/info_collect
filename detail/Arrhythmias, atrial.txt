
Arrhythmias, atrial: Abnormal heart rhythm due to
electrical disturbances in the atria (the upper chambers of the heart) or the AV node
"relay station", leading to fast heart beats. Examples of atrial arrhythmias
includes <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/atrial_fibrillation/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">atrial fibrillation</a>, atrial flutter, and paroxysmal atrial tachycardia (PAT).
