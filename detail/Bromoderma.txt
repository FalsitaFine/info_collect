
Bromoderma: A skin eruption brought on by chronic exposure to bromine. Essentially an allergic reaction to bromine. There are diverse sources of bromine exposure including brominated vegetable oil, a commonly used emulsifier and flavor carrier in food products. Bromoderma is a form of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23343">bromism</a>.
