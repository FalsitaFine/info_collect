
Intelligence test: A questionnaire or series of exercises designed to measure intelligence. There are many types of intelligence tests, and they may measure learning and/or ability in a wide variety of areas and skills. Scores may be presented as an IQ (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11517" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">intelligence quotient</a>), as a mental age, or on a scale.
