
Mastectomy, preventative: Removal of one or both breasts without the current presence of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a>. This surgery is sometimes chosen as a preventative measure by women who have a strong history of familial <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/breast_anatomy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">breast</a>  cancer. Also known as a prophylactic mastectomy.
