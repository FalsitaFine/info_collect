
Messenger RNA: The key intermediary in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3564" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">gene expression</a>, which translates the DNA's <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3574" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">genetic code</a> into the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39555" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">amino acids</a> that make up proteins. Abbreviated mRNA.
