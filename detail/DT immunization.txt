
DT immunization: DT (diphtheria and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tetanus/article.htm">tetanus</a>) vaccine
does not protect from <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pertussis/article.htm">pertussis</a> and is usually reserved for individuals who have had a
significant adverse reaction to a DPT shot or who have a personal or family history of a
seizure disorder or brain disease.
