
Elbow, arthritis of the: Inflammation of the elbow joint. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/arthritis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Arthritis</a> of the elbow can be due to many systemic forms of arthritis, including <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rheumatoid_arthritis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">rheumatoid arthritis</a>, gouty arthritis, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/psoriatic_arthritis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">psoriatic arthritis</a>. Elbow arthritis is associated with signs of warmth, swelling, pain, tenderness, and decreased range of motion.
