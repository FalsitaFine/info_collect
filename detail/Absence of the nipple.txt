
Absence of the nipple: Medically called athelia, this is a rare condition that may occur with a number of hereditary diseases or chromosomal defects, such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/poland_syndrome/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Poland Syndrome</a>, progeria (premature aging), ectodermal dysplasia, and Yunis-Varon syndrome. Athelia tends to occurs on one side (unilaterally) in children with the Poland sequence and on both sides (bilaterally) in certain types of ectodermal dysplasia.
