
Intraocular lens: An artificial lens made of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/plastic/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">plastic</a>, silicone, acrylic or other material that is implanted in the eye during <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cataract_surgery/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">cataract surgery</a>. Abbreviated IOL.
