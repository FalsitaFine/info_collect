
Kneecap: The patella, the small bone in the front of the knee. The patella is a little (sesamoid) bone, embedded in the tendon of insertion of the quadriceps muscle. If the patella is shattered beyond repair, it can be removed in an operation called a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11870" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">patellectomy</a> and sometimes replaced with prosthetic material.
