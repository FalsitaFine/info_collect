
Nictitating spasm: Spasm of the eyelid with continuous winking. To nictitiate is to wink. "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10145" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Nictitate</a>" and the older verb "nictate" both come from the Latin word for winking, "nictare."
