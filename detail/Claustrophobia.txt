
Claustrophobia: An abnormal and persistent <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9965" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">fear of closed spaces</a>, of being closed in or being shut in, as in elevators, tunnels, or any other confined space. The fear is excessive (and quite common). The word "claustrophobia" is an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11923" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">amalgam</a> made from the Latin "claudere", to shut + the Greek "phobis", fear.
