
Keratotomy, radial: A surgical procedure designed to flatten the cornea and thereby correct nearsightedness (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/myopia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">myopia</a>). It is called a radial keratotomy because the radial incisions in the cornea resemble the spokes in a bicycle wheel.
