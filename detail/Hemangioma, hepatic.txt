
Hemangioma, hepatic: A common benign <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a> of the liver made up of small blood vessels. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hepatic_hemangioma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Hepatic hemangioma</a> is the most common benign liver tumor and is 4-6 times more common in women than men (female hormones may promote its formation and growth).
