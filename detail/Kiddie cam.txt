
Kiddie cam: A camcorder (a video camera and video recorder in one unit) that displays a live feed so that parents can monitor their children from a remote location. A kiddie cam may have other uses, as in a day-care center. Also known as a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33935" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">kinder-cam</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33936" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cradle cam</a>.
