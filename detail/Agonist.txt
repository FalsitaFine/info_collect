
Agonist: A substance that acts like another substance and therefore stimulates an action. Agonist is the opposite of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7836" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">antagonist</a>. Antagonists and agonists are key players in the chemistry of the human body and in pharmacology.
