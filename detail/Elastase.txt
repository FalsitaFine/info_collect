
Elastase: An <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3266">enzyme</a> that digests and degrades a number of proteins including <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24541">elastin</a>, an elastic substance in the lungs and some other organs that supports their structural framework. Elastase is specifically inhibited by alpha-1 antitrypsin. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2208">Alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency</a>.
