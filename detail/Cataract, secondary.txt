
Cataract, secondary: A cataract that develops secondary to another disease or surgery. The other disease may, for example, be <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/glaucoma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">glaucoma</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/retinal_detachment/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">retinal detachment</a>. A secondary cataract is in contrast to a primary cataract, one that develops independently of any other disease.
