
Fever, West Nile: A febrile disease caused by the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/west_nile_encephalitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">West Nile virus</a> that is transmitted from birds to the common Culex mosquito and then to people. The virus is named after the area it was first found in Uganda.
