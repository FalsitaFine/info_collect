
Heart-lung machine: A machine that does the work both of the heart and of the lungs: pumping and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38579" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">oxygenating</a> blood. Blood returning to the heart is diverted through a heart-lung machine before being returned to arterial <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2735" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">circulation</a>. Such machines may be used during open-heart surgery. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/urinary_tract_infection/article.htm2" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pump-oxygenator</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/human_immunodeficiency_virus_hiv/article.htm1" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cardiopulmonary bypass</a> machine.
