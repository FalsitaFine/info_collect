
Audiologist: A health care professional who is trained to evaluate <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/deafness/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hearing loss</a> and related disorders, including balance (vestibular) disorders and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tinnitus_ringing_in_the_ears/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tinnitus</a> (ringing in the ears) and to rehabilitate individuals with hearing loss and related disorders.
