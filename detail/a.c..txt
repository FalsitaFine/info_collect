
a.c.: Abbreviation on a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11896" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">prescription</a> meaning before meals; from the Latin "ante cibum", before meals. This is one of a number of abbreviations of Latin terms that have traditionally been used <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">in</a> writing prescriptions.
