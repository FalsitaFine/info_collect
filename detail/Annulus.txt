
Annulus: A ringlike structure, or any body part that is shaped like a ring. Applied to many small ring-shaped structures.  
The base of a heart valve that supports the valve's leaflets is called the annulus. A ring-shaped pancreas that encircles the duodenum is an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=19230" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">annular pancreas</a>. 
"Annulus" is a misspelling of the Latin "anulus" (a little ring), the diminutive of the Latin "anus" (a ring of more substantial size).
