
Conjunctiva: A thin, clear, moist membrane that coats the inner surfaces of the eyelids (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9896" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">palpebral conjunctiva</a>) and the outer surface of the eye (ocular, or bulbar, conjunctiva). Inflammation of the conjunctiva is called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2822" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">conjunctivitis</a> (pinkeye).
