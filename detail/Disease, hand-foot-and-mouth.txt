
Disease, hand-foot-and-mouth: A viral syndrome with a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">rash</a> on the hands
and
feet and in the mouth. The internal rash (the enanthem) consists of
blisters
and little ulcers that may involve not only the lining of the mouth but
also
the gums, palate, and tongue. The external rash on the body (the exanthem)
typically affects the hands (most commonly), feet and, sometimes, the
buttocks. There may also be <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sore_throat_pharyngitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">sore throat</a>, irritability,  decreased appetite,
and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a>.
