
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8177" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Juxtaposition</a>: The prefix "juxta-" comes from the Latin preposition meaning near, nearby, close. Juxtaposition is thus the act of placing two or more things side by side or the state of being so placed.
