
Mansfield, Peter: British scientist (1933-) who shared the 2003 Nobel Prize for Physiology or Medicine with the American scientist Paul C. Lauterbur for discoveries concerning "magnetic resonance imaging." (Magnetic resonance imaging is better known perhaps as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/mri_scan/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">MRI</a>). Mansfield did his research principally at the University of Nottingham.
