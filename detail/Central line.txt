
Central line: An infusion tube located in or near the heart, which is at the center of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2738" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">circulatory system</a>. For example, a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=14400" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Swan-Ganz catheter</a> with its tip in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26566" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">right atrium</a> and ventricle of the heart is a central line.
