
Diabetic ketoacidosis: High <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32858">blood glucose</a> with the presence of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39574">ketones</a> in the urine and bloodstream, often caused by taking too little <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3989">insulin</a> or during illness. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4102">Ketoacidosis</a>.
