
Hemorrhagic cyst: : This type of functional cyst occurs when bleeding occurs within a cyst. Symptoms such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/abdominal_pain_causes_remedies_treatment/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">abdominal pain</a> on one side of the body may be present with this type of cyst.
