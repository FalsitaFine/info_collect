
Bulbar conjunctiva: That part of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9893" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">conjunctiva</a>, a clear membrane of the eye, which covers the outer surface of the eye. The other part of the conjunctiva is the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9896" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">palpebral conjunctiva</a>, which lines the inside of the eyelids.
The bulbar conjunctiva is also called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9895" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ocular conjunctiva</a>.
