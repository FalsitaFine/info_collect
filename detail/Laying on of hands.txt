
Laying on of hands: In <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2211">alternative medicine</a>, an ancient method of healing by touching a person with the hands or palms, usually on the head, shoulders, or waist. Also called contact healing. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33524">Therapeutic touch</a>.
