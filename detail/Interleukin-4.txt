
Interleukin-4: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6554">protein</a> that stimulates the 
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3907"> immune system</a> to 
develop mast cells, resting T-cells, and activated B-cells. Abbreviated 
IL-4.
