
Litmus: A pigment used as a test for acidity and alkalinity. Litmus paper turns red in acid and blue in an alkaline solution. Litmus is prepared from lichens like Roccella tinctoria. Figuratively, a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39411" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">litmus test</a> is a decision in which a single factor is the determinant.
