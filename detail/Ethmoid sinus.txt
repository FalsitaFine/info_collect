
Ethmoid sinus: A collection of air cells within the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10339" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ethmoid bone</a>, a spongy bone which makes up the front of the floor of the skull and the roof of the nose.
From the Greek ethmos, sieve + eidos, resemblance = like a sieve.
