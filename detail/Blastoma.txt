
Blastoma: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a> thought to arise in embryonic tissue. This term is commonly used as part of the name for a tumor, as in glioblastoma and medulloblastoma (types of brain tumors), hepatoblastoma (a liver tumor), nephroblastoma (a Wilms tumor of the kidney), <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/neuroblastoma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">neuroblastoma</a> (a childhood tumor of neural origin), osteoblastoma (a bone tumor), and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/retinoblastoma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">retinoblastoma</a> (a tumor of the retina in the eye).
