
Cataract surgery: Removal of the clouded (cataractous) lens in its entirety via surgery and replacement of the lens with an intraocular lens (IOL) made of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/plastic/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">plastic</a>. A typical cataract operation takes about an hour, requires local anesthesia only, and usually does not require hospitalization.
