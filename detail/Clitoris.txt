
Clitoris: A small mass of erectile tissue in the female that is situated at the anterior apex of the vulva, near the meeting of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6190" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">labia majora</a> (vulvar lips). Like the penis, the clitoris is highly sensitive to stimulation during sex. The clitoris corresponds to the penis in the male.
