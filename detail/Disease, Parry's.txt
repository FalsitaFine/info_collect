
Disease, Parry's: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5826" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Toxic multinodular goiter</a>, a condition in which the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5778" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroid gland</a> contains multiple lumps (nodules) that are overactive
and produce excess <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5780" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroid hormones</a>. This condition is also known as Plummer's
disease.
