
Mother: (1) The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3408">female</a> parent. (2) To produce <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9558">offspring</a> as a female. To attribute the maternity of. (3) A cell or other structure from which similar cells or structures are formed. Accordingly, such a cell might be referred to as the mother cell.(4) To provide maternal protection, guidance, and nurturing to children.
