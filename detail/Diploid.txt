
Diploid: The number of chromosomes in most cells of the body. The diploid in humans is 46, which is twice the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3653" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">haploid</a> number of 23 chromosomes contained in human eggs (ova) and sperm.
