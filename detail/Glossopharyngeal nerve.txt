
Glossopharyngeal nerve: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7624" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ninth cranial nerve</a>, which supplies the tongue, throat, and one of the salivary glands (the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4785" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">parotid gland</a>). Problems with the glossopharyngeal nerve result in difficulties with tasting and 'swallowing.
