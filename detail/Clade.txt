
Clade: Related organisms descended from a common ancestor. For example, isolate M of HIV-1 (the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/human_immunodeficiency_virus_hiv/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">human immunodeficiency virus</a>) consists of at least ten clades. Imported from the Greek, klados, branch in 1911 in reference to the Tree of Life.
