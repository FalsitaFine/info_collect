
Cup, optic: The white, cup-like area in the center of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10620" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">optic disc</a>. The optic disk is the circular area at the back of the inside of the eye where the optic nerve connects to the retina.
