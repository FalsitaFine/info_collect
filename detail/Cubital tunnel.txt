
Cubital tunnel: The opening between the two heads of a muscle through which the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39992" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ulnar nerve</a> passes at the elbow and enters the forearm. (The muscle is called the flexor carpi ulnaris.) Compression of the ulnar nerve in this passageway results in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39989" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cubital tunnel syndrome</a>.
