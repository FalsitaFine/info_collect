
Morton's syndrome: A syndrome 
 characterized by a shortened first <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25071">metatarsal</a> (foot) bone 
that results in excessive force on the head of the second metatarsal (foot) bone 
and a hypermobile first metatarsal segment. The external appearance of the foot 
in Morton's syndrome is characterized by an abnormally shortened first toe in 
comparison to the second toe. Calluses are seen on the plantar surface (bottom) of the foot beneath the second and third metatarsal bones.
