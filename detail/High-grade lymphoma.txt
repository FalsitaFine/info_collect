
High-grade lymphoma: A lymphoma that grows and spreads quickly but has a better response to anticancer drugs than that seen with low-grade lymphomas. High-grade lymphomas include large cell, immunoblastic, lymphoblastic, and small noncleaved cell lymphomas. Also called an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25085" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">aggressive lymphoma</a>.
