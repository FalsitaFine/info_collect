
Foramen: A natural opening. Although a foramen is usually through bone, it can be an opening through other types of tissue, as with the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3504" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">foramen ovale</a> in the heart. The plural of foramen is foramina.
