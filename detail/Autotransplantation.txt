
Autotransplantation: The process of transplanting tissue from one part of the body to another in the same individual. The transplanted tissue is termed an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40488" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">autotransplant</a> or an autograft. Ovarian autotransplantation has been done to the upper arm to protect the ovary while the patient had radiotherapy to the abdomen.
