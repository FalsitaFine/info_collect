
Dilated cardiomyopathy: A disorder in which the chambers of the heart are dilated (enlarged) because the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9658" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">heart muscle</a> is weakened and cannot pump effectively. There are many causes, the most common being myocardial ischemia (not enough oxygen supplied to the heart muscle) due to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10267" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">coronary artery disease</a>.
