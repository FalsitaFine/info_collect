
Baker cyst: A swelling in the space behind the knee (the popliteal space) that is composed of a membrane-lined sac filled with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5686" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">synovial fluid</a> that has escaped from the joint. Also known as synovial cyst of the popliteal space.
