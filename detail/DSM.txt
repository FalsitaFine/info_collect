
DSM: Abbreviation for the "Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders," a comprehensive classification of officially recognized  
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15157">psychiatric</a> disorders, published by the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32503">American Psychiatric Association </a>,  
for use by mental health professionals to ensure uniformity of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2979">diagnosis</a>. 
DSM describes symptoms and does not discuss the causes of the disorders. DSM-IV designates the 4th edition. Issued in 1993, DSM-IV is currently the latest edition (as of 2001).
