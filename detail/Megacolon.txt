
Megacolon: An abnormally enlarged colon. Megacolon can arise as a serious congenital problem in the newborn, caused by abnormal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=34005" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">innervation</a> of the colon and frequently requiring surgery.
