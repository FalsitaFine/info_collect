
Anterior cruciate injury: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/knee_injury_and_meniscus_tears/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">knee injury</a> involving the anterior cruciate ligament (ACL). The ACL runs diagonally deep inside the knee from the center of rotation of the end of the femur (the thigh bone) to the top of the tibia (the larger bone in the lower leg).
