
Heights, fear of: An irrational unreasonable <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12320" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">fear of heights</a> is called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9944" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">acrophobia</a>. From the Greek "acron", height + "phobos", fear.
