
Chromosome 4p- syndrome: The 4p- syndrome or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20245" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Wolf-Hirschhorn syndrome</a>, is a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2725" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chromosome disorder</a> due to partial deletion of the short (p) arm of chromosome 4. It is, therefore, also called the 4p- syndrome.
