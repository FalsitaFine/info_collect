
Hormone, parathormone: Hormone made by the parathyroid
gland (behind the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5778" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroid gland</a> in the neck). <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4771" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Parathormone</a> (pronounced <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7885" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">para-</a>thor-mone) is
critical to calcium and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4880" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">phosphorus</a> balance. Deficiency of parathormone results in
abnormally low calcium in the blood (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3855" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hypocalcemia</a>). Also call <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4772" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">parathyrin</a>.
