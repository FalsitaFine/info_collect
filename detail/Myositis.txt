
Myositis: Inflammation of muscle tissue. There are many causes of myositis, including injury, medications, and diseases, such as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2955" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">dermatomyositis</a>. Myositis may require no treatment, stopping medications, or treatment of an underlying disease, if present.
