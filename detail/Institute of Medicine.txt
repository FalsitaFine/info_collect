
Institute of Medicine: A nonprofit organization established in 1970 as a component of the US <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18440" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">National Academy of Sciences</a> that works outside the framework of government to provide evidence-based research and recommendations for public health and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13754" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">science</a> policy. Abbreviated IOM. The IOM is also an honorific membership organization.
