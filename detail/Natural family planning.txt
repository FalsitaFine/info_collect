
Natural family planning: Also known as fertility awareness, periodic abstinence and the rhythm method, this approach entails not having sexual intercourse on the days of a woman's menstrual cycle when she could become pregnant or using a barrier method (such as a condom, the diaphragm or a cervical cap) for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/birth_control_methods/article.htm">birth control</a> on those days.
