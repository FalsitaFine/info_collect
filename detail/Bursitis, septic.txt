
Bursitis, septic: Inflammation of a bursa due to infection, usually with bacteria. Septic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/acute_and_chronic_bursitis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">bursitis</a> is treated with antibiotics, aspiration, and surgery. Also known as infectious bursitis.
