
Bypass, cardiopulmonary: A bypass of the heart and lungs as, for example, in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=19211" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">open heart surgery</a>. In this procedure, blood returning to the heart is diverted through a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6414" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">heart-lung machine</a> (a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/urinary_tract_infection/article.htm2" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pump-oxygenator</a>) before being returned to the arterial <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2735" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">circulation</a>.
