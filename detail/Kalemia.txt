
Kalemia: The presence of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9970">potassium</a> in the blood. The word "kalemia" is seldom used today although it is the basis for the  terms for abnormally high blood potassium (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3837">hyperkalemia</a>) and low blood potassium (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3857">hypokalemia</a>). See also: Potassium balance.
