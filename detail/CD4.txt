
CD4: A large <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16842">glycoprotein</a> molecule found on the surface of T lymphocytes that serves as the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5236">receptor</a> for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3769">HIV</a>. The CD4 gene is on chromosome 12 in region 12pter-p12. CD4 is also called T4. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2100">Absolute CD4 count</a>.
