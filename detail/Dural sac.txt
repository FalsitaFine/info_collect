
Dural sac: The membranous sac that encases the spinal cord within the bony structure of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11547" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">vertebral column</a>. Dural refers to the dura, the name of the membrane around the spinal cord (and brain, too).
