
Cascade: A sequence of successive activation reactions involving enzymes (enzyme cascade) or hormones (hormone cascade) characterized by a series of amplifications of an initial stimulus. In blood coagulation, for example, each enzyme activates the next until the final product, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3450" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">fibrin</a> clot, is reached.
