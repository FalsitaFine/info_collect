
Chorioamnionitis: Inflammation of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2719" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chorion</a> and the amnion, the membranes that surround the fetus. Chorioamnionitis usually is associated with a bacterial infection. This may be due to bacteria ascending from the mother's genital tract into the uterus to infect the membranes and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2226" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">amniotic fluid</a>. Chorioamnionitis is dangerous to the mother and child.
