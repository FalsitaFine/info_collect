
Bronchiectasis: Permanent abnormal widening of the bronchi (air tubes that branch deep into the lungs). <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/bronchiectasis_acquired_congenital/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Bronchiectasis</a> can cause recurrent lung infections, a disabling cough, shortness of breath, and coughing up blood.
