
Bilharziasis: a parasite infection by a trematode worm  acquired from infested water. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/schistosomiasis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">schistosomiasis</a>. Species which live in man can  produce liver, bladder, and gastrointestinal problems. Species of the schistosomiasis  parasite which cannot live in man cause swimmer's itch.
