
Gland, sudoriferous: The sudoriferous (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9298">sweat</a>) glands are small 
tubular structures situated within and under the skin (in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8265">subcutaneous</a> tissue). 
They discharge sweat by tiny openings in the surface of the skin.
