
Morton's neuroma: A swollen inflamed <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4537">nerve</a> in the ball of 
the foot, usually between the base of the second and third toes (known as the 
third intermetatarsal space), caused by chronic compression of a branch of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4932">plantar</a> nerve between the ends of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25071">metatarsal</a> bones. The disorder is especially common in women who wear high-heeled and/or narrow shoes.
