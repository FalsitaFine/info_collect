
MAB: Or mab. Abbreviation for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4425">monoclonal antibody</a>. At the end of a generic drug name, -mab indicates that the drug is a monoclonal antibody. As in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/adalimumab/article.htm">adalimumab</a>, bevacizumab, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/infliximab/article.htm">infliximab</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rituximab/article.htm">rituximab</a>, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/trastuzumab/article.htm">trastuzumab</a>.
