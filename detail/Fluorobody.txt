
Fluorobody: A hybrid molecule that combines the affinity and specificity of an antibody with the visibility of a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40501" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">green fluorescent protein</a> (GFP). The GSP provides the antibody with a built-in detection system. The creation of the fluorobody in the laboratory was first reported in 2003.
