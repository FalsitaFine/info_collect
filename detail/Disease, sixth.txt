
Disease, sixth: A viral disease of infants and young children with sudden onset of high <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a> which lasts several days and
then suddenly subsides leaving in its wake a fine red <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">rash</a>. The causative agent is herpesvirus type 6, so the disease is known as Sixth Disease. Also known as Exanthem subitum (sudden rash), Pseudorubella, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/roseola/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Roseola</a>, Roseola infantilis, and Roseola infantum.
