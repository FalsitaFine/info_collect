
Echopraxia: The involuntary imitation of the movements of another person. Echopraxia is a feature of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/schizophrenia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">schizophrenia</a> (especially the catatonic form), <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tourette_syndrome/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Tourette syndrome</a>, and some other neurologic diseases. From echo + the Greek praxia meaning action.
