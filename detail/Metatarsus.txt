
Metatarsus: The part of the foot that lies just beyond the tarsus, between the tarsus and the toes. The skeleton of the metatarsus consists of five long bones termed the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4365" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">metatarsals</a> that extend from the tarsus to the phalanges. From the Greek meta-, beyond + tarsos, the tarsus = beyond the tarsus.
