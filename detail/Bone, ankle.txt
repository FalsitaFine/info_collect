
Bone, ankle: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10311" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ankle bone</a> is termed the talus. It is the bone of the foot that joins the tibia and fibula to form the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9058" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ankle joint</a>. Plural tali.
