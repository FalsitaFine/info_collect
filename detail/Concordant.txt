
Concordant: 1.  Showing <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38929" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">concordance</a>, complete agreement.  A couple may be concordant for a sexually transmitted infection, with both partners having it.   2. In <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18549" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">transplantation genetics</a>, between closely related species. A concordant <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10739" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">xenograft</a> is a transplant between members of closely related species.
