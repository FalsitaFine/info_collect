
Familial Parkinson disease type 2: A familial form of Parkinson disease inherited in an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15971" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">autosomal recessive</a> manner due to mutation in the gene encoding parkin on chromosome 6q25.2-q27. Also known as PARK2.
