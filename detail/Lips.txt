
Lips: Aside from the lips of the mouth, there are two pairs of lips at the entrance to the vagina. They are the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6190" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">labia majora</a> (the larger outside pair) and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6191" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">labia minora</a> (the smaller inside pair). Together they form part of the vulva (the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20894" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">female external genitalia</a>).
