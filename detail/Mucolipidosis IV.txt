
Mucolipidosis IV: A type of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11680" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">mucolipidosis</a> that is due to mutation in the gene that encoded mucolipin-1. Most patients with mucolipidosis IV have <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2965" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">developmental delay</a>, mental retardation, clouding of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7248" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cornea</a> of the eye, and severe visual impairment.
