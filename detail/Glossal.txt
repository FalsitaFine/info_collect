
Glossal: Of or pertaining to the tongue. Glossal is used as both an adjective and a compound word, as in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7652" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hypoglossal nerve</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5777" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroglossal cyst</a>. From the Greek glossa meaning tongue.
