
Actinomycetes: A group of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12095" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">gram</a>-positive bacteria (order Actinomycetales)  that produce various bioactive agents including antibiotics, enzymes, and vitamins. Streptomyces belong to this bacterial order.
