
Cephalothoracic lipodystrophy: A disorder characterized by painless symmetrical diffuse deposits of fat beneath the skin of the neck, upper trunk, arms, and legs. The condition is genetic and is inherited as an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11974" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">autosomal dominant</a> trait. Also known as multiple symmetrical lipomatosis, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9397" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Launois-Bensaude syndrome</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9399" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Madelung disease</a>, and familial benign cervical lipomatosis.
