
Magnesia: Named after a town in present day Turkey where an ore
containing <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4243">magnesium</a> carbonate was mined. Milk of Magnesia, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6465">laxative</a>, is magnesium
hydroxide.
