
Arachnoiditis: Inflammation of the middle layer of membranes covering the brain and spinal cord. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/arachnoiditis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Arachnoiditis</a> can occur as a complication of procedures such as myelograms, spinal operations, epidural steroid injections, and injury to the spine.
