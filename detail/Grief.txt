
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/loss_grief_and_bereavement/article.htm">Grief:</a> The normal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5054">process</a> of reacting to a loss. The loss may be physical (such as a death), social (such as divorce), or occupational (such as a job). Emotional reactions of grief can include anger, guilt, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9947">anxiety</a>, sadness, and despair. Physical reactions of grief can include sleeping problems, changes in appetite, physical problems, or illness.
