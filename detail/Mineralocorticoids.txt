
Mineralocorticoids: A group of hormones (the most important being
aldosterone) that regulate the balance of water and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/electrolytes/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">electrolytes</a> (ions such as sodium and
potassium) in the body. The mineralocorticoid hormones act on the kidney (and specifically
on the tubules of the kidney).
