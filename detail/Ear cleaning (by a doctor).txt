
Ear cleaning (by a doctor): When <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/ear_wax/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">ear wax</a> accumulates enough,  it may block the ear canal (and hearing). A health care professional may have to wash it out, vacuum it, or remove it with special instruments. Alternatively, your physician may prescribe ear drops that are designed to soften the wax.
