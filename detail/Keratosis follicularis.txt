
Keratosis follicularis: A genetic skin disease that is characterized by slowly progressive hardening of the skin (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4098" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">keratosis</a>) around the hair follicles. This disorder is inherited in an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11974" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">autosomal dominant</a> manner and is due to mutation in a gene called ATP2A2 on chromosome 12. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=19449" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Darier disease</a>.
