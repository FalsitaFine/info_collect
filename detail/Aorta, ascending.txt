
Aorta, ascending: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8638" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ascending aorta</a> is the first section of the aorta, the largest artery in the body. The ascending aorta starts from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9127" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">left ventricle</a> of the heart and extends to the arch (the bend) of the aorta.
