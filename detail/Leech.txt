
Leech: 1. An aquatic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4769">parasite</a> that sucks blood from animals. Leeches play a role in medicine today. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33718">Leech therapy</a>.  
2. (Archaic) A physician or surgeon (because they did so much leeching). 3. A person who behaves as a parasite.
