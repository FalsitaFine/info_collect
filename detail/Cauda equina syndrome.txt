
Cauda equina syndrome: Impairment of the nerves in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7239" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cauda equina</a>, characterized by dull pain in the lower back and upper buttocks and lack of feeling (analgesia) in the buttocks, genitalia, and thigh, together with disturbances of bowel and bladder function.
