
Infraspinatus muscle: A muscle that assists the lifting of the arm while turning the arm outward (external rotation). The tendon of the infraspinatus muscle is one of four tendons that stabilize the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9059" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">shoulder joint</a> and constitute the rotator cuff.
