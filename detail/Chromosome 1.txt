
Chromosome 1: The largest human chromosome,  comprising approximately 10% of the human genome. Chromosome 1 contains over 240 million base pairs and over 3000 genes including the UROD gene for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/porphyria/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">porphyria</a> cutanea tarda, the GBA gene for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/gaucher_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Gaucher disease</a>, the GLC1A gene for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/glaucoma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">glaucoma</a>, the HPC1 gene for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/prostate_cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">prostate cancer</a>, and the PS2 gene for Alzheimer disease.
