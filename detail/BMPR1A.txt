
BMPR1A: A gene located on chromosome 10 in band 10q22.3 that encodes bone morphogenic protein receptor 1A, a kinase receptor that is involved in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16238" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">bone morphogenetic protein</a> (BMP) signaling. Mutations in BMPR1A account for 25% to 40% of cases of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38838" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">juvenile polyposis</a>, an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11974" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">autosomal dominant</a> gastrointestinal polyposis syndrome in which patients are at risk for developing gastrointestinal cancers.
