
Microcytic: Literally, referring to any abnormally small cell; in practice, referring to an abnormally small red blood cell. For example, microcytic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anemia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anemia</a> is characterized by small red blood cells. The opposite of microcytic is macrocytic.
