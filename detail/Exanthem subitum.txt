
Exanthem subitum: Means a sudden <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm">rash</a>. A
viral disease of infants and young children with sudden onset of high <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm">fever</a> which lasts
several days and then suddenly subsides leaving in its wake a fine red <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rash/article.htm">rash</a>. The causative
agent is herpesvirus type 6 so the disease is known as Sixth Disease. Also called
Pseudorubella, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/roseola/article.htm">Roseola</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/roseola/article.htm">Roseola</a> infantilis, and Roseola infantum.
