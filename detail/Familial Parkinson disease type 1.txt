
Familial Parkinson disease type 1: A familial form of Parkinson disease inherited in an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11974" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">autosomal dominant</a> manner due to mutation in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25040" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">alpha-synuclein</a> gene (SNCA) on chromosome 4q21. Also known as PARK1.
