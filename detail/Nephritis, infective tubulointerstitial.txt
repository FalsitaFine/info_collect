
Nephritis, infective tubulointerstitial: Inflammation of the kidney that is due to infection. Symptoms include nausea, pain in the kidney area, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aches_pain_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fever</a>, and chills. Early diagnosis is essential to save the kidneys. Treatment involves use of antibiotics or antiviral medications.
