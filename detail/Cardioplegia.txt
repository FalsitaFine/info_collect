
Cardioplegia: Paralysis of the heart, as may be done electively in stopping the heart during cardiac surgery. Cardioplegia may be done using chemicals, cold (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25980" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cryocardioplegia</a>), or electrical stimulation.
