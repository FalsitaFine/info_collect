
Larynx transplant: A transplant of the larynx, or voicebox. This procedure permits a human-sounding voice with inflection, range, and qualities unique to the patient, and normal swallowing. The risks of the procedure include those of organ rejection and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3942" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immunosuppression</a> therapy.
