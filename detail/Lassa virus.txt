
Lassa virus: The deadly cause of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/lassa_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Lassa fever</a>. Lassa virus is a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6912">virulent</a> and highly transmissible RNA virus that belongs to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=26655">arenavirus</a> group. Lassa virus was discovered in 1969 by a team led by Dr. Jordi Casals-Ariet.
