
Inheritance, Y-linked: Inheritance of genes on the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6049" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Y chromosome</a>. Because normally only males have Y chromosomes, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6059" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Y-linked</a> genes can be transmitted only from father to son. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8900" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">holandric inheritance</a>.
