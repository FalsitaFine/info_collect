
Fecal incontinence: Inability to hold <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3400">feces</a> in the
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5253">rectum</a>. This is due to failure of voluntary control over the anal sphincters permitting untimely passage of feces and gas. Also called rectal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3958">incontinence</a>.
