
Barotrauma: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8171">Trauma</a> caused by rapid or extreme changes in air pressure, especially affecting enclosed cavities within the body such as the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7867">middle ear</a> (otic barotrauma), the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22702">sinuses</a>  (sinus barotrauma), and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4209">lungs</a> (pulmonary barotrauma). 
See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4691">Otic barotrauma</a>; <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5496">Sinus barotrauma</a>; and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=31723">Pulmonary barotrauma</a>.
