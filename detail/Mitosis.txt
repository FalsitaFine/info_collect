
Mitosis: The ordinary division of a body cell (a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18253" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">somatic cell</a>) to form two daughter cells, each with the same <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2724" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chromosome complement</a> as the parent cell.
