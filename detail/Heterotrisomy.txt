
Heterotrisomy: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38317">Trisomy</a> of different <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2723">chromosomes</a>. The occurrence of two children in the same family, one with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/trisomy_18_edwards_syndrome/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="DT">trisomy 18</a> and the other with trisomy 21, is an instance of heterotrisomy.
