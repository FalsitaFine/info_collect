
Eicosapentaenoic acid: One of the principal omega-3 fatty acids. Abbreviated EPA. The body has a limited ability to manufacture EPA by converting the essential fatty acid, alpha-linolenic acid (ALA) which is found in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/flaxseed_linum_usitatissimum-oral/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">flaxseed</a> oil, canola oil or walnuts.
