
Lyonization: The inactivation of an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6030" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">X chromosome</a>. One of the two
X chromosomes in every cell in a female is randomly inactivated early in embryonic
development. Named after geneticist Mary Lyon.
