
Aniso-: Unequal, unlike, or dissimilar. As in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7034" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">anisocoria</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25450" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">anisocytosis</a>, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20150" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">anisometropia</a>. From the Greek anisos meaning unequal, from an-, not, + isos, equal. The opposite of aniso- is iso-.
