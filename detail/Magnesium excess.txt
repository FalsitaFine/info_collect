
Magnesium excess: Persons with 
impaired kidney function should be especially careful about their <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4243">magnesium</a> intake because they can accumulate magnesium, a dangerous situation. According to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18440">National Academy of Sciences</a>, the Recommended Dietary Allowances of magnesium are 420 milligrams 
per day for men and 320 milligrams per day for women. The upper limit of 
magnesium as supplements is 350 milligrams daily, in addition to the magnesium 
from food and water.
