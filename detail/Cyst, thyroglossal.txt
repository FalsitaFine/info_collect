
Cyst, thyroglossal: A fluid-filled sac that is present at birth and located in the midline of the neck. A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5777" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroglossal cyst</a> is a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2820" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">congenital malformation</a> (a birth defect). It results from incomplete closure of a segment of the thyroglossal duct, a tube-like structure that normally closes as the embryo develops. Also called a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyroglossal duct cyst</a> or a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5789" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thyrolingual cyst</a>.
