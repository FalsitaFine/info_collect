
Abdominal: Relating to the abdomen, the belly, that part of the body that contains all of the structures between the chest and the pelvis. The abdomen is separated anatomically from the chest by the diaphragm, the powerful muscle spanning the body cavity below the lungs.   The abdomen includes a host of organs including the stomach,  small intestine, colon, rectum, liver, spleen, pancreas,  kidneys, appendix,    gallbladder, and bladder.   The word "abdomen"  comes   from the Latin "abdodere", to hide.
