
Bergamot oil: An aromatic extract of the rind of the bergamot orange used to flavor Earl Grey tea and in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15655" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">aromatherapy</a>. It causes photosensitivity, due largely to the presence of 5-methoxypsoralen. Drinking very large amounts of Earl Grey tea can block the intestinal absorption of potassium.
