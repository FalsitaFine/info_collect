
Malformation, arteriovenous (AVM): An <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/arteriovenous_malformation/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">arteriovenous malformation</a> (AVM) is a congenital disorder (one present at birth) of blood vessels in the brain, brainstem, or spinal cord that is characterized by a complex, tangled web of abnormal arteries and veins connected by one or more fistulas (abnormal communications).
