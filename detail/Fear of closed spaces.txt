
Fear of closed spaces: Fear of closed spaces, of being closed in or being shut in as in elevators, tunnels, or any other small confined space is termed <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9951" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">claustrophobia</a>.  The word "claustrophobia" is derived from the Latin "claudere", to shut + the Greek "phobis", fear.
