
Nerve, facial: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7518" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">facial nerve</a> is the seventh cranial nerve. It is a mixed nerve that has fibers both going out and coming in (both efferent and afferent fibers). It supplies the muscles of facial expression.
