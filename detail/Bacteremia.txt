
Bacteremia: The presence of live <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13954">bacteria</a> in the 
bloodstream. Bacteremia is analogous to viremia (the presence of a virus in the blood) and parasitemia (the presence of a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4769">parasite</a> in the blood). Bacteremia, viremia and parasitemia are all forms of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/sepsis/article.htm">sepsis</a> (bloodstream infection). The term "bacteremia" was compounded from "bacteria" and "-emia" (in the blood). Also called bacillemia.
