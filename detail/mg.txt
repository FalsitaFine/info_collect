
mg: Abbreviation for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12079">milligram</a>, a unit of measurement of mass in the metric system equal to a thousandth of a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12095">gram</a>. A gram is equal to the mass of one milliliter, one thousandth of a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12127">liter</a>, of water at 4 degrees C.
MG (in capital letters) is the abbreviation for the disease <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4473">myasthenia gravis</a>.
