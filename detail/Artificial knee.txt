
Artificial knee: A replacement for the human knee. An artificial knee typically has a metal shell on the end of the thigh bone (the femur), a metal and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/plastic/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">plastic</a> trough on the shin bone (the tibia), and sometimes a plastic button in the kneecap (the patella).
