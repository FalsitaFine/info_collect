
Anti-: Prefix generally meaning "against, opposite or opposing, and contrary." In medicine, anti- often  connotes "counteracting or effective against" as in antibacterial, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2285" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">anti-infective</a>, and antiviral. Sometimes medical terms containing anti- take on new meanings as has occurred with antibiotic and antibody. As a prefix, anti- may be shortened to ant- as in antacid.
"Anti" is the Greek word for "against."
