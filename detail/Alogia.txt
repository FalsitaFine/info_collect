
Alogia: 1. Complete lack of speech, as in profound mental retardation or advanced <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/dementia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">dementia</a>. Alogia is synonymous in this sense with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/aphasia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">aphasia</a>. <br> 2. Poverty of speech, as commonly occurs in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/schizophrenia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">schizophrenia</a>.<br>
From the Greek a-, without + logos, speech.</br></br>
