
Bronchospasm, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/exercise/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">exercise</a>-induced:  Also called <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/exercise-induced_asthma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">exercise-induced asthma</a>, this is asthma that is triggered by vigorous physical activity. Exercise-induced asthma tends particularly to affect children and young adults (because of their high level of physical activity) but can occur at any age.
