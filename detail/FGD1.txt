
FGD1: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10442" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">faciodigitogenital dysplasia</a> gene.  FGD1 normally plays an essential role in embryonic development. Mutations in FGD1 result in faciodigitogenital <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3142" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">dysplasia</a>, which is also called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10440" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Aarskog-Scott syndrome</a>.
