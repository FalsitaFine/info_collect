
Culdocentesis: The puncture and aspiration (withdrawal) of fluid from the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8463" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">rectouterine pouch</a> (the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8502" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pouch of Douglas</a>), an extension of the peritoneal cavity between the rectum and back wall of the uterus.
