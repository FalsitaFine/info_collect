
Encephalitis, Nipah virus: Brain inflammation (encephalitis) due to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9272" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Nipah virus</a> that infects pigs and people. Nipah is the name of the first village the virus struck near Kuala Lumpur in Malaysia. (The Nipah virus is similar to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/liver_anatomy_and_function/article.htm67" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Hendra virus</a> that was discovered in Australia in 1994.) Nipah virus caused a severe outbreak of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=16594" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">viral encephalitis</a> in Malaysia in 1998-1999.
