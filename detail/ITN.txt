
ITN: Insecticide-treated bednet. "Provision of insecticide-treated bednets (ITNs) is universally accepted as an efficacious and essential public health service in most parts of sub-Saharan Africa <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3234">endemic</a> for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4255">malaria</a>." (H Guyatt and S Ochola, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6209">Lancet</a> 2003;362:1549)  See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=32139">Bednet</a>.
