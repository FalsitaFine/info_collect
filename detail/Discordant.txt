
Discordant: 1.  Showing <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38930" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">discordance</a>, lack of agreement.  A couple may be discordant for a sexually transmitted infection, with one partner having it and the other not.   2. In <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18549" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">transplantation genetics</a>, between different species. A discordant <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10739" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">xenograft</a> is a transplant between members of very different species.
