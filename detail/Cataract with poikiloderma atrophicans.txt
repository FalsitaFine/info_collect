
Cataract with poikiloderma atrophicans: Also known as the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/rothmund-thomson_syndrome/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Rothmund-Thomson syndrome</a>, this is an hereditary disease characterized by progressive degeneration (atrophy), scarring and abnormal pigmentation of the skin together with stunting of growth,  baldness, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cataracts/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cataracts</a>, depressed nasal bridge, and malformations of the teeth, nails and bone.
