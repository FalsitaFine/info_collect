
Click-murmur syndrome: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/mitral_valve_prolapse/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Mitral valve prolapse</a> (also known as "Barlow's syndrome"), the most common heart valve abnormality, affecting 5% to 10% of the world population. Most patients have no symptoms and require no treatment, but some have <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/fatigue/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">fatigue</a> and/or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/palpitations_overview/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">palpitations</a>. The mitral valve prolapse can often be detected by a doctor during examination of the heart and confirmed with an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/echocardiogram/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">echocardiogram</a>.
