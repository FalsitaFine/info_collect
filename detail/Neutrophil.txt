
Neutrophil: A type of white blood cell, a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8780" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">granulocyte</a> that is filled with microscopic granules, little sacs containing enzymes that digest microorganisms. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4993" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">polymorphonuclear leukocyte</a> or poly.
