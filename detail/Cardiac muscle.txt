
Cardiac muscle: A type of muscle tissue that is found only in the heart and is distinguishable from the two other forms of muscle, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5514" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">smooth muscle</a> (that moves internal organs, such as the bowels, and vessels, such as the artery walls) and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5502" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">skeletal muscle</a> (that powers joints). Cardiac muscle is responsible for pumping blood throughout the body.
