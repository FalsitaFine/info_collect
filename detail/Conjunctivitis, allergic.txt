
Conjunctivitis, allergic: Inflammation of the whites of the eyes (the conjunctivae), with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/itch/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">itching</a>, redness, and tearing, that is caused by an allergic reaction and frequently accompanied by <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hay_fever/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hay fever</a>.
