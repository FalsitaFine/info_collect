
Choana: An opening at the back of the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9072" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">nasal passage</a> (there is a left and a right side) that empties into the space behind the nose called the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.comhttps://www.medicinenet.com/pneumonia_facts/article.htm7" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">nasopharynx</a>, where the adenoids and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8525" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">eustachian tube</a> are. The passage way continues down into the back of the mouth and into the throat.
