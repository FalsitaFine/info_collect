
Arrhythmias, ventricular: Abnormal rapid heart rhythms
(arrhythmias) that originate in the lower chambers of the heart (the ventricles).
Ventricular arrhythmias include ventricular tachycardia and ventricular fibrillation. Both
are life threatening arrhythmias most commonly associated with heart attacks or scarring
of the heart muscle from previous <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/heart_attack/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">heart attack</a>.
