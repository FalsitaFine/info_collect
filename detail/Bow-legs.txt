
Bow-legs: A condition in which the legs curve out to leave a gap between the knees after the period of infancy has passed. It can be corrected with surgery or casting. Also called genu <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40003" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">varum</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11325" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">tibia vara</a>.
