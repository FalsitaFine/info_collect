
Belching: A normal process of releasing through the mouth air that accumulates in the stomach, thereby relieving <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13145" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">distention</a>. Upper abdominal discomfort associated with excessive swallowed air may extend into the lower chest, producing symptoms that suggest heart or lung disease.
