
In-the-ear hearing aid: A hearing instrument in a custom-made shell designed to fit in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7883">outer ear</a> and ear canal. An in-the-ear (ITE) aid may be suitable for mild to moderately severe hearing loss but not for infants or young children. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22561">Hearing aid</a>.
