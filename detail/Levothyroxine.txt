
Levothyroxine: A synthetic thyroid hormone used as a thyroid hormone replacement drug (brand names include  Levothroid, Levoxine, Levoxyl, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/levothyroxine_sodium/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="PHARM">Synthroid</a>) used to treat an underactive thyroid gland (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hypothyroidism/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hypothyroidism</a>).
