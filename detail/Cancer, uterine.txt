
Cancer, uterine: A malignant <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a> of the uterus (womb), which occurs most often in women between the ages of 55 and 70. Abnormal bleeding after <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/menopause/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">menopause</a> is the most common symptom. <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Cancer</a> of the uterus is diagnosed based on the results of a pelvic examination, Pap test, biopsy of the uterus, and/or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/dilation_and_curettage/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">dilation and curettage</a> (D &amp; C).
