
Cranial: 1. Pertaining to the cranium or skull. 
2. Toward the head. As opposed to caudad. The eye is cranial to the jaw. For a more complete listing of terms used in medicine for spatial orientation, please see the entry to "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9210">Anatomic Orientation Terms</a>".
