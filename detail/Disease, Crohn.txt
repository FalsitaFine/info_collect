
Disease, Crohn: A chronic inflammatory disease primarily involving the small and large intestine but which can affect other parts of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/the_digestion_process_organs_and_functions/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">the digestive system</a>. It is named for Burrill Crohn, the American gastroenterologist who first described the disease in 1932.
