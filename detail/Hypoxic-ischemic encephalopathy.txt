
Hypoxic-ischemic encephalopathy: Damage to cells in the central  nervous system (the brain and spinal cord) from inadequate oxygen. Hypoxic-ischemic  <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/encephalopathy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">encephalopathy</a> allegedly may cause death in the newborn period or result in what is  later recognized as developmental delay, mental retardation, or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cerebral_palsy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cerebral palsy</a>. This is an  area of considerable medical and medicolegal debate.
