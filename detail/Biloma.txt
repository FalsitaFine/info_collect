
Biloma: An encapsulated collection of bile within the abdomen. A biloma may form if there is bile duct disruption, as from a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cholecystectomy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">laparoscopic cholecystectomy</a>. From bile + -oma (a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a>).
