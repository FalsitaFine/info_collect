
History, social: An account of a patient that puts his or her illness or behavior in context. A social history may include aspects of the patient's developmental, family, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3768" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">medical history</a>, as well as relevant information about life events, social class, race, religion, and occupation.
