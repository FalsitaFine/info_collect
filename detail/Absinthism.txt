
Absinthism:  The disorder associated with the habitual abuse of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7485">absinthe</a>. The symptoms included hallucinations, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22519">sleeplessness</a>, tremors, and convulsions. There has been debate over whether absinthism was due to absinthe or the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20078">alcohol</a> contained therein. For more information, see: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7485">Absinthe</a>.
