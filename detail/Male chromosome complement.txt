
Male chromosome complement: The whole set of chromosomes for a human male. The large majority of males have a 46,XY <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2724" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chromosome complement</a>: 46 chromosomes, including 1 X and 1 <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6049" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Y chromosome</a>. A minority of males have other chromosome constitutions, such as 47,XXY (47 chromosomes, including 2 X chromosomes and 1 Y chromosome) or 47,XYY (47 chromosomes, including 1 X and 2 Y chromosomes).
