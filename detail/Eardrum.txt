
Eardrum: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5871" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">tympanic membrane</a> of the ear, or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5873" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">tympanum</a>. The eardrum separates the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7867" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">middle ear</a> from the external ear.
