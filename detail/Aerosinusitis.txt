
Aerosinusitis: Painful sinus troubles due to changing atmospheric pressures. Aerosinusitis is the cause of sinus pain when going up or down in a plane. Also known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2429" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">barosinusitis</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5496" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">sinus barotrauma</a>.
