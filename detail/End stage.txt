
End stage: The last phase in the course of a progressive disease. As in end-stage <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/liver_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">liver disease</a>, end-stage lung disease, end-stage renal disease, end-stage <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a>, etc. The term "end stage" has come to replace "terminal".
