
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/dehydration/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Dehydration</a>: Excessive loss of body water. Diseases of the gastrointestinal tract that cause vomiting or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diarrhea/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">diarrhea</a> may lead to dehydration. There are a number of other causes of dehydration including heat exposure, prolonged vigorous <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/exercise/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">exercise</a>, kidney disease, and medications that cause voiding (diuretics).
