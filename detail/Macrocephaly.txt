
Macrocephaly: An abnormally large head. The opposite of macrocephaly is <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/microcephaly/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">microcephaly</a>. Macrocephaly can be a normal variant or be a sign of pressure within the growing head during childhood, such as from <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hydrocephalus/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">hydrocephalus</a>.
