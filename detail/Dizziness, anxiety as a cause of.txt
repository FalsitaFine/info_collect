
Dizziness, anxiety as a cause of: One cause of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/dizziness_dizzy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">dizziness</a> is over breathing (hyperventilation) due to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anxiety/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anxiety</a>. The over breathing also causes lightheadedness, a sense of unsteadiness and tingling around the mouth and fingertips.
