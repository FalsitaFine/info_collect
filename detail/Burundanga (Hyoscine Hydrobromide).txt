
Burundanga (Hyoscine Hydrobromide): a slang term used to refer to the drug <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/scopolamine/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">scopolamine</a>. Scopolamine is an oral, intravenous ophthalmic or topical drug with many uses, including the prevention of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/motion_sickness_sea_sickness_car_sickness/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">motion sickness</a>.
