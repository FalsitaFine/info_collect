
Dacryo-: A combining form denoting tears, as in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25273" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">dacryocyst</a> (tear sac) and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25270" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">dacryocystorhinostomy</a> (surgery to open up a tear duct). From the Greek dakry meaning "a tear."
