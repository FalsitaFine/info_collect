
Eukaryote: An organism that consists of one or more cells each of which has a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4597">nucleus</a> and other well-developed <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24383">intracellular</a> compartments. Eukaryotes include all organisms except <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13954">bacteria</a>, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6000">viruses</a>, and certain (blue-green) algae which, by contrast, are prokaryotes. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5062">Prokaryote</a>.
