
Brachy-: Prefix indicating short, as in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13274" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">brachycephaly</a> (short head) and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13275" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">brachydactyly</a> (short fingers and toes).
