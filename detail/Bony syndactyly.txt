
Bony syndactyly: A condition in which the bones of the fingers or toes are joined together. Bony syndactyly is not the same as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6769" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cutaneous syndactyly</a>, which only involves webbing of the skin between the digits.
