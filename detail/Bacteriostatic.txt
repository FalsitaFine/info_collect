
Bacteriostatic:  Capable of inhibiting the growth or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5315">reproduction</a> of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13954">bacteria</a>. Different from <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11030">bactericidal</a> (capable of killing bacteria outright).
