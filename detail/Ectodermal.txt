
Ectodermal: Pertaining to the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20835" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ectoderm</a> or to tissues derived from the ectoderm, one of the three primary <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8591" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">germ cell</a> layers that make up the very early embryo. The ectoderm is the outermost of the three layers.
