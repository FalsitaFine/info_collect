
Inconclusive clinical trial: A clinical trial that shows that a new treatment is  neither clearly superior nor clearly inferior to standard treatment. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2752">Clinical trials</a>. See also:
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39074">Negative clinical trial</a>;
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39071">Non-inferior clinical trial</a>;
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39070">Positive clinical trial</a>.
