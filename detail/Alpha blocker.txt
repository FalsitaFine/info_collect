
Alpha blocker: A drug that blocks receptors in arteries and smooth muscle. This action relaxes the blood vessels and leads to an increase in blood flow and a lower pressure for the control of  hypertension. The action in the urinary tract enhances urinary flow in prostatic hypertrophy (enlarged prostate).
