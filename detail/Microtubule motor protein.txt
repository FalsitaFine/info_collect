
Microtubule motor protein:   A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6554">protein</a> that plays a role in spindle assembly and function or in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=14018">chromosome</a> movement during <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4335">meiosis</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4404">mitosis</a>. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=30959">Dynein</a>; and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=30958">Kinesin</a>.
