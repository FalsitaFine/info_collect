
Ketone: A chemical substances that the body makes when it does not have enough <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3989">insulin</a> in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2483">blood</a>. When ketones build up in the body for a long time, serious illness or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2803">coma</a> can result. 
See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39573">Diabetic ketoacidosis</a>; <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4102">Ketoacidosis</a>.
