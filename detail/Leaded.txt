
Leaded: 1. Framed, covered, or weighted with lead. As a leaded window. 2. Added in the form of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40211">tetraethyl lead</a> as an anti-knock agent to gasoline (petrol).
