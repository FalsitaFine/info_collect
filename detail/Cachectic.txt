
Cachectic: Having cachexia, physical wasting with loss of weight and muscle mass due to disease. Patients with advanced <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a>, AIDS, severe <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/heart_failure/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">heart failure</a> and some other major chronic progressive diseases may appear cachectic.
