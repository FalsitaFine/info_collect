
Lumbar vertebrae: The five vertebrae situated between the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8765" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thoracic vertebrae</a> and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8786" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">sacral vertebrae</a> in the spinal column. The lumbar vertebrae are represented by the symbols L1 through L5.
