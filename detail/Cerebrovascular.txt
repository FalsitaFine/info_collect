
Cerebrovascular: Pertaining to the blood vessels and, especially, the arteries that supply the brain. As in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2677" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cerebrovascular accident</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40116" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cerebrovascular disease</a>.
