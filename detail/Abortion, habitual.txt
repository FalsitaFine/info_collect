
Abortion, habitual: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/miscarriage_causes_and_symptoms/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">miscarriage</a> of three or more consecutive pregnancies with no intervening pregnancies. Habitual abortion is a form of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/infertility/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">infertility</a>. Also known as recurrent abortion and multiple abortion.
