
Circum-: Prefix meaning around, surrounding, or encircling. As in circumcision, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23419" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">circumflex</a>, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=40138" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">circumjacent</a>. From the Latin preposition circum meaning round.
