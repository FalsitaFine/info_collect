
Lumbar: Referring to the five <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8769" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">lumbar vertebrae</a>, the disks below them, and the corresponding area of the lower back. The lumbar vertebrae and their disks are situated below the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8765" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">thoracic vertebrae</a> and above the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8786" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">sacral vertebrae</a> in the spinal column and are surrounded by soft tissues, including ligaments and large muscles.
