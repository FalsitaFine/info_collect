
Complex partial seizure:  A form of partial <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5442">seizure</a> during which the person loses awareness. The patient does not actually become unconscious, and he or she may carry out actions as complex as walking, talking, or driving. The patient may have physical, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=15733">sensory</a>, and thought disturbances. When the seizure ends, the patient has no memory of those actions. See also: Partial seizure.
