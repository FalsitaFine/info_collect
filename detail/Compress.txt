
Compress: Cloth or another material applied under pressure to an area of the skin and held in place for a period of time. A compress can be any temperature, and it can be dry or wet. It may also be impregnated with medication or an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11464" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">herbal remedy</a>. Most compresses are used to relieve inflammation.
