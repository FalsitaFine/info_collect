
Nucleocapsid: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3580">genome</a> + the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6554">protein</a> coat of a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5997">virus</a>. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4594">nucleic acid</a> (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5382">RNA</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3090">DNA</a>) of the virus is its genome. The protein coat is its capsid. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24448">Capsid</a>.
