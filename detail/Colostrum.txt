
Colostrum: A sticky white or yellow fluid secreted by the breasts during the second half of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pregnancy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">pregnancy</a> and for a few days after birth, before <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/breast_anatomy/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">breast</a> milk comes in. It is high in protective antibodies that boost the newborn's immune system.
