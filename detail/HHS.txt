
HHS: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10711" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Department of Health and Human Services</a> of the US government, which has jurisdiction over public health, welfare, and civil rights issues and is the highest-level US government body with such jurisdiction. Agencies under HHS include the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) and the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9786" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">National Institutes of Health</a> (NIH).
