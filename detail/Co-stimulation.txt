
Co-stimulation: An event in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3907" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">immune system</a> involving the delivery of a second signal by an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10098" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">antigen-presenting cell</a>. The second signal rescues the activated T cell from <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10094" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">anergy</a> (which is a state of immune unresponsiveness), allowing the T cell to produce the lymphokines necessary for the growth of additional T cells.
