
National Program of Cancer Registries: A program administered in the US by the Centers for Disease Control and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/prevention/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Prevention</a> (CDC) since 1994 that helps the states and US territories to improve their <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a> registries; meet standards for data completeness; timeliness, and quality; use cancer data to support <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer_prevention/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer prevention</a> and control programs; train registry personnel; establish computerized reporting and data-processing systems; and develop laws and regulations that strengthen registry operations.
