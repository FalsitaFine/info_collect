
Anal papilla: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/skin_tag/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">skin tag</a> that projects up from the junction between the skin and the inside lining of the anus. Anal papillae tend to occur with anal fissures and may be detected on a digital examination of the anus or with a scope. Removal is only necessary if the anal papillae are causing symptoms.
