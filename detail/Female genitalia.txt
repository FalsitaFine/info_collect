
Female genitalia:  The genital organs of the female. These are usually separated by convention into the external and internal genitalia.
The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=20894">female external genitalia</a> comprise the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=33681">pudendum</a>, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2753">clitoris</a>, and the female <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5907">urethra</a>.
