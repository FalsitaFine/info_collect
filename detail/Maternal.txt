
Maternal: 1. Pertaining to the mother as, for example, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4297" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">maternal mortality rate</a>.
2. Related through the mother as, for example, the maternal grandparents.
3. Inherited from the mother as, for example, the maternal <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6030" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">X chromosome</a>.
