
Female internal genitalia: The internal genital structures of the female, which include the ovaries, the Fallopian tubes, the uterus, the uterine cervix, and the vagina. These are, collectively, the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8874" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">female organs of reproduction</a>.
