
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7136" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">JAMA</a>: The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7134" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Journal of the American Medical Association</a>. JAMA began publication <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">in</a> 1883. JAMA ranks as one of the two leading general <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9611" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">medical journals</a> published in the United States. (The other is the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13487" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">New England Journal of Medicine</a>.)
