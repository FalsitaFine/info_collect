
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9956" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">Intern</a>: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3950" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">In</a> medicine, a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9237" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">doctor</a> who has completed <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=30954" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">medical school</a> and is engaged in a year of additional training at a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8390" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hospital</a> before residency. An intern may, for example, be in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4814" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pediatrics</a> or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3995" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">internal medicine</a>. The internship year is often quite rigorous.
