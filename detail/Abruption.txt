
Abruption: A sudden breaking off or away. Abruption of the placenta (<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2096" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">abruptio</a> placentae) is the premature separation of the placenta from the wall of the uterus, a potentially very serious situation for the mother and baby.
