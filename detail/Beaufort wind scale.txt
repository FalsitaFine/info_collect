
Beaufort wind scale: A widely   used wind force scale which is a simple numerical relationship to wind speed    based on an observation of the effects of the wind. The Beaufort wind scale is of importance in preparations for a tropical storm or <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=38917" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">hurricane</a>.
