
Immune response: Any reaction by the immune system. For example, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/poison_ivy_oak_and_sumac/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">poison ivy</a> can cause an immune response in the skin characterized by inflammation with tiny blisters, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/itch/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">itching</a>. Also, a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/influenza/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">flu</a> shot is designed to produce an immune response by stimulating the production of antibodies against the flu virus.
