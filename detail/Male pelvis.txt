
Male pelvis: The lower part of the abdomen that is located between the hip bones in a male. The male pelvis is more robust, narrower, and taller than the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9034" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">female pelvis</a>. The angle of the male pubic arch and the sacrum are narrower as well.
