
Autoimmune hemolytic anemia: A condition in which the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3907">immune system</a> attacks the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=5260">red blood cells</a>, resulting in fewer of these oxygen-transporting cells. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3695">Hemolytic anemia</a>.
