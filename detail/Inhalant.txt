
Inhalant: 1. Generally, any breathable chemical vapor.<br> 2. Specifically, a psychoactive substance whose volatile vapors are subject to abuse such as solvents, household and commercial gases, medical <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2247">anesthetic</a> gases, and nitrites (for example, in antifreeze). See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24242">Abused inhalant</a>.</br>
