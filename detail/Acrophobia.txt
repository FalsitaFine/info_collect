
Acrophobia: An abnormally excessive and persistent fear of heights. Sufferers experience severe <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anxiety/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anxiety</a> even though they usually realize that, as a rule, heights pose no real threat to them.
Derived from the Greek "acron", height + "phobos", fear.
