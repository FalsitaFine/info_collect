
Jaundice, obstructive: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/jaundice_in_adults/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">Jaundice</a> caused by obstruction of the drainage through the bile ducts, as may occur with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/gallstones/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">gallstones</a>. Symptoms of obstructive jaundice include the typical yellowing of jaundice as well as dark urine, pale feces, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/itch/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">itching</a>. Also known as post-hepatic jaundice. Sometimes the cause of obstructive jaundice is <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a>, a condition referred to as malignant obstructive jaundice.
