
Human papillomavirus: HPV. A family of over 100 viruses including those which cause <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/warts_common_warts/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">warts</a> and are transmitted by contact. Some types of human papillomavirus are associated with tumors of the genital tract including, notably, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/cancer/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">cancer</a> of the cervix.
