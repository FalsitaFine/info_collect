
Brittle diabetes: A type of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/diabetes_mellitus/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">diabetes</a> when a person's blood <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/glucose/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="pharm">glucose</a> (sugar) level often swings quickly from high to low and from low to high. Also called "unstable diabetes" or "labile diabetes."
