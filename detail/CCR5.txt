
CCR5: A <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2696" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chemokine receptor</a> found on monocytes and lymphocytes that serves as the coreceptor for HIV. HIV strains that use this coreceptor are referred to as R5 viruses. Polymorphisms in the coding and non-coding regions of the CCR5 gene influence the natural history of HIV infection. CCR5 (or CCCR5) stands for the CC <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2695" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">chemokine</a> receptor 5.
