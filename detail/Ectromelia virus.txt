
Ectromelia virus: A member of the poxvirus family which is a highly virulent natural <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6383" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">pathogen</a> of mice that causes <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=25050" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">mousepox</a>, a severe disease with high mortality rate in mice but does not infect humans. The complete genome sequence of ectromelia virus is known.
