
Barium swallow: A test that involves filling the esophagus, stomach, and small intestines with a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2426" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">barium solution</a> in preparation for an X-ray, to define the anatomy of the upper digestive tract. Also known as upper gastrointestinal series.
