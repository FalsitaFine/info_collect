
Genetic testing: Tests done for clinical genetic purposes.  Genetic tests may be done for diverse purposes pertaining to clinical genetics, including the diagnosis of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/genetic_disease/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">genetic disease</a> in children and adults; the identification of future disease risks; the prediction of drug responses; and the detection of risks of disease to future children.
