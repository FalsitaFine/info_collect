
Neuroradiologist: A radiologist who specializes in the use of radioactive substances, x-rays and scanning devices for the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the nervous system. A neuroradiologist mat be concerned with the clinical imaging, therapy, and basic <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13754" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">science</a> of the central and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8258" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">peripheral nervous system</a>, including but not limited to the brain, spine, head and neck.
