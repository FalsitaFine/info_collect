
Echolalia: The involuntary parrotlike repetition (echoing) of a word or phrase just spoken by another person. Echolalia is a feature of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/schizophrenia/article.htm">schizophrenia</a> (especially the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11106">catatonic</a> form), <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tourette_syndrome/article.htm">Tourette syndrome</a>,  and some other disorders. From echo + the Greek lalia, a form of speech.
