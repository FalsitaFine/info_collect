
Non-inferior clinical trial: A clinical trial that shows that a new treatment is equivalent to standard treatment. Also called a non-inferiority trial. See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2752">Clinical trials</a>. See also:

<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=39070">Positive clinical trial</a>.
