
Humor, aqueous: The fluid, continually produced by the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9891" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">ciliary body</a>, that is normally present in the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10586" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">anterior chamber</a> of the eye, between the <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7248" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cornea</a> and the iris.
