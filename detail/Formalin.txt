
Formalin: A 37% aqueous (water) solution of formaldehyde, a pungent gas, with the chemical formula HCHO, used as an <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2288" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">antiseptic</a>, disinfectant, and especially today as a fixative for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=7318" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">histology</a> (the study of tissues under the microscope).
