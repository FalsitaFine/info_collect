
Mantoux test: A skin test for <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6304">tuberculosis</a>, named for  the French <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=4887">physician</a> Charles Mantoux (1877-1947). See: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=23027">Tuberculin</a>.
