
Anomaly, congenital: A birth defect. A minor <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=12758" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">congenital anomaly</a> is an unusual anatomic feature such as a short second toe that is of no serious medical or cosmetic consequence. By contrast, a major congenital anomaly is a defect such as a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=6538" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">cleft palate</a> that is of serious medical or cosmetic consequence.
