
Drug, antimicrobial: A drug used to treat a microbial infection. The term "<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9621" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">antimicrobial</a>" is a general one that refers to a group of drugs that includes antibiotics, antifungals, antiprotozoals, and antivirals.
