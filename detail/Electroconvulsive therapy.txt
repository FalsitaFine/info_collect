
Electroconvulsive therapy: The use of controlled, measured doses of electric <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/shock/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">shock</a> to induce convulsions. Convulsions so induced can sometimes treat clinical <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/depression/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">depression</a> that is unresponsive to medication. Abbreviated ECT.
