
Hematology: The diagnosis, treatment, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/prevention/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">prevention</a> of diseases of the blood and bone marrow as well as of the immunologic, hemostatic (blood clotting) and vascular systems.
