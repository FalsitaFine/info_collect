
Acupressure: A traditional Chinese medicine bodywork technique based on the principles of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/acupuncture/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">acupuncture</a>. Acupressure is the application of pressure rather than needles on specific points on the body to control symptoms such as pain or nausea.
