
Immunization, HIB: This <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3909">immunization</a> is designed to prevent diseases caused by <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=3647">Haemophilus influenzae type B</a> (HIB), a <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=13954"> bacteria</a> responsible for a range of serious "invasive" diseases including <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10375">meningitis</a> with potential brain damage and 
epiglottitis with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=8541">airway obstruction</a>.
