
Melanoma, ocular: A rare type of malignant <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/melanoma/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">melanoma</a> that arises from a structure within the eye. The most common sites for ocular melanoma are the choroid, the ciliary body, and the iris. The <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a> may metastasize (spread), most often to the liver. Ocular melanoma tends to occur after age 40.
