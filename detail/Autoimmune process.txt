
Autoimmune process: A process in which the body's immune system attacks and destroys body tissue that it mistakes for foreign matter. See also: <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=2402">Autoimmune disease</a>; and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=18985">Autoimmunity</a>.
