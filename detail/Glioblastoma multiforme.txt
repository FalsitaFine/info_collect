
Glioblastoma multiforme: A highly malignant, rapidly growing type of brain <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/tumor_grade/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">tumor</a> that arises from glial cells in the brain. Early symptoms may include sleepiness, <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/headache/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">headache</a>, and vomiting. Also called a grade IV astrocytoma. Treatment can involve surgery and radiation treatment.
