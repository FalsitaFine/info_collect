
Anemic: Relating to <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/anemia/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">anemia</a>, the condition of having less than the normal number of red blood cells or less than the normal quantity of <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/hemoglobin/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="proc">hemoglobin</a> in the blood. The oxygen-carrying capacity of the blood is, therefore, decreased.
