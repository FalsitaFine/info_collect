
Endometrioma: : Part of the condition known as <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/endometriosis/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">endometriosis</a>. Endometrioma is a type of cyst formed when endometrial tissue (the mucous   membrane that makes up the inner layer of the uterine wall) grows in the   ovaries. It affects women during the reproductive years and may cause chronic   pelvic pain associated with <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/menstruation/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dt">menstruation</a>.
