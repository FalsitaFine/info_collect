
Hypnagogic hallucination: A vivid dreamlike hallucination that occurs as one is falling asleep. The opposite of an
<a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=24173" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict"> hypnopompic hallucination</a> which is a vivid dreamlike hallucination that occurs as one is waking up.
