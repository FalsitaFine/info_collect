
Bodywork: Any of a number of therapeutic or simply relaxing practices that involve the manipulation, massage, or regimented movement of body parts. Examples include massage, craniosacral therapy, and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/pilates/article.htm" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="sub">Pilates</a>. Bodywork may be used as an adjunct to medical treatment, or it may be prescribed as a form of physical therapy for certain conditions.
