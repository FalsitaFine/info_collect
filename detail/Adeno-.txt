
Adeno-: Prefix referring to a gland, as in <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=10917" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">adenoma</a> and <a href="https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=22429" onclick="wmdTrack('embd-lnk');" rel="dict">adenopathy</a>. From the Greek aden meaning originally "an acorn" and later "a gland" in the form of an acorn.  Before a vowel, adeno- becomes aden-, as in adenitis (inflammation of a gland).
